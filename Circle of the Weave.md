The Circle of the Weave is one of the Seven Circles of [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]], devoted to the study and mastery of the arcane arts. Its Nexus, the **Locus Arcanum**, rises from the city alongside the other six citadels, converging at the [[Confluence of the Seven]].

The current Meridian of the Weave is [[Illythia Voss]], who assumed leadership when her predecessor, [[Ayana Syndrosa]], departed Sumara during [[The Emerald Ceremony]].
