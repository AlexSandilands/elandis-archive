A legendary autonomous flying city — a Point of Interest of significant cultural weight for the world's mages, and one of the few surviving creations of the Chaos Wars.

## Established
- Autonomous flying city; fully self-governing with no permanent residents (Session 29 prep / stub)
- Completely neutral ground — sworn enemies are expected to acknowledge one another; political rivalry is left at the door (stub)
- Enforcement is handled by powerful automatons, purpose-built to counter mages: nearly immune to most magic and capable of stripping magic from casters (stub)
- Centuries of accumulated magical knowledge; origins unknown to most of the world (stub)
- Mages come and go freely, but are eventually expected to contribute something to the city's knowledge (stub)
- [[Belinda Bubblegum]] had reaching Arkangel as a long-standing goal; she achieved it after the party helped her harness the [[The Emberspire]]'s energy during the Solstice to build a teleportation rod (Session 29)

## Appearance
- Elegant arcane spires rising from a floating platform, held aloft by magical means
- Visually striking when glimpsed — though it rarely drifts near large cities, it is occasionally visible in the sky to those who know where to look

## Movement & Access
- Arkangel moves; its position is not fixed or publicly announced
- Part of the rite of passage is solving access yourself — there is no prescribed method. Valid approaches have included: building a teleportation rod calibrated to its location (Belinda's solution), or in principle flying there if you can determine exactly when and where it will be and navigate the arcane wards
- You cannot simply be brought there by someone else; you must earn your own way in
- Arcane wards surround the city and are a meaningful obstacle for any approach

## Culture
- Explicitly neutral ground; the city has been structured so it can never be weaponised for political or personal gain — only the pursuit, exploration, and preservation of magic
- Any conflict results in immediate expulsion by the automatons

## Restricted
- Arkangel was created during the Chaos Wars as a means for mages to store their collective knowledge and travel across the world offering aid where needed
- It is one of the few surviving intact creations from that era — most others are ruins
- This origin is not public knowledge; the city appears to have no known creator or founding story from the outside
