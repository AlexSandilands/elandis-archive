> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Valtorran Empire]]*

## The True Master

- The Empire is not [[Empress Morganna Eventide|Morganna's]] design but [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow|Kharazoth's]] — one of the three red dragons that survived the [[Chaos Wars]], lairing inside [[Valfyria]]. He is the true power behind the throne, content to let Morganna wear the crown while he works the world at a remove.
- Morganna is a lich bound to him, acting as his instrument. The serene, eternal Empress the continent believes in is a century-long performance over a far older, undead form. (Full detail in [[Empress Morganna Eventide]]'s restricted entry.)

## The Wealth Engine

- The Empire is, at root, an engine of extraction. A century of Imperial taxation, conquest, and conscription — the dwarven gem mines above all — has been funnelling wealth toward Kharazoth's true goal: the ritual to bring Tiamat into the world.
- The conquest that reads as one woman's ambition is a dragon's long game — a continent strip-mined of wealth and labour to fund the reopening of the rift torn during [[The Shattering]].
- Open thread: where a century of Imperial wealth has physically gone is still unanswered on the page. [[Valfyria]] is the likeliest sink.

## The Crimson Name

- The real reason the Empire is crimson is neither sovereignty nor the blood of conquest — both are after-the-fact rationalisations the public believes. Kharazoth is vain, and he commanded the colour. The entire continent salutes a red dragon's vanity every time it raises the banner, and none of them know it.

## Manufactured Loyalty

- The "absolute loyalty" of the Empire's arms is not devotion but structural mind-control. Morganna's violet orb — her phylactery — bends the will of the [[Hand of The Empire|Hands]] and [[Voice of the Empire|Voices]] in a graduation/conditioning rite, and the [[Eye of the Empire|Eyes]] answer to her alone. (See the arms' restricted entries.)
- The conditioning has a flaw the Empire does not know exists: [[Riftshard|Riftshards]] degrade it. This is what undid [[General Korvas]]. If the party realises it, the implication reaches every conditioned Hand and Voice on the continent. (See [[General Korvas]]'s restricted entry and the Hand restricted entry.)

## The Red Priests' True Allegiance

- The [[Red Priests]]' loyalty runs through Morganna to [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow|Kharazoth]], not to the Empire as an institution — which is exactly why even Imperial soldiers sense the priests answer to something else. They are Kharazoth's hand inside the Empire's wars, on loan rather than truly Imperial.
