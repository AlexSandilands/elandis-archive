## World Gate Access — Eryndor

[[Eryndor]] carries Silmara blood through his mother [[Eirieneth Silmara]], but is half-human. Whether the Ryordan binding runs fully through a mixed-blood Silmara is left deliberately open. If Eryndor ever attempts to open a [[World Gates|World Gate]], call for a roll — roughly 33% chance the gate responds. Do not telegraph this in advance; let it be discovered in play.

## Ryo's Reluctance

[[Ryo]] currently has no desire to take the Silmara seat in Sumara. This is not a permanent refusal, but it is where he stands. The throne is vacant by choice, not circumstance.
