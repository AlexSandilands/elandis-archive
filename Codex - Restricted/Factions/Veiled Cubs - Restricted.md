> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Veiled Cubs]]*

## Succession — the Cubs' real fragility

- The Cubs are, in every meaningful sense, [[Ellesar]] himself: his code, his reserves, his judgement on who gets through the Den's door. At ninety, the whole network is one old man's life away from scattering.
- [[Rolan]] is the intended answer. He is being groomed to take over — already Ellesar's right hand and the children's protector. Whether he can hold fifty orphans together the way Ellesar does is an open thread, and a live one.
- Plot hook: Ellesar's death or incapacity throws the Cubs into question. Does Rolan hold them, do they fracture, or does something move into the vacuum?

## Lia's placement

- [[Lia]]'s transfer to the [[Order of Ravens]] was as much protection as contract. Her hatred of the [[Valtorran Empire]] (after [[General Varan Brado]] executed her parents) made her a liability — sooner or later she would act against an Imperial and bring blowback down on the whole Den. Ellesar moved her to spare both her and the Cubs.
- Genuinely a one-off. The Cubs are not a feeder or recruiting ground for the Order of Ravens — the relationship is informal and case-by-case, not a pipeline.
