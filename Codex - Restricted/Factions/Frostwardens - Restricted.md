> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Frostwardens]]*

Appearance notes for crew members who don't have their own Codex pages. Fox Crew (Lysa, Dane Verric, Sela Ren) are covered by their own public entries and are omitted here.

## Wolf Crew

- Veyra Thorne (Pathfinder, deceased) — gruff older woman, silver hair hacked short, missing two fingers on her left hand, heavy fur-lined cloak patched a dozen times
- Kellan Reeve (Scout, survivor) — young, wiry man, close-cropped black hair, keen alert gaze; always fiddling with a piece of knotted rope
- Mira "Whisper" Sal (Ice Listener, deceased) — small woman, dark brown skin, bundled heavily in layers, rarely speaks; carried a delicate iron hammer for tapping the ice

## Bear Crew

- Orrek Stoneshield (Pathfinder) — massive, barrel-chested man, thick grey-streaked beard, permanent squint; furs layered over thick iron bracers
- Tamsin Holt (Scout) — broad-shouldered woman, sun-browned skin, short practical black hair; heavy crossbow strapped to her back
- Bryn Cutter (Ice Listener) — lean man, long shaggy hair, sharp hawkish nose, calloused hands; listens with a simple wooden stave

## Hawk Crew

- Elyen Windstrider (Pathfinder) — tall and spare, sharp-featured, windburned cheeks, piercing grey eyes; deep blue cloak
- Calla Vens (Scout) — short woman, platinum blond hair cropped roughly at the chin; nimble, always scribbling quick maps into a journal
- Joran Pell (Ice Listener) — gangly man, long neck, dark olive skin, eyes too big for his face; taps the ice with metal spikes

## Stag Crew

- Koren Vorr (Pathfinder) — middle-aged man, salt-and-pepper hair in a neat knot, tall fur-collared cloak, gold ring on his right thumb
- Torran Wrynn (Scout) — lean, smiling man, messy mop of dark curls, sly confidence to his every movement
- Maera Kett (Ice Listener) — sturdy woman, cropped copper hair, network of old scars across her face and arms; carved bone listening stick

## Raven Crew

- Selvyn Drae (Pathfinder) — tall, gaunt man, slick black hair, constant smirk; black leather gloves, unsettling calm
- Cass Tallow (Scout) — stocky young man, thick scarf always covering half his face; darting, nervous movements
- Olric Fenn (Ice Listener) — middle-aged man, greying temples, heavy fur-lined hood pulled low; often seen muttering quietly to himself

## Lynx Crew

- Veyl Shardstep (Pathfinder) — lean woman, sharp silver eyes, close-cut dark hair; walks with eerie silence even on crunchy snow
- Aren Dass (Scout) — tall and angular, pale skin, dark tattoos curling along his arms; favors simple leather wraps over heavier gear
- Sethra Vale (Ice Listener) — slender, reedy woman, almost translucent skin, snow-white hair braided with tiny silver charms; said to feel storms before they come
