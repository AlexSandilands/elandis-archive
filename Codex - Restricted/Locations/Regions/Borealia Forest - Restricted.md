> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Borealia Forest]]*

## The New Necromancer (cause of the escalation)
- A **new necromancer** has taken up residence in the ancient ruins at the heart of the forest — an acolyte or aspirant inspired by the legend of the original. They have begun replicating the old ritual, and are the cause of the recent escalation in both the number and aggression of the undead.
- The vision [[Berberis]] received at the Heartstone (Session 47) — *a frail figure completing a ritual in a ruined castle, the dead clawing upward through an endless dark forest* — depicted **this new figure**, not the historical one.
- Identity, motive, and full detail are deliberately undeveloped — this thread is for later in the story. The aspirant framing is set, but their exact nature is open.

## The Historical Necromancer & Ruins
- The forest's reputation dates to an incident several centuries ago. A powerful necromancer — known only as **The Necromancer of Borealia Forest**, their true name lost — established a foothold in the deep ruins and performed a ritual of significant and lasting consequence that tainted the land itself. When it was over, the dead did not stop walking, and never entirely have.
- The deep ruins are an old stone fortification, half-swallowed by centuries of growth, where the original necromancer worked. Items have occasionally been recovered from the site by those daring or foolish enough to venture that far. **The ruins now have a new occupant.**
- These are the same ruins from which a **Ring of Protection** was recovered north of [[Val Aran]] (Session 22). A useful hook if the party ever traces that item back to its origin.

## Berberis's Arc
- Ending the dead walking the north is Berberis's personal goal. He is aware the undead are worsening and has left his grove to seek allies, but does not yet know the cause. The revelation that a *new* necromancer is responsible — rather than the original curse simply running its course — should land as a meaningful discovery.
- The grove of [[Berberis]], Quercus, and [[Prunus Serrulata - Restricted]] sits just south of the forest's edge, north of [[Val Aran]], and is itself increasingly exposed to the threat.

## Plot Hooks
- The party could be drawn here via Berberis's quest, rumours from [[Val Aran]], or investigation into why the undead are worsening. The ruins are the likely endpoint.
