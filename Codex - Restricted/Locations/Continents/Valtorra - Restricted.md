## The Truth of Valfyria
- The volcano at the heart of the [[Silver Mountains]] is not merely a landmark. Within it lies the lair of [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow]] — one of the three red dragons that survived the [[Chaos Wars]].
- The [[Red Priests]] operate out of Valfyria, in direct service to the Empress.
- Publicly, Valfyria is just a smoking peak in the central range. The dragon, the priests, and the Empress's connection to either are not common knowledge. (The party has begun to piece this together — see [[Empress Morganna Eventide]].)

## The Empire's Hidden Master
- The [[Valtorran Empire]] is, at root, an engine of Kharazoth's design. [[Empress Morganna Eventide|Morganna]] is a lich bound to him, and a century of Imperial wealth has been flowing toward the ritual to bring Tiamat into the world.
- The conquest of Valtorra — human kingdoms turned to governorships, the dwarves driven into the gem mines — reads on the surface as one woman's empire-building. Beneath it, it is a dragon's long game: a continent strip-mined of wealth and labour to fund the reopening of the rift. (Full detail in the [[Empress Morganna Eventide]] restricted entry.)
