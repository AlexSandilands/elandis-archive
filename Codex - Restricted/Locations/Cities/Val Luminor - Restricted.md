> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Val Luminor]]*

## Prison Tower — Tactical Details

The prison tower is not entered from below or from outside the Citadel complex. The only connection between the main Citadel body and the tower is a narrow exposed walkway — hundreds of feet above the river, no handrails, fully open on both sides. A permanent guard post is stationed at the Citadel end. Any entry to the tower from within the complex requires passing it.

From the outside, the tower rises above the river channel on Eventide Isle's edge. Alternative approaches would be from the river side (scaling the exterior from a boat), from the roof above, or through the island's foundations — none of which are straightforward. The public entry notes the prison tower's existence but not its access configuration.

## Eirieneth — Confirmed Location

[[Eirieneth Silmara]] is alive and held in the prison tower as of the Session 47 vision. DM-confirmed. The party believes this but does not have certainty; they are treating it as a strong lead rather than a known fact.

## Jema's Intelligence — Limitations

Jema's report on the elven prisoners was gathered secondhand — overheard staff gossip and guard chatter — during an unrelated visit to the city on another mission. She is not embedded in Val Luminor, there is no [[Order of Ravens]] nest there, and she left after completing her original mission. Her information on prisoner numbers, the tower's internal layout, and current guard rotations should be treated as incomplete and likely stale.

## Undiscovered Locations

The inner bank districts, Eventide Isle's noble residences, the Docklands establishments, and the interiors of the Citadel and Imperial Academy are all undeveloped. Stage specific locations as the party approaches and explores. The [[Citadel of Val Luminor]] and [[Imperial Academy]] each need their own POI entries before the party arrives.
