> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Val Noren]]*

## Ayana Syndrosa's True Identity

- [[Ayana Syndrosa]] is secretly an elf, operating in Val Noren under the alias Ayana Wynne. She has been shaping [[King Marius Noren]] for years before the rebellion — guiding him from reluctant governor into the king the resistance needed. Her presence at the heart of his court is entirely deliberate.
- She is aware of the Emerald Ceremony and is aggressively protecting her identity. When Ryo accidentally discovered her pointed ears (Session 43), she cast Modify Memory on him and wiped the entire encounter from his mind. He has no memory of the conversation.
- No player character currently knows she is an elf or that her true name is Ayana Syndrosa.

## The Necromancer in the Ruins

- Berberis holds a personal quest to "end the dead walking the north — the necromancer in the ruins of Val Noren" (referenced Session 49). A dark presence is still operating in the siege-damaged parts of the city, even after liberation.
- Whether this necromancer is connected to the [[Red Priests]], their master, or is an independent power operating in the aftermath of the occupation is not yet determined.
- The Ratways and the bombed-out sections of the South Quarter are the most likely staging grounds for this threat.
