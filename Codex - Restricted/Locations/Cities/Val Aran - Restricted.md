> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Val Aran]]*

## The Val Aran Death Plague

- Centuries ago, a powerful necromancer rose in the deep [[Borealia Forest]] and performed a ritual of catastrophic and lasting consequence. The dead began walking in great numbers and did not stop. This event became known to those who recorded it as the Val Aran Death Plague.
- A party of heroes ultimately confronted and ended the necromancer. The specifics — who they were, what it cost, how it was done — are deliberately undeveloped. This is intended as a potential mini-campaign thread set in the past.
- The taint the ritual left has never fully lifted. The lingering undead in the forest to this day are its ongoing consequence.
- The ruins where the necromancer worked still stand in the deep forest, half-swallowed by centuries of growth. Objects have been recovered from there before (a Ring of Protection, Session 22). The ruins now have a new occupant — see Borealia Forest - Restricted.

## The Current Siege

- Val Aran is currently under assault. Undead have been massing north of the city, growing in number and aggression each day. The city's walls are holding for now, but the situation is deteriorating steadily.
- [[Bjorn Aran]] is managing a slow-building crisis with no clear cause in sight from within the city.
- The players do not yet know this.
- The cause — a new necromancer operating from the ancient ruins in the heart of the forest — is documented in [[Borealia Forest - Restricted]] and is unknown to the city's defenders.

## Plot Hooks

- [[Berberis]] carries [[King Marius Noren]]'s letter directly into Bjorn Aran's sphere of influence. When the party arrives, they will find a governor in crisis rather than the stable ally Marius intended to introduce them to.
- The Val Aran Death Plague is a potential flashback or mini-campaign thread — who were the heroes who stopped the first necromancer, and what did it cost them?
