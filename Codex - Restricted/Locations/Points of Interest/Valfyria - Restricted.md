> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Valfyria]]*

## Kharazoth's Seat

Valfyria is more than the Red Priests' base of operations — it is [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow|Kharazoth]]'s seat of power on [[Valtorra]]. The temple beneath the mountain serves as the nerve centre through which he works the continent at a remove, bending the Red Priests and, through them, [[Empress Morganna Eventide|The Empress]] toward his ends.

## The Temple

The Red Priests live in a temple carved beneath the mountain. Its full extent is unknown. The temple is the home of all known named Red Priests: [[Violeta]] (deceased), [[Ordelia]], [[Rhazien]], [[Norrix]], and [[Karlene]].

## The Red Priests' True Allegiance

The priests' devotion is ultimately to Kharazoth, not the Empress. They were drawn to him by different means — some seduced by him appearing as a powerful and attractive man, others recruited when they were at their lowest and he provided for them. A faction within the order are true believers who have dreamed of the arrival of the Queen of Dragons — an entity referred to in their doctrine as the Breaker of Realms and the Mother of Magic.
