**Quercus Robur** is the Head Druid of the Grove near [[Val Aran]], known to those within it simply as Oak. Wise and unshakeable, he is the steady heartwood at the centre of the Grove — the kind of man who has weathered enough seasons that very little surprises him, and nothing breaks him.

## Biography

Quercus leads a small druid community in the forests north of [[Val Aran]], gathered around and in service to [[The Green Lady]], the ancient spirit — or goddess, or mere will of the wood — who watches from the heart of the Grove. Where others in the Grove are thorny or bright, Quercus is simply present: enduring, patient, and possessed of a calm that feels less like temperament and more like bedrock.

He took in [[Berberis]] when that man was at his lowest — a lost ranger giving up on everything — and gave him both shelter and purpose within the Grove. It is perhaps his most defining quality: not the power to command, but the steadiness to receive those who have nothing left and help them find their footing again.

## DM Notes

- **Role in Berberis's backstory:** Quercus is the foundational relationship in Berberis's arc — the father figure who gave a broken man roots again. Any story beat involving Berberis's sense of belonging, loyalty, or faith runs through Quercus.
- **Relationship to Prunus Serrulata:** The three Grove members named after plants form a deliberate trio — Oak (wise elder), Barberry (militant pragmatist), Cherry (bright diplomat). Quercus anchors the group.
- **Plot hooks:** Could serve as a quest-giver or moral anchor if the party interacts with the Grove; his patience and trust in Berberis make him a compelling off-screen presence. Threat to Quercus or the Grove would be a strong lever on Berberis.
- **The Grove's location:** North of Val Aran; near the undead-troubled northern forests. Quercus's leadership is likely under growing pressure from the escalating undead threat that drove Berberis south.
