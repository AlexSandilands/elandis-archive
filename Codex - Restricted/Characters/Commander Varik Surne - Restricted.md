> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Commander Varik Surne]]*

## Fate

- After the breakout, Surne was left holding a burned camp, a catastrophically reduced prisoner pool, and no way to contain the damage.
- [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]] received reports of the chaos at [[Point Blackrock]] within days — Blackmarsh was the obvious candidate to execute Surne for the failure, but his own fate in [[Val Noren]] intervened before he could act.
- Surne was almost certainly dealt with by [[General Korvas]] ([[Vaeleran]]) after departing [[Val Noren]] — a quiet consequence of Vaeleran's cover identity playing out in the background. As the senior Imperial officer withdrawing from the campaign's collapse, Korvas would have been the natural authority to settle accounts with a failed subordinate.
- The players have no knowledge of this. From their perspective, they left Surne standing in the smoke. What became of him is entirely unrevealed.
