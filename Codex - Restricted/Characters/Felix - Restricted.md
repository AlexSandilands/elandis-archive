**Felix** is an elven mage operating in [[Val Miriel]] under a carefully maintained human disguise, running [[The Bridge of Stars]] on [[The Artisan Way]] as his cover. What the city sees is an eccentric, sharp-tongued magic dealer with a fondness for peculiar hats; what the [[Valtorran Empire]] does not see is one of its most quietly active opponents, embedded in the heart of one of its most important cities.

## Description

#### Appearance

To the world, Felix presents as a human male of apparent middle age — silver robes stitched with small blue stars, always topped by an odd hat that never quite coordinates with the rest of his outfit. The mismatched aesthetic reads as harmless eccentricity, the kind of affectation that makes customers smile and guards look elsewhere. Beneath the illusion that sustains this form, he is an elf: sharp-featured, ageless in the way elves are, with the unhurried stillness of someone who has seen centuries and learned not to show it.

#### Personality

Felix is genuinely sharp and genuinely passionate about arcane curios — the shop is not merely a front but a real obsession, and his enthusiasm for unusual items is entirely his own. He is theatrical by nature, given to expansive gestures and the comfortable confidence of a man who has played this role long enough that parts of it are no longer a role at all. Beneath the performance, he is patient, careful, and committed to something larger than himself — a man who has chosen his ground and intends to hold it.

## Biography

#### Background

Felix came through [[The Emerald Ceremony]] alongside [[Ayana Syndrosa]], arriving in [[Val Miriel]] as part of what appears to have been a coordinated effort to place agents against the [[Valtorran Empire]] from within. While Ayana's path led her elsewhere, Felix anchored himself in the city's artisan quarter, establishing [[The Bridge of Stars]] as a legitimate business and a useful cover from which to pursue that work over the years. His human disguise is maintained through magic, convincing enough to pass casual and professional scrutiny alike.

His role in [[Serenity Plaza]] made his methods plain: the explosion that incinerated the Imperial soldiers and ignited two Fire Elementals during the anti-Empire rally was his doing, witnessed by [[The Bloody Nails]] from the middle of the chaos. The act was precise — the soldiers were the target — and Felix has shown no indication of regret.

### [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 22 - Inferno at Serenity Plaza]]

The party visited [[The Bridge of Stars]] while resupplying in [[Val Miriel]]. Felix sold them an Enduring Spellbook, an Immovable Rod, and several decks of cards, and showed them a Ring of Protection priced at 6,000 gold — recovered from ruins north of [[Val Aran]]. He also directed [[Ryo]] toward a contact in [[Val Aerie]] who might possess a magical quiver. Later that same session, Felix caused the explosion at [[Serenity Plaza]], though the party did not witness who was responsible.

#### [[Session 27 - The Wanted Dwarf]]

[[Kai]] returned to [[The Bridge of Stars]] to purchase ruby dust, and took the opportunity to apologise to Felix for a previous attempt to use magic to wrangle a discount.

## DM Notes

- **Unrevealed identity:** The party do not know Felix is an elf, that he is magically disguised, or that he has any connection to [[Ayana Syndrosa]] or [[The Emerald Ceremony]].
- **Serenity Plaza:** The party witnessed the explosion but did not identify its source. Felix is the culprit — this is an open hook if they ever investigate.
- **Plot hooks:** Felix could become an ally, an informant, or a complication depending on how the party's relationship with the resistance develops. His connection to Ayana gives him a potential reason to trust them — or to be cautious of them.
- **Disguise mechanics:** His human form is maintained by a sustained magical effect. Detect Magic, True Sight, or a high-enough Insight roll during unusual circumstances could crack it.
