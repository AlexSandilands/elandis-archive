> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Governor Tarlis Evandron]]*

## Core Secret
- His patience is not resignation — it is **preparation**. The compliance he shows the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] is a well-fitted mask. Beneath it runs steady, patient fury: a governor who has watched his people taxed and humiliated for years and has been architecting their liberation for just as long.
- He is the **architect of a continent-wide unified rebellion** against the Empire, operating entirely in the shadows. He knows a single city rising is a massacre; a continent rising at once is something else. He refuses to move before the board is set — not a reluctant rebel but a deliberate one.
- The party are now his agents, whether they fully grasp the scope or not.

## Hidden Connections
- The **High Priest of [[Val Aerie]] is a cousin of Tarlis** — a personal connection the party has been pointed toward but may not yet have fully unpacked.

## Hidden Motivations
- The grief of losing Vallarra has calcified into quiet, controlled rage. He protects [[Vallania Miriel]] to the point of controlling her partly from fear of what happens if she becomes entangled in the rebellion before he can keep her safe. Her independence is both his greatest fear and his deepest pride.

## DM Notes
- **Relationship with the party:** He trusts them provisionally. Their value is that they are **deniable** — if caught, he can credibly distance himself. This calculation may eventually become a source of friction.
- **Plot hooks:** If the rebellion succeeds, the Empire will eventually trace coordination back to [[Val Miriel]] and Tarlis becomes a marked man. [[Vallania Miriel]] is both his greatest vulnerability and potentially his most effective asset — she is already more embedded in resistance activity than he knows.

## Session Notes (Restricted Detail)

- **Session 49:** Arriving alone with no flanking guards was a deliberate choice that speaks to how carefully he compartmentalises his dual life.
