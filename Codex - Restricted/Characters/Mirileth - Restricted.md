> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Mirileth]]*

## Unrevealed Connections
- The exact terms of the pact between Mirileth and [[Ellesandra Silmara]] are unknown to the party — what Ellesandra gave, promised, or surrendered inside the Vale to earn the peace has never been spoken of. Mirileth knows.
- The agreement is the foundation of two thousand years of border quiet and may carry obligations that outlast Ellesandra herself.

## Asha Vayne
- [[Asha Vayne]] disappeared on the Vale's borders sixty years ago in the same skirmish that killed a significant member of the Hunt.
- The party does not yet know whether he is alive, imprisoned, transformed, or serving some function within the Court. Mirileth chose not to let him simply die — [[Warden Sylvaris]]'s read is correct.

## Plot Hooks
- The party intends to enter the Vale as a Silmara embassy, invoking Ryo's bloodline. Mirileth will be aware of this before they arrive.
- How she receives a descendant of the woman who made the pact is entirely a matter of what you decide the pact actually was.

## Nature of Ascension (blank to fill)
- The circumstances of her rise are not openly discussed within the Court — a deliberate blank. She may have taken the throne by force, by succession, or by being the architect of the Long Hunt's end herself.
