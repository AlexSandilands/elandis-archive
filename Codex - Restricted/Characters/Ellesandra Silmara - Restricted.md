> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Ellesandra Silmara]]*

## Description (DM-only)
- **Appearance:** No physical description survives. Typically imagined in elven art as solemn and spare — a ruler who dressed for practicality over ceremony.
- **Personality:** Known by her decision, not her temperament. Walking alone into the Vale and returning suggests someone who had weighed the odds, accepted the cost, and moved anyway. Whether driven by desperation, strategic calculation, or something more personal has never been answered.

## The Pact (Full Truth)
- Ellesandra made a deliberate pact with the archfae queen [[Mirileth]]: Mirileth would take control of the Vale and bring the Hunt to heel, and the war would cease.
- Ellesandra likely gave something in return — what that was remains a potential plot thread. The full terms are unrevealed and a significant potential plot hook: Mirileth has held a two-thousand-year peace, but the original pact may have come with conditions that could become relevant when the party approaches the Vale as a Silmara embassy.

## Ellesandra's Fate
- She disappeared into the Vale; it is not recorded whether she left again by a different means, died there, or simply ceased to be spoken of. Marked Deceased by convention, but the manner of her death is unknown. She may have died in the Vale after the pact was struck, or returned to Sumara and faded from record — either reading works.

## What Mirileth Knows
- The Star-Veiled Queen is the only living being present for whatever Ellesandra did or said inside the Vale. If the party ever has an audience with [[Mirileth]], she may be the only one who could speak of Ellesandra truthfully.

## Ryo's Lineage
- Ellesandra is confirmed as an ancestor of [[Ryo]]'s via the [[Silmara Family]] line. The party now knows the shape of the story; the pact's terms remain hidden.
