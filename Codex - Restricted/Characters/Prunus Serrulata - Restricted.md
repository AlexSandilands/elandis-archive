**Prunus Serrulata** is a young druid of the grove near [[Val Aran]], known by her grove-name Cherry for the warmth and blossoming cheer she carries wherever she goes. Where her grove-brother [[Berberis]] meets the world with thorns, she meets it with flowers — and somehow, she is the one who gets through to him.

## Biography

Prunus earned her name in the same way all members of the grove do: it reflects her nature rather than her birth. The cherry tree blooms bright and briefly, filling the world with colour when the cold has barely left the ground — and so does she. Among the druids of the grove, she serves a quieter role than the oak's steadying wisdom or the barberry's bristling vigilance, but no less vital: she is the one who smooths the edges, finds common ground, and brings words where others would bring silence or swords. [[Quercus Robur]] values her diplomatic instincts; the elder oak has long understood that not every adversary is best confronted with roots and thorns.

Her bond with Berberis is one the grove watches with quiet fondness. His spiky temper and deep-seated distrust of the wider world would calcify without her; she carries enough warmth for both of them, and she knows it. She does not soften him — she simply reminds him, now and then, that there is still something worth blooming for.
