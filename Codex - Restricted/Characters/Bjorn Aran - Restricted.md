**Bjorn Aran** is the current Governor of [[Val Aran]] and the latest of a family whose ties to the city run generations deep. He keeps formal correspondence with [[King Marius Noren]], who counts him among the trusted figures of the north.

## Biography

The Aran family has held standing in [[Val Aran]] across generations, and Bjorn's tenure as Governor extends that lineage into the present day. Beyond the city's walls he maintains diplomatic ties with allied rulers, [[King Marius Noren]] among them.

Referenced in absentia when [[King Marius Noren]] provided [[Berberis]] with a letter of recommendation addressed to the Governor of Val Aran.
