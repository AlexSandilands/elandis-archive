> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Elaris Moonsong]]*

## Core Secret
- Elaris Moonsong was a silver-haired elf cleric — the mortal champion of **[[Selûne]]**, a God of Old and the true goddess of the moon. At the end of the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] he joined the other six champions in the working that sealed the breach, was consumed in it, and ascended to lingering demigodhood.
- He is a real power but not the first god of the moon — that is [[Selûne]], whose sphere he tends in her absence. The whole faith the world offers him in her name is a worship she is owed, given unknowingly to the champion she made.
- Keep consistent with the restricted [[The Pantheon]], [[Selûne]], [[The Shattering]], and [[World Tree]] pages (World Tree ▸ Gods of Old ▸ Seven Divines).

## Elaris in Mortal Life
- A cleric of [[Selûne]] — calm, unhurried, shaped by a determination that did not advertise itself.
- He carried the Moon's Tear as a genuine gift of his goddess *before* it became a holy relic, and through it read the visions she sent.
- At the Shattering he was raised above any ordinary Chosen to carry her sphere as her arm in a war she could not enter directly; when the breach was sealed, he did not end but persisted as a real demigod, able to pass a thread of power to those who pray to him.

## Selûne's Designs Behind the Faith
- The holy days the moon faith keeps — Silvernight, the Unshadowed, the Pardon — and the prophecy of the Moon's Chosen are [[Selûne]]'s designs, not inventions of the champion's faith. The Pardon's alignment with the dark moon's absence is her doing.
- The prophecy is her live mechanism: a standing means by which a God of Old forbidden from acting directly may yet raise a new mortal champion to carry her sphere into a crisis she cannot touch herself. **It has been triggered.**

## Campaign — Hidden Truths
- **[[Session 04 - To Free a Raven]]:** the vision [[Kai]] received at Elaris's statue was not from Elaris — it was [[Selûne]] herself, reaching directly through her champion's shrine to claim a new Chosen and mark him with her symbol.
- **[[Session 20 - The Streets of Val Miriel]]:** [[High Priest Oldir]] named the truth his line has guarded — the Seven are ascended champions, the True Gods stand behind them, Kai has been touched by [[Selûne]]. He also disclosed that [[Selûne]]'s dark sister has taken an interest in Kai.
- **[[Session 26 - A Midnight Romp]]:** Oldir acknowledged Kai as a potential — if darkly corrupted — Chosen.
- **[[Session 27 - The Wanted Dwarf]]:** the Stones of the Moon respond to one another as fragments of [[Selûne]]'s power reduced to physical form for mortal hands.
- **[[Session 49 - The Silver Sage]]:** the Moonlit Shield will point the way to the third Stone. The prophecy is in motion.

## The Live Prophecy
- Three Stones of the Moon exist — one with [[Kai]] (the Moonlit Shield), one with [[High Priest Oldir]] at the High Sept, one not yet located (the shield points the way).
- The Celestial Cavern and what lies within are open for development.

## The Contest
- [[Selûne]] is trying to complete Kai as a Chosen of Light; her dark sister is corrupting him toward a dark champion — the black stars in Kai's Starry Form are her mark. The moon faith's rites are the battlefield's staging ground.
