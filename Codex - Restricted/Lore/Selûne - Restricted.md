> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Selûne]]*

> The public page presents Selûne as a faded memory — a forgotten God of Old, folded unknowing into the worship of her champion. The truth is that she is **alive, active, and afraid**, and has broken an age of silence to reach directly into the world.

## The Cosmic Truth
Selûne is one of the **Gods of Old**: the true gods of the cosmos, older than the [[The Shattering|Shattering]], who by ancient habit do not act directly in the mortal world. They are the powers the deepest cosmology names the **True Gods** — real divinities, lesser than the [[World Tree]] that stands above them all, but greater by far than the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]] the world mistakes for eternal gods. The order: [[World Tree]] ▸ Gods of Old (True Gods) ▸ Seven Divines (ascended mortal champions).

Selûne is the genuine goddess of the moon; the Divine **[[Elaris Moonsong]]** whom the faithful pray to is the mortal champion she herself raised up, ascended and venerated until his worship buried hers. Her eclipse by Elaris is the whole history of the Seven in miniature: the goddess raised the champion, the champion died a god, and the world kept the one it could see.

The world's forgetting of her was, in a sense, chosen. The Gods of Old withdrew behind their champions and let their own names lapse; Selûne's silence is the same restraint that has kept the True Gods from striking their enemies directly since the war that wounded the [[World Tree]]. The scholar's footnote, the orphaned prophecy, the moons whose names no one can place — not the ruins of a dead faith but the embers of a living goddess who chose to be unseen.

## Why She Is Stirring Now
That she is reaching into the world — marking a mortal with her own hand, choosing a Chosen — is extraordinary, and a measure of her fear. The Gods of Old do not do this; the same restraint that keeps them hidden kept her silent for centuries. She has broken it because she believes a **second [[The Shattering|Shattering]] is coming**, and soon, and that the world will need a champion of the light to meet it. To move a Chosen across the board is, for a God of Old, an act close to desperation, and should be read as exactly that.

The **prophecy of the Moon's Chosen** is no dead legend but her own standing mechanism — the means by which a God of Old forbidden to fight may yet raise a mortal who can. The **Unshadowed** (four nights a year when Sel rides full and Sha goes dark) are the nights she stands strongest and her shadow is blind; the **Pardon** is keyed, secretly, to her.

## The Two Sisters
Selûne is not the only old power bound to the moon. Her dark sister — known to the world only as the **[[Shar|Lady of Vengeance]]** — is her shadow and eternal opposite: a God of Old who delights in suffering, vengeance, and chaos, feeding on cruelty of every scale. The two moons are named for them both, **Sel** for the Moonmaiden and **Sha** for her sister; for every Unshadowed when Selûne stands full and her sister blind, there is its inverse when Sha rides full over a dark Sel. The sisters' struggle is older than the Shattering, and has now found a new field: the soul of Selûne's would-be Chosen. See [[Shar]].

## Restricted Campaign Notes
- **S04 (To Free a Raven):** Kai's mark at [[Darmouth]] was the goddess's own touch — the first move of a God of Old reaching directly into the world.
- **S20 (Streets of Val Miriel):** [[High Priest Oldir]] let slip the hereditary heresy — the Seven are "false idols masking the existence of the True Gods," ascended champions — and warned that Selûne's **dark sister, the [[Shar|Lady of Vengeance]]**, had also fixed her gaze on Kai.
- **S26 (A Midnight Romp):** the **black stars** in Kai's Starry Form are the Dark Sister's corruption taking hold; Oldir named him a potential corrupted Chosen, and "the path of light" is literally the terms of the two sisters' contest over his soul. The moonstone matching [[Elaris Moonsong - Restricted|Elaris Moonsong]]'s altar was the first of the three Stones of the Moon.
- **S49 (The Silver Sage):** [[Praetor'Varanous]] confirmed the second moonstone lies in Oldir's keeping — the race for her Chosen.

## DM Notes
- **Unrevealed truth:** Selûne is a living God of Old / True God who *empowered* the Divine [[Elaris Moonsong]] then let the world forget her. The tier and the fact that the Seven are ascended mortals are the core secrets; keep consistent with restricted [[World Tree]] and [[The Shattering]].
- **The contest:** [[Shar]] works to corrupt Kai into a *dark* champion before Selûne can complete him (the black stars). Oldir's "path of light" is the terms of that fight.
- **Plot hooks:** the hunt for the three Stones of the Moon (one with Oldir at the High Sept) and the hidden Celestial Cavern; whether other Gods of Old stir as the second Shattering nears, Selûne's breaking of silence perhaps the first; what completing — or losing — the Chosen costs Kai.
- **Open threads:** what Selûne knows of the coming Shattering that the party does not; whether she can act more directly than through a Chosen, or this is the limit of what a God of Old will risk; what she would owe, and to whom, for having reached in at all.
