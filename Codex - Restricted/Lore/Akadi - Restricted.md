**Akadi**, known to the oldest records as the **Queen of Air**, is the goddess of elemental wind, speed, and the boundless sky. She is one of the Gods of Old — ancient powers who held the world long before the [[The Shattering\|Shattering]] — and is known to the present age only through scattered scholarly fragments and a handful of folk epithets for the wind.

## Overview

Akadi governs air in all its forms: the steady wind that carries seed and sail, the gale that breaks a forest, the open sky that promises freedom and speed without obstruction. She is paired with elemental counterparts in the oldest theology — [[Grumbar]] (earth), Istishia (water), Kossuth (fire) — though the names of the elemental lords mean little to common folk in Elandis today.

Like most of the Gods of Old, Akadi withdrew from direct involvement in the mortal world after the [[The Shattering\|Shattering]]. Her one recorded act in that age was the selection and empowerment of her mortal champion, [[Gale Winddancer]], during the [[Chaos Wars]].

## In Memory & Belief

Akadi is not worshipped. No temple stands in her name; no holy order traces its lineage to her. She survives only in scattered epithets — *the Lady of the Winds*, *Queen of Air*, *She Who Fills the Sails* — found in mariners' prayers, the invocations of storm-callers, and the marginalia of pre-Shattering theological texts. Most who speak these names do not know they invoke a god at all.

What the world has forgotten is that the faith it offers to [[Gale Winddancer]] for swift wind and safe passage was once, at its root, a faith directed elsewhere.
