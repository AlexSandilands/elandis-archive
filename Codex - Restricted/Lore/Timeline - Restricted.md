> [!abstract] Note:
> DM-only chronology of [[Elandis]]. The world's cosmic history from before the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] to the present day — almost all of it secret or lost to the people of the world. Parked here whole for now; individual eras are also expanded in [[The Shattering]], [[Chaos Wars]], and related Lore pages.

## Pre-Shattering (> 2500 years ago)
- Magic is extremely rare — limited only to the few people who have been plane-shifted by gods.
- Divine magic is also rare, but not quite as rare as arcane.
- There are no monsters in the world, only fairly standard beasts and humanoid races.
- Ancient civilisations were highly technologically advanced (not electricity) — incredible cities, deeply educated in the sciences.

## The Shattering (2500 years ago)
- [[Tiamat]] cracked an opening between the dragon plane and the prime material.
- This caused magic to enter the world, along with dragons and monsters.

## The Chaos Wars (2500 – 2000 years ago)
- A period of 500 years of chaos after the [[The Shattering|Shattering]].
- An endless war between the civilisations and the dragons / monsters.
- [[Tiamat]]'s goal was for the dragons to collect enough gemstones to be used as arcane reagents in a ritual to permanently open a large enough door between the realms, allowing her to enter.
- People began being born with magic — previously perhaps one in 10,000 could do magic, now it was more like one in 10.
- This gave the people of the world a fighting chance, but at huge cost — cities were razed to the ground, and who knows what knowledge was lost.
- There was a final showdown at the portal of the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. The majority of the people on the planet showed up, and the dragons retreated to defend the portal. Eventually, when it looked like the dragons were slowly gaining the advantage, 7 strong archmages — heroes of the realm — used their final option: a joint ritual that caused massive devastation, closing the portal (seemingly) but killing 90% of the people and dragons in the region.
- The portal was not completely closed; invisible cracks can be found throughout the world. A red, green, and black dragon survived, fleeing and hiding in the world.
- This essentially reset civilisation. History was mostly lost to time, apart from rumours or what can be found in ruins.

## Post-Shattering (2000 years to now)
- The 3 dragons spread across the world, once again gathering wealth for the ritual — only now in secret, plotting to insert themselves into society so they could rule by proxy or by force.
- People spread out across the world again, slowly rebuilding over thousands of years.
- Ruins can be found dotted around all of the continents — reminders of a world that used to be, but which no one remembers.
- Monsters still roam the lands, but not in the chaotic way they did during the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] — they have learned to live within the environment.
- Adventurers can sometimes find cracks of energy spread around the world, called [[Riftshard|Riftshards]], all pointing towards the location of the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. In proximity to a Riftshard they feel energised, but any magic used is unpredictable.
