**Maris Wavecaller** was a triton cleric who became the chosen champion of [[Istishia]], the Water Lord — one of the Gods of Old — and was raised at the end of the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] to a lingering demigodhood. The world prays to her as one of the eternal [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]]; in truth she was a mortal, and the sphere she governs is Istishia's, carried forward by the saint the world mistook for the god.

## Overview

As with all of the Seven, Maris Wavecaller stands in front of an elder god the world has entirely forgotten. [[Istishia]], the Water Lord, is the true God of Water — one of the Gods of Old who sit beneath the [[World Tree]] — and Maris was his chosen champion in the Chaos Wars. At the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]'s end she was consumed in the working that sealed the breach, as the other six champions were, and ascended to the demigodhood she now holds. She is a real power who answers real prayers; but the sphere she governs is not hers by origin, and the elder god behind her has no faith of his own in the world.

For the structure common to all Seven — the World Tree, the Gods of Old, the champions raised as demigods — see the restricted [[The Pantheon]].

## DM Notes

- **Unrevealed truth:** Maris Wavecaller was a triton cleric and the mortal champion of [[Istishia]], the Water Lord (a God of Old). She ascended in the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] working, as all Seven did. The world worships a demigod standing in front of a forgotten elder god. Consistent with the restricted [[The Pantheon]], [[The Shattering]], and [[World Tree]] pages.
- **The relic's name:** "Umberlee's Grasp" carries a name the public faith cannot explain. It is the most persistent in-world trace of [[Istishia]] — a marker connecting the relic directly to the elder god the world no longer remembers.
- **Istishia:** the Water Lord is among the most withdrawn of the Gods of Old. He has no active faith, no current champions, and no clear designs on the world. Whether he is stirring, as [[Selûne]] is, remains an open question.
- **Open threads:** Umberlee's Grasp is a direct material link to [[Istishia]] — the most powerful surviving connection to a God of Old currently in anyone's possession. What the Wavecaller herself understands of her own origins, and whether Istishia is aware of a second Shattering approaching, are unresolved.
