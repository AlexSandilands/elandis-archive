> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Green Lady]]*

## The Truth

- She is real, and she is one of the true Gods of Old — a nature goddess of the elder pantheon, of the same tier as [[Selûne]] and [[Shar]], not a mere wood-spirit or local fancy. The world's uncertainty about what she is (goddess, sprite, will of the wood) is exactly the cover a forgotten god hides behind.
- Her portfolio is the wild wood and the green growing world: forests, groves, springs and still pools, peace and the slow patience of nature. Where the moon faith forgot [[Selûne]], the wild faith forgot her — folded into hedge-tales and druidcraft rather than into a successor's church.
- Public Status reads `Forgotten`; her true Status is `Active`. Like Selûne, she has not withdrawn — she still reaches into the world, just quietly and through the wild rather than through temples.

## What She's Doing Now

- The great living tree at the heart of the Grove in the [[Borealia Forest]] is not merely sacred *to* her — it is a point through which she actually touches the world. The druids serve a present power, not a memory.
- She calls champions. [[Berberis]] was called by her directly: a lost, dying ranger drawn to the Grove at his lowest and given purpose. This is her pattern — taking in those who have nothing left and rooting them again. Quercus (oak) and the others were likewise gathered.
- Open question for later: why a forgotten nature god is stirring now. Tie to the escalating undead in the northern woods (Berberis's own concern) and to whatever the other Gods of Old fear — left deliberately unresolved.
