**Eldrin Starweaver** was a human wizard — the chosen champion of **[[Mystra]]**, the Lady of Mysteries, the true and ancient goddess of magic — raised at the end of the Chaos Wars and the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] to the lingering demigodhood the world has since mistaken for eternal divinity. The Weave he is said to govern was hers; and she, forgotten, stands behind the worship the world gives to her champion.

## Overview

Eldrin Starweaver's place in the divine order follows the same structure as all seven of his kind: the [[World Tree]] at the summit; beneath it, the Gods of Old (the True Gods), real and ancient and withdrawn; beneath them, the Seven Divines — ascended mortal champions whose worship has buried the memory of the elder powers who made them. For Eldrin, the elder power in question is **[[Mystra]]**, the Lady of Mysteries — the genuine goddess of magic and the Weave.

In life, Eldrin was a human wizard whose mastery of the arcane made him Mystra's chosen instrument for the Chaos Wars. He was consumed in the working that sealed the [[The Shattering|breach]], ascended into demigodhood as the other six champions did, and has since been worshipped as an eternal god of magic. Mystra withdrew into the silence the Gods of Old keep; the world forgot her entirely and kept him. The Weave he governs is genuinely hers in origin; his relic, the **Weave Loom**, may have been her instrument before it was his — the records do not say with certainty. Cross-reference the restricted [[The Pantheon - Restricted|Pantheon]], [[The Shattering]], and [[World Tree]] pages for the full cosmological structure.

## Campaign

### [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

The party fell into an ancient sealed shrine to Eldrin Starweaver in the wilds of [[Valtorra]] — a **Trial of the Weave** built into the bedrock as a lethal puzzle-room — and barely escaped.

#### [[Session 07 - Grakthor Falls]]

Fighting through the same shrine, the party found its magic dangerously unstable — every spell triggered a wild surge. [[Vaeleran]] was struck mid-battle by a vision of a pale hand holding a crackling purple orb. After the fighting, [[Kai]] observed a **crack in space** near the shrine's ceiling — unexplained and uninvestigated.

## DM Notes

- **Core secret:** Eldrin Starweaver was a mortal human wizard, champion of [[Mystra]] (the true goddess of magic and the Weave). He ascended to demigodhood through the sacrifice that sealed the Shattering; Mystra withdrew; the world forgot her and kept him. Consistent with the restricted Pantheon / Shattering / World Tree structure (World Tree ▸ Gods of Old ▸ Seven Divines).
- **Mystra's status:** Unlike [[Selûne]], there are no established signs that Mystra is stirring or reaching into the world. Her status is genuinely open — dormant, withdrawn, or moving in ways not yet visible. An intentional blank for the DM to develop.
- **The shrine anomaly:** the crack in space [[Kai]] observed above the shrine (Session 07) is unexplained — possible readings include Weave instability at a site of old arcane significance, a symptom of an approaching second Shattering, or a connection to the Weave Loom. Not yet assigned a cause.
- **The purple orb vision:** [[Vaeleran]]'s vision of a pale hand holding a crackling purple orb (Session 07) is similarly unresolved — its connection to Eldrin Starweaver, Mystra, the Weave Loom, or something else is open.
