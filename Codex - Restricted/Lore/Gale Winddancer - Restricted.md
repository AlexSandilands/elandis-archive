**Gale Winddancer** was an aarakocra monk — the chosen champion of **[[Akadi]]**, the Queen of Air — who was raised to a lingering demigodhood at the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] and is now worshipped across [[Elandis]] as one of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]]. The elder god she served, [[Akadi]], has been silent and unworshipped ever since.

## Overview

Gale Winddancer is one of the seven ascended mortal champions whose sacrifice sealed the breach at the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. Her portfolio — air, freedom, swiftness — was [[Akadi]]'s first; it passed to Gale Winddancer at her ascension, along with the worship the world now directs to her in [[Akadi]]'s place. Her relic, the **Zephyr Quill**, was a mortal champion's instrument before it was a holy artifact, and the power it holds is an echo of the elder god's sphere. This pattern is consistent across all Seven; see the restricted [[The Pantheon]] and [[The Shattering]] pages.

## DM Notes

- **Core secret:** Gale Winddancer was a mortal aarakocra monk in life, the chosen champion of **[[Akadi]]** (Queen of Air, a God of Old). She ascended to demigodhood at the Shattering. The world has entirely forgotten [[Akadi]] and worships the champion in her place.
- **Elder-god thread:** [[Akadi]] is currently silent and unworshipped — parallel to [[Selûne]] before she began to stir. If the other Gods of Old wake as a second Shattering nears, Akadi is the one behind this Divine.
- **The Zephyr Quill:** a mortal champion's weapon before it was a holy artifact; the power it holds is an echo of Akadi's sphere, not merely a blessed feather.
- **Open threads:** does Akadi stir as Selûne has? Does Gale Winddancer know what she is, or know that [[Akadi]] stands behind her? Can the Seven communicate with the Gods of Old who made them?
