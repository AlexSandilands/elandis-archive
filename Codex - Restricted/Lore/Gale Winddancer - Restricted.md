> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Gale Winddancer]]*

## Core Secret
- Gale Winddancer was an aarakocra monk — the chosen champion of **[[Akadi - Restricted]]**, the Queen of Air (a God of Old) — who was raised to a lingering demigodhood at the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] and is now worshipped across [[Elandis]] as one of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]].
- Her portfolio — air, freedom, swiftness — was [[Akadi - Restricted]]'s first; it passed to Gale at her ascension, along with the worship the world now directs to her in [[Akadi - Restricted]]'s place. The world has entirely forgotten [[Akadi - Restricted]].
- Consistent with the restricted [[The Pantheon]] and [[The Shattering]] pages.

## Elder-God Thread
- [[Akadi - Restricted]] is currently silent and unworshipped — parallel to [[Selûne]] before she began to stir. If the other Gods of Old wake as a second Shattering nears, Akadi is the one behind this Divine.

## The Zephyr Quill
- A mortal champion's instrument before it was a holy artifact; the power it holds is an echo of Akadi's sphere, not merely a blessed feather.
