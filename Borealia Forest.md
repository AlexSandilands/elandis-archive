Region — Location entry. Load-bearing for [[Berberis]]'s arc and the northern undead threat.

## Established
- Located north of [[Val Aran]], covering the northern reaches of Valtorra. (user / Berberis character note)
- "Undead have always been a concern in the northern woods" — small numbers have persisted there for generations. (Berberis character note)
- Dead were seen walking again circa Session 21, described as the "first time in over a century." (Session 21 prep)
- [[Berberis]]'s grove (with Quercus and [[Prunus Serrulata]]) sits just north of [[Val Aran]], at the forest's southern edge. (Berberis character note)
- Ruins deep within the forest — a Ring of Protection was recovered from "ruins north of [[Val Aran]]." (Session 22)
- In recent years, undead numbers and aggressiveness have escalated significantly. (Berberis character note)

## Geography
- Vast, old-growth forest blanketing the northern edge of Valtorra above [[Val Aran]].
- Dense and dark, with little light reaching the floor — cold even in summer.
- A ruined castle sits deep within, remnant of whoever once held power here before the forest swallowed it.

## History
- Several centuries ago, a powerful necromancer rose to power here — known only as **The Necromancer of Borealia Forest**, no name survived. Their ritual left a lasting taint on the land, and the undead never fully left afterwards.
- Since then, small numbers of undead have been an accepted hazard — a known danger for the rangers of [[Val Aran]] and those who travel deep into the forest.
- The [[Valtorran Empire]] has largely ignored the problem, treating it as background noise in a remote region.

## Current Situation
- Undead numbers have grown significantly in recent years, becoming more aggressive — enough to alarm [[Berberis]] and send him south to seek allies.
- [[Berberis]] is aware the situation is worsening but does not know the cause.
- The grove sits at the forest's southern edge, outside the worst of it; Quercus and [[Prunus Serrulata]] remain there while Berberis travels.

## Restricted
- The vision [[Berberis]] received at the Heartstone (Session 47) — *a frail figure completing a ritual in a ruined castle, the dead clawing upward through an endless dark forest* — depicted the **current necromancer**, not the historical one.
- A new necromancer has moved into the ancient ruins deep in the forest and is replicating the original ritual, inspired by the legend of their predecessor. They are the cause of the escalating undead.
- The ruins are the same site Felix's Ring of Protection was recovered from. The current necromancer has taken up residence there.
- This figure is an acolyte or aspirant — identity, motive, and full detail left undeveloped until the thread becomes active in the story.
