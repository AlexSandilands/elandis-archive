- Minor character
- Member of the Finegolds
- **Role:** Cook and Healer
**Voice:** High, old.
* **Description:** Female, mid-50s, average height and stout figure. Graying wavy hair tied in a bun, with soft brown eyes holding a warm gaze. Dressed in simple, clean clothing with a healing satchel always at her side.
* **Background:** A skilled cook and proficient healer (formerly a village nurse). She joined for a change of scenery. She is caring, considerate, and often the voice of reason.
