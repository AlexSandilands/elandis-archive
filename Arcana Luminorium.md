The Arcana Luminorium is one of the two great bastions of magical learning on [[Valtorra]], located in [[Val Luminor]]. It pre-dates the [[Valtorran Empire]] but has since been wholly absorbed into Imperial control. It serves as the primary training ground for the [[Voice of the Empire|Voices of the Empire]] and as the Empire's foremost seat of arcane authority on the continent. Its counterpart in the dwarven world is the [[Prismatic Academy]] in [[Glimmerforge]].
