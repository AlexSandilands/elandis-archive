Findings note / backlog — the calendar of [[Elandis]], surfaced while brainstorming [[Selûne]]. Not yet a Codex entry; a future **Lore — Cosmology / Concept** candidate (it's a timeless feature of how the world works), or a reference page. The machine config lives at `Campaigns/The Bloody Nails/Calendar/Configuration.md`; this note is the human-readable summary plus the lunar mechanics I worked out.

## The year

- **336 days**, **12 months of 28 days** each, **7-day weeks** (so each month is exactly four weeks).
- Months are named in **four elemental seasons** of three months each: **Water 1st–3rd, Air 1st–3rd, Fire 1st–3rd, Earth 1st–3rd**. (Same four elements as the pantheon's elemental seats — Water/Air/Fire/Earth.)
- **Weekdays:** Starday, Sunday, Moonday, Earthday, Waterday, Airday, Fireday.
- Current campaign year: **2507** (the count appears to run from the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] ~2,500 years ago — worth confirming when this becomes an entry).

## The two moons

The sky has **two moons, Sel and Sha** — named, in a truth the world has forgotten, for [[Selûne|Sel]] the moon goddess and her dark sister [[Shar|Sha]] (the [[Shar|Lady of Vengeance]]). Nobody remembers the names are theirs.

- **Sel** — 28-day cycle, no offset. Tracks the month exactly: **new on the 1st, full on the 15th of every month.**
- **Sha** — 84-day cycle (three months), offset +42.

### Worked lunar mechanics

- **Sel is full on the 15th of every month** — 12 times a year.
- **Sha is new** on day-of-year 42 / 126 / 210 / 294 — and each of those lands exactly on a 15th. So **four times a year Sel is full while Sha is new**: the **15th of Water 2nd, Air 2nd, Fire 2nd, and Earth 2nd** (every 84 days). Bright sister at her height, dark sister blind → **the Unshadowed** (see [[Selûne]]).
- **Sha is full** on day-of-year 0 / 84 / 168 / 252 — the **1st of each season's first month** (Water 1st, Air 1st, Fire 1st, Earth 1st) — and on those days **Sel is new**. The exact inverse: **dark sister full, bright sister gone** — the Lady of Vengeance's ascendant nights (a dark counterpart to the Unshadowed; see [[Shar]]).

## Holy days of the moon faith

- **Silvernight** — the 15th of every month (Sel full). The ordinary moon-night of prayer.
- **The Unshadowed** — four times a year (15th of the 2nd month of each season). The faith's highest nights, given to ritual, favours, and **the Pardon** (forgiveness). Secretly Selûne's own.
- **(Unnamed dark counterpart)** — the 1st of each season (Sha full / Sel dark). The Lady of Vengeance's nights; no public observance, since she has no open faith. Name TBD.
