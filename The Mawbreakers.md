## The Albatross

### Barak Stormeater (Captain)

Role:

Leader of the Mawbreakers in [[Camaar]] (but not of the entire guild). Captain of [[The Albatross]]

Description:

Massive man with a long scraggly red beard, never shaved, braided.

Background:

Born on a ship. Grew up in the gulf working on Fathers fishing ship. Empire regulations eventually caused father to go out of business and ended up living rest of life in misery before dying young to alcohol. Worked his way up through the Mawbreakers, has a price to settle with the Empire.

### Ari (First Mate)

Role:

First mate on the Albatross

Description:

Half orc female, scar across one eye side of head shaved, mowhawk that hangs over one side

Background:

Doesn't know who her parents were, doesn't trust easily, but would give her life for Barak as he saved her life.

Tired of Empire racism

### Gromdal Half-foot (Bosun)

Role:

Bosun on the Albatross

Description:

Male Dwarf, long black hair and beard with specks of grey starting to show. Hair is done up in a top bun, beard is captured with a tie. Has a wooden foot with a basic hinge mechanism

Background:

Fought in the Dwarven rebellions. Was in charge of leading one section of the Gemhold mines in uprising against the Empire, joined with the force that overthrew Gemhold temporarily. Marched through the tunnels to Glimmerforge but lost a foot in the battle that ultimately ended the rebellion. Played dead and was able to escape from dwarven territories, then laid low for many years before joining the Mawbreakers for his craft skills

Is aware that the youngest Dwarven Prince (Goren) was taken to the underdark before the siege of Glimmerforge, but doesn't know anything else about it.

### Name: Erun Billo (Quartermaster)

Role:

Quartermaster on the Albatross

Description:

Halfling - middle-aged with a weathered complexion. Long, straight black hair often tied back with a strip of leather. Has a collection of small, silver earrings climbing up one ear. Quite a thin physique, spends a lot of time reading and pouring over maps. Wears a small pair of round glasses.

Keen green eyes that miss nothing. A long scar runs across his left forearm, a souvenir from a close call with a pirate's blade.

Background:

Growing up as the son of a merchant in the bustling capital city of Val Luminor, Erun was well-versed in the art of negotiation and inventory management from a young age. His talents caught the attention of a passing captain who offered her a place aboard his ship. He quickly proved his worth and eventually made his way to the Albatross.

Maintains the supplies and resources of the ship, doing a lot of work in the background to ensure that everything continues to be prosperous for the crew.

### Prax Shea (Topman)

Role:

Topman on the Albatross

Description:

Half Elf female - wiry, copper hair

Background:

Born in Camaar, never knew her parents. Used to be a Veiled Cub, before leaving and joining a ships crew (age 16) and lived on the sea ever since.

### Illaris Sharin (Sugeon)

Role:

Sugeon on the Albatross

Description:

Drow Female - Long silver hair that is usually kept up in a bandana, pale grey skin, large purple eyes.

Extremely nimble fingers. Can often be found playing a lute

Background:

Used to live in the Underdark beneath the Silver mountains.

Never felt comfortable with the rest of her tribe following Lolth. This became particularly bad when the tribe began sacrificing people to the creature (adult white dragon?) that had taken residence nearby. She began speaking out, but fearing she herself would be sacrificed decided to leave, eventually making her way to the surface in order to be safe.

Illaris never knew what the creature was.

### Gorath Harbur (Cook)

Role:

Cook on the Albatross

Description:

Half-Orc - very old. Grey hair pulled back into a pony tail, braids on either side of his face.

Two small tucks from the bottom lip, right one broken

Background:

Born into tribal wars, he grew up fighting in Aetheris (central continent). Got tired of it in his middle years. Left in the middle of the night and never looked back.

Worked in some coastal cities, cooking in markets, street food stores, eventually joined the crew of a ship and wound up travelling to Voltarra, eventually joining The Albatross as they are more open minded to outsiders.

### Name: Jorund Ironsight (Master Gunner)

Role:

Master Gunner on the Albatross

Description:

Human - stout and muscular, bald with a beard like a bushfire, fiery red and just as untamed. His hands are always blackened with gunpowder, and his one eye gleams with a spark that rivals the flash of a cannon's fire.

Carries a bandolier of various fuses and priming powders across his chest.

He is a little bit crazy…

Background:

Born in the deep mountain forges of Orespire, Jorund's fascination with explosives and weaponry was evident even in his earliest years, always underfoot in the smelting rooms and furnaces. He left with Gromdal after getting into a brief disagreement with the law and joined The Albatross with him. He spends a lot of time experimenting with new weapons, which caused him to lose an eye when an explosive backfired on him.

## Other Captains

### Name: Captain Thaddeus Blackwater

Role:

Captain of the Crimson Wave

Description:

Human - A rugged sea captain in his late forties, with a deep tan and a neatly trimmed beard peppered with gray. His eyes, a piercing blue, seem to reflect the ocean itself.

Background:

Captain Thaddeus Blackwater hails from a long line of seafarers. Commanding respect with his deep, authoritative voice, he is as unyielding as the gales. Known for his unorthodox tactics, he has navigated the Crimson Wave through the perilous Sirens' Strait and lived to tell the tale.

### Name: Captain Lysandra Starling

Role:

Captain of the Nightingale

Description:

Half-Elf - Tall and slender, with an almost otherworldly grace. Her long, moonlit silver hair flows freely, and her sharp, elven features give her a look of stern beauty.

Background:

Captain Lysandra Starling was once a renowned bard who sang tales of the sea. Her love for the ocean eventually pulled her away from the taverns and onto the deck of the Nightingale. Her crew is fiercely loyal, drawn to her charisma and the haunting melodies she sings on moonlit nights.

### Name: Captain Brom Ironfist

Role:

Captain of the Stoneheart

Description:

Dwarf - A stout figure, his physical presence is as solid as the ship he commands. His fiery red beard is streaked with white, and his arms are like the thick ropes used to anchor his vessel.

Background:

From the forges of the Iron Mountains to the helm of the Stoneheart, Captain Brom Ironfist has traded the heat of the forge for the chill of the open sea. Known for his indomitable spirit and a laugh that thunders above the roar of the waves, he is as reliable in a storm as the sturdy ship he captains.

### Name: Captain Marcellus Thorn

Role:

Captain of the Whispering Wind

Description:

Human - A wiry man with a hawkish gaze and a mass of curly black hair. He moves with a predator's precision, his skin tanned and lined from years at sea.

Background:

Captain Marcellus Thorn is a tactician, as sharp as the name of his ship. His past is a tapestry of rumors and whispers, much like the wind he sails upon. Under his command, the Whispering Wind has slipped through blockades and past patrols, a ghost on the water.

### Name: Captain Fiona "Stormbringer" Galesworn

Role:

Captain of the Tempest Fury

Description:

Human - A formidable woman, with a presence that rivals the squalls of the fiercest storms. Her eyes are a steely gray, her hair a cascade of waves, as dark as the storm clouds that sailors fear.

Background:

The sea speaks to Captain Fiona "Stormbringer" Galesworn, and she listens. She earned her moniker from her uncanny ability to predict and navigate through the worst of tempests. She captains the Tempest Fury with an iron will and a heart as deep as the ocean.
