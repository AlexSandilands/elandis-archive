Morganna, a descendant of one of the ancient families that date back to pre-Shattering civilization, was always seen as the wild and eccentric one in her family. Her family's history, thought to be lost, was of little concern to her relatives, who dismissed Morganna's adventurous nature and passion for the past as the whims of a rebellious girl who shunned her noble duties. 

As a teenager, Morganna's curiosity led her to explore ancient ruins in search of forgotten history. It was during one of these expeditions that she uncovered texts detailing the incredible powers unleashed during the Chaos Wars. Among these powers, necromancy captivated her the most, as she believed it could liberate her from the constraints of her life. 

In her early twenties, Morganna's fascination with dark magic took a tragic turn when she accidentally killed her brother during a heated argument. Fearing retribution from her family, she fled and continued her pursuit of forbidden knowledge and power. 

It was during her self-imposed exile that she caught the attention of [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow|Kharazoth]], one of the three remaining red dragons that secretly roamed the world. Recognizing her potential, Kharazoth approached Morganna in disguise and guided her deeper into the dark arts of necromancy. All the while, he subtly manipulated her, nurturing her ambition to further his own ancient goal: amassing enough wealth to fuel the ritual that would bring Tiamat into the world. 

As Morganna's powers grew, Kharazoth convinced her that becoming a lich would grant her the freedom from worldly constraints and the eternal life she sought, allowing her to preserve her family's history until the end of time. However, he knew that Morganna's vanity would not tolerate the undead appearance that came with lichdom. Feigning concern, Kharazoth offered her a solution: an enchanted orb capable of not only stealing the minds of others and bringing them under her control, but also draining their life essence to maintain her youthful appearance. In exchange for this gift, Morganna had to amass power and wealth in the world, furthering Kharazoth's own ambitions. 

With her newfound power, Morganna set sail for [[Valtorra]], where she began her campaign to forge the [[The Valtorran Empire|Crimson Empire]], forever altering the course of history and leaving a dark legacy in her wake.
