Was part of Kai's backstory:
Discovered by a traveling half elf priestess Lirien who found me while investigating the burning village the following day. She ran an orphanage with a group of half elves in the forest. During my childhood they raised and taught us about nature as well as ways to survive in the wilds.  

When we turned 16 years of age we all undergo a rite of passage set by Lirien. I was tasked to journey to the snow capped mountains. These mountains were a 5 day journey on foot. While surviving in the mountains I needed to hunt a white fox. As proof I needed to bring back two canine teeth along with a white fox pelt. After successfully completing the task I traveled back to the orphanage but found the orphanage abandoned. With no trace of Lirien, the half elves or my fellow orphans. I had no choices left and so  I decided to pack up my belongings that night and set off at dawn on a new journey. A journey to find Lirien and my friends but more importantly to seek revenge for the death of my parents.
