## Timeline

### Pre - Shattering (> 2500 years ago)

- Magic is extremely rare - limited only to few people that have been plane shifted by gods
- Divine magic is also rare, but not quite as rare as arcane
- There are no monsters in the world, only fairly standard beasts and humanoid races
- Ancient civilisations were very technologically advanced (not electricity), incredible cities and deeply educated in the sciences

### The Shattering (2500 years ago)

- Tiamat cracked an opening between the dragon plane and the prime material
- This caused magic to enter the world, along with dragons and monsters

### The Chaos Wars (2500 - 2000 years ago)

[[Chaos Wars]]

### Post - Shattering (2000 years to now)

- The 3 dragons spread across the world, once again gathering wealth for the ritual, only now in secret. Plotting to insert themselves into society so they could rule by proxy or by force
- People spread out across the world again slowly rebuilding over a thousands of years
- Ruins can be found dotted around all of the continents, reminders of a world that used to be but no one remembers
- Monsters still roam the lands, but not in such a chaotic way as before during the shattering - they have learned to live in the environment
- Adventurers can sometimes find cracks of energy spread out around the world, called Riftshards, all pointing towards the location of the shattering. When in proximity to a Riftshard they feel energised, but any magic used is unpredictable
