- Member of the [[The Mawbreakers]]

Role:

Sugeon on the Albatross

Description:

Drow Female - Long silver hair that is usually kept up in a bandana, pale grey skin, large purple eyes.

Extremely nimble fingers. Can often be found playing a lute

Background:

Used to live in the Underdark beneath the Silver mountains.

Never felt comfortable with the rest of her tribe following Lolth. This became particularly bad when the tribe began sacrificing people to a creature that had taken residence nearby. She began speaking out, but fearing she herself would be sacrificed decided to leave, eventually making her way to the surface in order to be safe.

Illaris never knew what the creature was.

Joined the Mawbreakers because they accepted her without asking questions as they have many people who have left their past lives looking for new ones, she gets to stay underdeck a lot to avoid the sun, and loves the calming presence of always being on the move with the wind, and the nighttime sky on the ocean
