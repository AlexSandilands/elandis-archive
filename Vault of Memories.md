## Overview

The Verdant Vault is a vast sealed chamber located beneath the [[Sumara, The Shining City|Royal Citadel]] of Sumara. It is one of the most ancient and significant locations in elven civilization — a void left by a root of the World Tree, now a place of unpredictable and primordial magic. It has been sealed for 100 years, since the Exile.

## Location

- Beneath the Royal Citadel, accessed via a hidden staircase in the Citadel's lower level
- The entrance is a pair of enormous stone doors carved with the image of the World Tree
- The approach is below the foundation of the [[Sumara, The Shining City#The Bridge of Recollection|Bridge of Recollection]]

## Entry

The doors are sealed absolutely. They will not respond to any spell, any tool, any force. The sole exception: **Silmara blood**.

When a member of the Silmara royal bloodline places their hand upon the doors, they open.

No one has entered since the Exile, 100 years ago.

## Interior

An enormous, roughly-spherical underground chamber where a root of the World Tree once pierced the earth. The void it left behind is immense. The walls are not stone — they are compressed root-memory, ancient organic material that has petrified over millennia but retains the Wood Tree's primordial properties.

**Magical properties (unpredictable):**
- Spatial anomalies — distances may not be what they appear
- Visions from the past, particularly of the Silmara royal line
- Voices — faint, unintelligible, as though from great distance or great time
- Objects manifesting from nothing, then vanishing
- Time may behave strangely close to the chamber walls

## What Awaits Inside

*(Full detail: Session 47 prep)*

An **ancient Silver Dragon** has lived in the Vault for centuries — believed dead by the elves above. The dragon chose the Vault because only those of true Silmara blood could reach it, and waited. For what, it has not said.

## Significance

- The only proof that cannot be forged of Silmara bloodline — if Ryo can open the doors, his claim is real
- Established at the [[Sumara, The Shining City#The Confluence|Confluence of the Seven]] as the test of Ryo's heritage
- Whatever is inside may answer more than just the question of succession
