The Luminor River is the great central waterway of [[Valtorra]] and the [[Valtorran Empire|Crimson Empire]]'s spine of trade — the navigable artery that carries goods, orders, and tribute across the heart of the continent. Rising in the [[Silver Mountains]] and emptying into the [[Gulf of Miriel]], it gives the [[Luminor Plains]] their name, the capital its harbour, and the Empire the single route by which its southern shipping reaches the throne.

## Geography & Landscape

The Luminor River rises in the [[Silver Mountains]] and runs west-southwest across the breadth of the [[Luminor Plains]], threading the fertile heartland of [[Valtorra]] before reaching the sea at [[Val Miriel]], where it empties into the [[Gulf of Miriel]]. Along the way it is the defining feature of the central plains: a broad, slow, dependable channel that waters the farmland, carries the harvest, and links the great cities of the interior to the coast. Goods and orders flow both ways along it, and from the gulf at its mouth they pass out to the wider Empire.

Roughly at the midpoint of its crossing the river widens into a broad expanse of slow, deep water, and there it splits cleanly around Eventide Isle — the large, gently raised island on which the Imperial capital of [[Val Luminor]] is built, both channels running swift and deep before rejoining downstream. At its southern end the river reaches [[Val Miriel]], the continent's greatest port, where it cuts north-west to south-east through the heart of the city, dividing it into two banks joined at the single great span of [[Valena's Bridge]] before opening into the gulf. The river touches everyone's life along its course, and nowhere more visibly than in the [[Val Miriel River Market|River Market]] grown along its banks and the riverside taverns such as [[River's Watch]] that look out over the water.

## [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 19 - Blood and Sorrow]]

The party first reached the Luminor at [[Val Miriel]], the massive port city bisected by the river, and took lodging at the [[River's Watch]] tavern on its banks — their introduction to the waterway that runs through the heart of the Empire.

#### [[Session 21 - The Governor's Daughter]]

Returning to [[River's Watch]], [[Eryndor]] met his long-lost father [[Jarek]] of the [[Order of Ravens]] for a tense, poignant reunion by the river, where the old veteran revealed that Eryndor's mother might be held in the dungeons of [[Val Luminor]], upriver at the capital.
