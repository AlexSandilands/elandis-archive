**The Redleaf Inn** is the only inn in [[Faelon]], the small mining town at the foot of the southern mountains in the [[Gulf of Miriel]]. Named for the crimson canopy of the [[Everred Forest]] that surrounds the settlement, it serves as both lodging house and the town's de facto common room, where Faelon's miners and foothill families gather over ale at the end of a working day.

## Location & Geography

The inn stands among the loose cluster of stone-and-timber buildings strung along Faelon's single dirt road, with the red boughs of the [[Everred Forest]] pressing close at its back. It is a low, wide building of heavy timber beams, built broad rather than tall to shoulder the mountain winters. Inside, a fire burns through the cold months and the air carries a permanent smell of woodsmoke and pine resin. The rooms are clean and the kitchen serviceable; a bed runs at two silver a night.

## The Inn

The Redleaf is kept by Dammond and Ania, a couple who have run the place for as long as most of the town can recall. Dammond, a broad and quiet man, manages the bar and the cellar, while Ania handles the kitchen and the rooms and is the more talkative of the two. In a town with no walls, no garrison, and only informal governance, the inn's common room doubles as Faelon's town hall — serious disputes tend to find their resolution here over a drink. Its most fixed feature is Torvin, an old man who occupies the same corner of the bar most evenings, nursing his ale and recounting local tales to any traveller who will listen; he is, in effect, the town's living memory.

## [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 10.5 - Diverging Paths]]

[[The Bloody Nails]] checked into the Redleaf at dusk on arriving in Faelon to investigate a disappearance. At the bar, [[Kai]] overheard Torvin telling the tale of the ghost girl — the local legend of [[Salla the Brave]] — which set the party on the trail toward [[Tumbler's Crag]].

#### [[Session 11 - The Tale of Salla the Brave]]

The inn served as the party's base of recovery before they set out north into the snow toward [[Tumbler's Crag]].

#### [[Session 12 - How to Start a Rebellion]]

The party returned to Faelon and the Redleaf to rest after the battle beneath [[Tumbler's Crag]].
