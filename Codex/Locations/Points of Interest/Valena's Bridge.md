**Valena's Bridge** is the single great stone span that carries [[Val Miriel]] across the [[Luminor River]], joining the city's western and eastern banks. Massive, old, and raised high above the water, it stands as a landmark in its own right — and as the one crossing between the halves of the city, very nearly everyone passes over it.

## Location & Geography

The bridge sits at the heart of [[Val Miriel]], where the [[Luminor River]] cuts north-west to south-east through the city on its way down to the gulf, dividing it into a western and an eastern bank. The river enters the city from the north through Valena's Portal — a portcullis gate set where the [[Luminor River]] pierces the curtain wall, which can be lowered across the water to seal the river entrance in time of need. The bridge itself carries the city's one broad causeway across the river from bank to bank, the great west-to-east road that runs the full width of [[Val Miriel]] to the eastern gate.

It is a massive work of grey stone, built to a scale that befits its place as the city's spine. Squat towers anchor either end, and stone piers and tall arches carry the span. The bridge deck is raised a full tier above the riverside — high enough that the tall-masted ships working up the river from the gulf pass cleanly beneath it. The deck itself is kept clear as a thoroughfare; the [[Val Miriel River Market|River Market]] and its moored boat-shops crowd the banks a tier below, at the water's edge, rather than spilling onto the bridge.

## Significance

As the only crossing between the two banks, the bridge is the strategic throat of the city — whoever holds it controls movement across the [[Luminor River]]. In ordinary times it is simply the busiest stretch of stone in [[Val Miriel]], unburdened by everyday tolls or checkpoints, which would bring the city to a standstill. But the Empire watches it closely, and in a crisis it can be shut: during the unrest the span was sealed and heavily guarded, with watchmen posted on its piers and atop its towers.

The river it crosses is itself a great open artery, carrying shipping up from the gulf to the Imperial capital of [[Val Luminor]] far upstream. The bridge's height is part of what keeps that traffic moving — vessels pass beneath without ever breaking the flow of the causeway above.

## History

Valena's Bridge dates to the founding era of [[Val Miriel]] and takes its name — like the river-gate of Valena's Portal upstream — from Valena, the matriarch who first raised House Miriel's kingdom and its city, and the distant forebear of [[Vallania Miriel]].

## [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 26 - A Midnight Romp]]

With the city locked down under curfew after the unrest, [[Kai]] and [[Dondar]] made a reckless night-crossing toward the [[High Sept]]. Kai took the shape of a giant grey elk with Dondar clinging to his back and sprinted down the [[Military Way]], drawing a hail of crossbow bolts. The chase carried them to the heavily-guarded bridge, where Dondar incinerated a guard with a Fireball before both adventurers leapt from the span into the dark river below to shake their pursuers.
