The Theatre of Dreams is the grand theatre that crowns the [[Artisan Way]] in [[Val Miriel]], and the cultural centrepiece of the city. One of the four monumental buildings that stand at the heads of the city's Ways, it is the home of performance in the gulf, drawing the most talented performers of the continent — and beyond — to its stages.

## Location & Geography

The Theatre of Dreams stands on the high northern ground of [[Val Miriel]]'s eastern bank, at the head of the [[Artisan Way]] and hard against the old grey curtain wall that rings the city. From its steps the Artisan Way descends toward the [[Luminor River]] as the quarter of craft, wealth, and culture — gilt and velvet, fine shops, and live music — so that the Theatre presides over the glittering face of the city. It is a grand, ornate building, the cultural counterpart to the [[Red Bastion]], the [[Val Miriel - Governor's Keep|Governor's Keep]], and the [[High Sept]] that crown the other three Ways.

## The Stages

At the Theatre's heart is a great opera house, and around it stand several smaller stages and studios given over to performances of every kind. Audiences travel from far afield to see the finest performers the continent has to offer, and its reputation reaches further still — to many it is counted among the great venues of the world.
