The Shield Wall is the great sea-fortification of [[Val Solis]] — a structure so old and so formidable that the [[Valtorran Empire]] sent three fleets to break it, and turned back after losing all three.

## Location & Geography

Sailors who have passed through the north-western sea lanes describe it simply: the wall comes up before the island does. Vast, dark, and ancient-looking, it sweeps across the western approach to [[Val Solis]] in a long arc, its towers rising from the waterline into low cloud. The single gate visible in its face has not been seen open within living memory. Nothing passes in or out.

## Reputation

The Shield Wall's reputation rests almost entirely on what the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] failed to do. Three separate expeditions were sent to force the harbour; none succeeded. What exactly broke them — whether the Wall's defenders used fire, or some older weapon, or simply the narrowness of the approach — varies in the telling. Sailors who have passed within sight of it at distance report something vast and cold and clearly still manned. Whether anyone has gotten close enough to see more and lived to describe it accurately is uncertain.

Most of what is said about the Wall by those who have not seen it is old enough to be legend — that it was built before the Kingdoms of Old, that it predates the Chaos Wars, that it was raised by methods no longer known. None of this has been confirmed. The Wall stands, and the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] stopped trying. That much, at least, is fact.
