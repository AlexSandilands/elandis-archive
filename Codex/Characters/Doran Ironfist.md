**Doran Ironfist** is a dwarven blacksmith and wagon-wright travelling with [[The Finegolds]], the merchant caravan led by [[Gemma Finegold]]. A craftsman of genuine skill and easy warmth, he is the kind of man who keeps a caravan rolling — literally and figuratively — and who can fill an hour around the fire with stories of the trade.

## Biography

Doran spent most of his life in [[Silverdeep]], where he built a reputation as a reliable smith with a flair for fine metalwork. He might have stayed there indefinitely had he not crossed paths with [[Gemma Finegold]], who befriended him and, in her particular way, opened his eyes to a wider world. She offered him a place with her caravan: steady work tending the wagons and horses, fair pay, and the promise of adventure along the road. He accepted, and has not looked back.

On the caravan he serves as blacksmith and wagon-wright — repairing broken axles, reshoing horses, sizing armour, and appraising metalwork when it crosses his path. He takes evident pride in his craft and is quick to share opinions on workmanship, whether the subject is a goblin's battered splint mail or a masterwork greatsword of unknown origin.

#### [[Session 08 - The High Road]]

Rejoined the caravan alongside Gemma after a brief absence, distributing the reward from the party's goblin contract — 820 gold split among the group.

Assisted the party in [[Camaar]] at his smithy, the Stonehearth. Resized a goblin splint mail for Vaeleran and examined an engraved greatsword recovered on the road, confirming it was of masterwork quality and custom-made, though the maker was unknown to him.

#### [[Session 19 - Blood and Sorrow]]

Found among the captives held at a bandit camp in the ruins of Farhaven, along with the rest of [[The Finegolds]]. Freed by the party following a fierce ambush.
