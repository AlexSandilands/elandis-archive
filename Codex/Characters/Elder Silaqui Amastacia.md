**Elder Silaqui Amastacia** is the elven Meridian of [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]]'s [[Circle of the Dawn]] — the city's physicians, clerics, and alchemists — and the keeper of its great glass infirmary, the [[Luminarium]]. A soft-spoken, deeply empathetic cleric who rarely leaves her botanical healing halls, she is widely regarded as the most compassionate of the city's public figures.

## Biography

Silaqui tends the sick from within the Luminarium, a conservatory of glass and copper whose living vines trail beneath it like ribbons when it floats. Her Circle's medicine leans heavily on the unique and potent flora of the [[Faewild]], coaxed to bloom beneath the conservatory's glass, and she rarely strays far from those infirmary beds. Her manner sets the tone of the Dawn itself: decisions are made gently, by consensus, and the Circle leads with kindness before authority.

## [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 47 - Confluence of the Seven]]

At the [[Sumara, The Shining City#The Confluence of the Seven|Confluence of the Seven]], Silaqui was the first of the uncertain Meridians to engage the party directly — asking them, not [[Ryo]], what kind of person he was. When [[Berberis]] answered plainly — *"He does what needs to be done. He keeps his word."* — she visibly softened. She raised her hand in favour of opening the [[Vault of Memories]], joining [[Illythia Voss]], [[Warden Sylvaris]], [[Master Artisan Fenian|Fenian]], and [[Justiciar Naivara Siannodel|Naivara Siannodel]] in a five-to-two result.
