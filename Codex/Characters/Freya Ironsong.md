**Freya Ironsong** is a gnomish woodworker of [[Darmouth]], one half of the twin sisters who run the [[Darmouth#Ironsong Forge / Lumberloft *(smithy — mid-grade and woodworking)*|Ironsong Forge & Lumberloft]] on the southern edge of the city's trading quarter. Pink-haired to her sister's blue, she is the quieter of the two — content to let her craft speak for itself and leave the patter to her sister — and no less skilled for it.

## Biography

Freya grew up in [[Orespire]], the forge city of the [[Silver Mountains]], where she and her twin sister [[Thora Ironsong|Thora]] were raised around its furnaces and workshops before setting out to travel. When the pair heard of new opportunities in a young Imperial port to the south, they settled in [[Darmouth]], where Freya's lumberloft handles the custom woodwork beside Thora's forge, the two workshops sharing a courtyard. Reserved and exacting, Freya is happy to stay at her bench and let Thora work the counter — she rolls her eyes at her sister's endless chatter, but privately she is grateful Thora is there to handle the customers so she doesn't have to.
