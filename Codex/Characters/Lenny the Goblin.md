**Lenny the Goblin** is a male goblin and former raider of the tribe that preyed on travellers along the high road between [[Camaar]] and [[Darmouth]]. Captured by [[The Bloody Nails]] after a failed ambush, he struck a desperate bargain that spared his life — and has since left the life of a bandit behind entirely, harbouring a quiet, earnest dream of one day running a small shop selling handmade jewellery alongside the artwork of his wife, [[Ellie the Goblin]].

## Biography

Lenny served among the ambushers of a goblin tribe that had been raiding merchant caravans on the high road between [[Camaar]] and [[Darmouth]]. When [[The Bloody Nails]] turned the tables and sprang their own trap, Lenny was the sole survivor. Frightened and cornered, he chose his life over his loyalty: under heavy intimidation, he gave up the location of the tribe's lair — an ancient temple hidden in the nearby cliffsides — in exchange for a promise that he would be spared.

His tribe's lair was subsequently cleared by the party. Lenny himself was held captive until he could be reunited with [[Ellie the Goblin|Ellie]], his wife, who had been found hiding within the lair. At the [[Gemma Finegold|Finegold]] merchant camp, the party honoured their deal: both goblins were released, warned never to return to the high road, and left to find their own way. Lenny and Ellie fled together into the trees.

What drives Lenny now is not survival but ambition of an entirely different kind. He wants to open a shop — somewhere peaceful, somewhere far from raids and bounty hunters — where he can sell handmade jewellery and Ellie's paintings. The dream is small by most measures. To Lenny, it is everything.
