**Korvin Blackfeather** is the legendary founder and first Grand Raven of the [[Order of Ravens]], the criminal and intelligence network whose reach still threads through Valtorra centuries after his death. A figure of legend more than record, he is remembered not for the man he was but for the single audacious act that gave a scattered underworld a name and a symbol.

## Biography

By legend, Korvin made his name with a heist so daring it passed into myth — the theft of a tyrant king's treasure, an exploit still recounted as the **Raven's Flight**. On the strength of that legend he drew Valtorra's scattered thieves, smugglers, and cutthroats under a single banner, founding the Order of Ravens and taking its highest seat as the first Grand Raven.

The Order he built outlived him by generations. For centuries it held to the principle he set at its founding — political neutrality, never taking a side in the broader struggles of the continent, manipulating events only for its own ends. Of the man himself, little reliable survives: his origin, his people, and the age in which he lived are all lost behind the legend of the Raven's Flight.
