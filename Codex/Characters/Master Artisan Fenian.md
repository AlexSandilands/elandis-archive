**Master Artisan Fenian** is the elven Meridian of [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]]'s [[Circle of the Loom]] — the city's builders and enchanters — and the master hand behind its physical fabric, presiding from the [[Architecton]]. A consummate perfectionist, he regards the whole of Sumara as a single ongoing work of art: one that can always be refined, and never truly finished.

## Biography

From the Architecton, the Loom weaves magic into stone and glass to raise and maintain Sumara's physical fabric; every Nexus, aqueduct, and bridge of solidified light in the city is their work. Fenian sits on the Council of Circles as the Loom's voice on matters of city-wide governance, a cautious and watchful presence who reads the room rather than the speaker. He follows strength, and is moved by demonstrated practical benefit far sooner than by sentiment.

## [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 47 - Confluence of the Seven]]

At the [[Sumara, The Shining City#The Confluence of the Seven|Confluence of the Seven]], Fenian was among the council's persuadable voices — cautious, watching the room and waiting to see how the debate would resolve. He ultimately raised his hand in favour of opening the [[Vault of Memories]], joining [[Illythia Voss]], [[Warden Sylvaris]], [[Justiciar Naivara Siannodel|Naivara]], and [[Elder Silaqui Amastacia|Silaqui]] in a five-to-two result.
