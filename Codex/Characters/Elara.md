**Elara** is a human mage and proprietor of the [[Gilded Grimoire]], a magic shop in [[Camaar]]. Studious and endlessly preoccupied, she conducts her work — identification, consultation, and trade in arcane wares — with genuine expertise, even when that work happens to be conducted from three feet off the ground.

## Biography

Elara runs the [[Gilded Grimoire]] as both a retail establishment and a practising consultancy, offering magical identification, item crafting, and the kind of unhurried academic advice that only someone deeply uninterested in social convention can provide. Her blonde hair is typically bundled up with a red ribbon, a practical concession she makes when she remembers to make it. She is warm and capable, but her mind is rarely entirely present in a conversation — often she is working through a problem at the same time, using whatever method seems most useful to her, regardless of how it appears to others. Floating to the ceiling to think, for instance, is simply how some problems get solved.

## [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 10 - The Coastal Cave]]

The party visited the [[Gilded Grimoire]] for identification services. Elara successfully identified several items — a *Dagger of Warning*, a *Rope of Climbing*, *Dust of Disappearance*, and a variety of potions — but was unable to identify a strange White Gemstone recovered from a goblin cave, noting only that it felt "deep and ancient" and unlike any standard magic she had encountered.

#### [[Session 17 - A New Journey Begins]]

The party returned to the [[Gilded Grimoire]] and found Elara floating on the ceiling, mid-thought, via a *Fly* spell. She picked up the conversation without remarking on the situation. After some skilled negotiation by [[Vaeleran]], she extended a significant bulk discount on the party's purchases, which included a *Bag of Holding*, a *Cloak of Elvenkind*, a *Decanter of Endless Water*, a *Scroll of Dispel Magic*, and a set of +1 arrows.
