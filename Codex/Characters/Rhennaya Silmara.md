**Rhennaya Silmara** is an elven leader of the [[Silmara Family]] royal bloodline who guided [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]] through one of its most consequential decisions — the decision to open its borders to the elves fleeing the rise of the [[Valtorran Empire]]. Remembered by her people as a fair and temperate ruler, she was the wife of [[Ratha Silmara]], the mother of [[Ryo]], and the sister of [[Eirieneth Silmara]]. She was killed by [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]].

## Biography

Rhennaya was born into the [[Silmara Family]], the ancient royal line of the Sumaran elves, and came to hold a place of quiet authority within the [[Sumara, The Shining City|Shining City]] that was grounded less in ceremony than in trust. She wore a simple circlet of silver, green, and brown — shaped like the roots and leaves of the World Tree — and was known to step into the [[The Confluence of the Seven|Confluence of the Seven]] only when the council had reached an impasse it could not break on its own. That restraint, as much as any act of governance, earned her the respect of her people.

When the [[Valtorran Empire]] rose and the elves of Valtorra had nowhere left to stand, it was Rhennaya who made the call: the Valtorran elves would be given refuge in Sumara. It was a decision that reshaped the city and the future of her people.

She married [[Ratha Silmara]], who was then of the [[Circle of the Blades]], and together they had a son. She was friends with both [[Ayana Syndrosa]] and [[Illythia Voss]]. Her sister [[Eirieneth Silmara]] chose to remain in Valtorra rather than join the exodus — a choice Rhennaya could not fully countermand — and was later taken by the Empire.

Rhennaya did not survive to see what became of either her son or her sister. She and [[Ratha Silmara|Ratha]] were killed by [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]], run through together with the Dread Sword in a single thrust. Her son [[Ryo]] — placed into the care of others as an infant to keep him safe — grew up knowing nothing of his origins.

### [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 46 - The Shining City]]

[[Ayana Syndrosa]] revealed to the party that [[Ryo]] is the son of Rhennaya and [[Ratha Silmara]] — the last surviving heir of the [[Silmara Family|Silmara]] royal bloodline. Ayana also disclosed that Rhennaya had a sister, [[Eirieneth Silmara|Eirieneth]], who fell in love with a human and stayed behind during the exodus, making [[Eryndor]] and Ryo first cousins.

#### [[Session 47 - Confluence of the Seven]]

During the descent into the Vault of Memories beneath the Royal Citadel, [[Ryo]] witnessed a vision of his parents: [[Ratha Silmara|Ratha]] standing beside Rhennaya seated on a chair, young and happy together. He also saw the moment of their deaths — Ratha holding a wounded Rhennaya as [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]] stepped forward and drove the Dread Sword through them both.

#### [[Session 49 - The Silver Sage]]

The vision of Rhennaya and [[Ratha Silmara|Ratha]]'s death was replayed within [[Berberis]]'s memory pool, confirming [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]] as their killer. An earlier vision showed a hooded Rhennaya handing an infant [[Ryo]] to a gnomish stranger in the dark before turning and walking away — the moment she gave up her son to protect him.

#### [[Session 50 - A New Morning in Sumara]]

[[Arcanist Levvel]] briefly touched [[Dread's Hunger]] during inspection and recoiled — through the blade she had seen Rhennaya and [[Ratha Silmara|Ratha]] killed. The sword carries the memory of their deaths.
