**Captain Erendir Thalos** is a half-elven officer of [[Val Noren]], serving as commander of [[King Marius Noren]]'s guard and the Keep's defences. Steady-handed and grimly loyal, he was born into the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire's]] occupation of the north and has known nothing else — but his hatred of it is not abstract.

## Biography

Erendir was born in [[Val Noren]] to a human guardsman father and an elven mother, a child of the occupied north in every sense. He was young when [[Valtorran Empire|Imperial]] forces pushed hard into the city — his mother was killed in the assault. His father raised him in the aftermath with little ceremony and few words about it, but the shape of that loss informed everything that followed: the guard career begun young, the grimness that sits just beneath a composed exterior, the quiet certainty that he is owed a reckoning with the Empire on behalf of someone who will never see it.

He rose through the city guard on the strength of his composure and his knowledge of [[Val Noren]]'s streets and defences. When [[King Marius Noren]] established his hold at the Keep, Erendir was among the first officers to present himself. Marius gave him the captaincy without hesitation. He has held it with the same steady intensity ever since — less concerned with rank than with seeing the north free before he is old.

### [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 38 - A Trail of Blood]]

Introduced to the party by [[Barak Stormrider]] in the aftermath of the undead attack on [[Val Noren]]. Delivered the grim intelligence that the King's kidnappers had worn Rebel armour to bypass defences, and that witnesses placed [[General Korvas]] — known to the party as Vaeleran — at their head.

#### [[Session 41 - Reclaim the Reclaimer]]

Present at a war council with [[King Marius Noren]] and [[Ayana Syndrosa]] following the King's rescue, assessing the military stalemate. The [[Valtorran Empire|Imperial]] army had massed at the bridge into the city, creating a chokepoint neither side had broken.
