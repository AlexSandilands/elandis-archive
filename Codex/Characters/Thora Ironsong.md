**Thora Ironsong** is a gnomish blacksmith of [[Darmouth]], one half of the twin sisters who run the Ironsong Forge & Lumberloft on the southern edge of the city's trading quarter. Blue-haired to her sister's pink, she is the talkative one — happy to walk any customer through every choice of steel and temper whether they asked or not — and the face that greets you across the counter.

## Biography

Thora was born in [[Orespire]], the great forge city of the [[Silver Mountains]], and learned her smithing in the shadow of its furnaces before she and her twin sister [[Freya Ironsong|Freya]] set out travelling. Hearing of new opportunities in a young Imperial port to the south, the pair settled in Darmouth and set up the Ironsong Forge alongside Freya's adjacent lumberloft, the two workshops sharing a single courtyard. The forge turns out weapons and armour of genuine, mid-grade quality, and Thora's easy chatter does as much to keep custom coming as the work itself. She and Freya make a natural team: Thora works the steel and the customers, Freya the finer woodwork — and where Thora's exuberance earns an eye-roll from her quieter sister, it also closes the sale.
