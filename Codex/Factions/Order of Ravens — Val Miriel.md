The **Val Miriel Nest** is the [[Order of Ravens]]' chapter in [[Val Miriel]] — a cell of thieves and spies run out of a tavern cellar in the city's artisan quarter and led by the Nest Father [[Thaddeus Karr\|Thaddeus "Ravenwing" Karr]]. It is among the Order's more active presences, and the heist that defines it helped tip the city into open unrest.

## Overview & Base

The Nest is headquartered beneath [[The Gilded Crow]], a tavern on the Artisan Way, with its entrance concealed behind a keg in the cellar; past it, the chapter is built directly into the [[The Underrun|Underrun]], the warren of passages beneath Val Miriel. Entry is granted to those who give the passphrase — *"I'll have one of your sharpest drinks"* — spoken to the right ear at the bar above.

From this hidden seat Thaddeus "Ravenwing" Karr directs the Order's work in the city: gathering intelligence on the Imperial presence, sheltering operatives passing through, and running the jobs handed down from above. The chapter keeps the tight, compartmentalised discipline the Order is built on, and the Nest Father expects word sent ahead from other Nests to be honoured when contacts arrive.

## Local Operations

- **The Bastion heist.** The Nest broke into [[The Bastion]] and stole sensitive Imperial documents. The job went clean until the Fledgling Lia abandoned the plan to attempt the assassination of [[General Varan Brado]] without sanction — an act that helped ignite the Val Miriel riots and left her fate an unresolved problem for Thaddeus to manage.

Word reached the party that Thaddeus "Ravenwing" Karr and the Val Miriel Nest expected them, with a further contact waiting at the Val Aran Nest down the road.

The heist unravelled when Lia broke from the plan to make her attempt on General Varan Brado, leaving Thaddeus to contain the fallout as the city began to riot.
