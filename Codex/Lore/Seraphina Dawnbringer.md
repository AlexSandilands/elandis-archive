**Seraphina Dawnbringer** is the goddess of the sun — patron of dawn, renewal, hope, and healing — and one of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]] of [[Elandis]]. Her relic is the **Dawnstar**, a halberd said to blaze with the light of morning.

## Overview

Seraphina holds the sphere of the sun and all that flows from it: the dawn, the light that heals, and the renewal that follows suffering. She is counted among the Seven as the patron of hope and new beginnings, and the Dawnstar — her blazing halberd — stands as the emblem of her sphere.

## Worship

### The First Light

Kept at dawn on **Sunday**, the week's sun-day: a candle lit at first light — in sickrooms and at the bedsides of the dying above all — with a prayer that the Dawnbringer carry the sufferer through the dark hours and into the morning. It is the rite of healers and of those who keep the night vigil, who greet the Sunday sunrise as the week's renewal of her light.

## In Memory & Belief

Seraphina is widely worshipped across [[Elandis]], her name invoked wherever there is hope sought or healing needed. Little beyond this broad devotion is reliably recorded.
