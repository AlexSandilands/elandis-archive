**Eldrin Starweaver** is the god of magic — patron of the arcane, of knowledge, and of the Weave that threads through all things in [[Elandis]] — and one of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]]. His relic is the **Weave Loom**, an ancient tome said to shape the very Weave of magic.

## Overview

Of the Seven Divines, Eldrin Starweaver governs the sphere of magic itself — the Weave, the invisible current that makes all arcane working possible — and with it the domain of knowledge and understanding that the practice of the art demands. He is revered by scholars, spellcasters, and all those whose work turns on learning, and is counted among the seven great powers that have held the world together since the end of the war that ended the old age. His relic, the Weave Loom, is held by the faith to be the most direct expression of his dominion: a tome said to be capable of shaping and reshaping the flow of magic itself.

## Worship

### The Starwatch

Kept at **midnight on Starday**, the week's first day and the day of the stars: worshippers go out beneath the open sky and say their prayers under the constellation they were born beneath, reading in it both where they have come from and where the Weave may yet carry them.

## In Memory & Belief

Eldrin Starweaver is widely worshipped across [[Elandis]], honoured in libraries and arcane institutions as much as in formal temples. Beyond his sphere and his relic, little of his history is commonly recorded — the deep accounts of the Seven Divines belong to the older and more obscure strata of the faith, and most of what the world holds of the Starweaver is practical: the god of magic, to whom those who work with it offer their devotion.

## Campaign

### [[The Bloody Nails|Campaign: The Bloody Nails]]

#### [[Session 06 - The Goblin Lair]]

The party fell through a trapdoor into an ancient sealed shrine to Eldrin Starweaver in the wilds of [[Valtorra]] — a **Trial of the Weave**, built into the bedrock as a lethal puzzle-room. They escaped with moments to spare.

#### [[Session 07 - Grakthor Falls]]

Fighting through the same shrine, the party found its magic dangerously unstable — every spell cast triggered a wild surge of blue lightning, disorienting whispers, and gravity shifts. [[Vaeleran]] was struck by a vision of a pale hand holding a crackling purple orb. After the battle, [[Kai]] observed a **crack in space** hovering near the shrine's ceiling — something the party could not explain and did not stay to investigate.
