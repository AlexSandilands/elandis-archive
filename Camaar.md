# Camaar

## Districts
The city is informally split into two distinct sections:
* **Old Camaar (North):** The older part of the city; generally a bit more run down and dangerous.
* **New Camaar (South):** The hub of newer commerce and development.

---

## Factions & Strongholds

### The Mawbreakers' Stronghold
A robust stone structure overlooking the docks, adorned with maritime insignias and flags. A sense of disciplined activity always surrounds it.

---

## Inns & Taverns

### The Lion Inn
**Description:** A two-story establishment with a wooden lion carving at the entrance. Known for its warm ambiance and plush rooms.
* **Innkeeper:** [[Mistress Serin]] — Graceful, with a reputation for discretion.
* **Entertainment:** [[Brevin]] — A bard who entertains guests in the evenings.

### Harbor's Edge Tavern
**Description:** A lively tavern close to the docks, always brimming with sailors and tales of the sea.
* **Owner:** [[Grimm]] — A burly man with a booming laugh and a penchant for tall tales.

---

## Shops & Commerce

### Thornwick's General
**Description:** A spacious store with a variety of goods stacked on wooden shelves, from dried foods and traveling rations to ropes, lanterns, and simple clothing. The scent of dried herbs mingles with the warm aroma of fresh bread from a small bakery corner. There's a large counter at the back for more valuable items.
* **Owner:** [[Master Thornwick]] — A portly man with a bushy mustache and a jovial demeanor. He's known to haggle but always offers a fair deal.
* **Assistant:** [[Lila]] — Thornwick's young niece who helps around the shop, organizing shelves or assisting customers.

### Cobblestone Apothecary
**Description:** A fragrant shop filled with neatly labeled jars of herbs, potions, and remedies.
* **Alchemist:** [[Thane]] — Middle-aged, known for his experimental brews.

### The Gilded Grimoire
**Description:** A bookstore specializing in arcane texts, scrolls, and magical paraphernalia.
* **Owner:** [[Elara]] — A studious mage who runs the store and often provides magical consultations.

### Stonehearth Blacksmith
**Description:** A forge with a roaring fire, producing weapons, armor, and everyday tools.
* **Blacksmith:** [[Dorn Stonehearth]] — Muscular, with a reputation for crafting the finest blades in Camaar.

### Whispering Woods Bowyer
**Description:** A shop dedicated to crafting and selling bows, crossbows, and arrows.
* **Owner:** [[Lyn]] — A skilled archer and craftswoman, known for her exquisite custom-made bows.

### Starlight Emporium
**Description:** A large store selling gemstones, jewelry, and artifacts. Its windows glisten with star-like twinkles from the items inside.
* **Owner:** [[Lord Varan]] — A nobleman turned merchant, known for his exquisite taste and vast collection.

### Moonlit Menagerie
**Description:** An exotic pet shop selling creatures both mundane and magical.
* **Owner:** [[Nyra]] — A gentle soul with a deep affinity for animals and creatures of all kinds.

### Lorelei's Loom
**Description:** A textile shop filled with vibrant fabrics, tapestries, and garments.
* **Owner:** [[Lorelei]] — An elderly woman with a keen eye for detail and unmatched skill in weaving.

### Bronzewind Bakery
**Description:** A cozy bakery emitting the delightful aroma of freshly baked bread and pastries.
* **Baker:** [[Jessa Bronzewind]] — Cheerful and famous for her cinnamon rolls.
