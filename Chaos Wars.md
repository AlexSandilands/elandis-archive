A period of 500 years of chaos after [[The Shattering]]
- And endless war between the ancient civilisations and dragons / monsters
- Tiamat's goal was for the dragons to collect enough gemstones so they could be used as arcane reagents in a ritual to permanently open a large enough door between the realms, allowing her to enter.
- People began being born with magic - previously there might be one in 10000 people who could do magic, now it was more like 1 in 10
- This allowed the people of the world a fighting chance, but at huge cost - cities were raised to the ground, who knows what knowledge was lost
- There was a final showdown at the portal of the Shattering - the majority of the people on the planet showed up and the dragons retreated to defend the portal. Eventually when it looked like the dragons were slowly gaining the advantage 7 strong archmages, heroes of the realm used their final option, and joint ritual that caused massive devastation closing the portal (seemingly) but kill 90% of people and dragons who were in the region.
- The portal was not completely closed, invisible cracks can be found throughout the world.
