# Roll Tables

A growing collection of rollable tables for improvising at the table. Add new sets under their own `##` heading.

## Sumara — Unnamed NPCs & Shops

> [!info]+ Quick rolls for unnamed NPCs and shops the party encounters in passing in [[Sumara, The Shining City]]. Mix freely — a sylvan named on the second column can run a shop rolled on the second table; a fungral can be a passing pedestrian. Surnames are optional for sylvans; fungrals and pixies go by a single name.
