### Acquisition and Maintenance 

- Purchasing Horses: Prices vary by type. Specialized or rare horses may cost more. 
- Feeding and Upkeep: 
    - Feed: 5 cp per day per horse. 
    - Stabling: 5 sp per night in a stable. 
    - Shoeing: 2 gp per month for new horseshoes or maintenance. 

### Training and Bonding 

- Training a Horse: Takes 10 days and a successful DC 15 Animal Handling check to teach a specific skill (e.g., battle maneuvers, tricks). 
- Bonding: Spending time with your horse increases loyalty. Each week of consistent care and positive interaction grants a cumulative +1 to future Animal Handling checks with that horse, up to a maximum of +5.
