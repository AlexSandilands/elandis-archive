### Pace

***Slow Pace:*** 20m a day (100m in 5 days) - Can Stealth 
***Normal Pace:*** 25m a day (100m in 4 days) 
***Fast Pace:*** 33m a day (100m in 3 days) - -5 to Perception 

Mounts allows travelling at Fast Pace without penalty 

[Source, PHB](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/phb/adventuring#Movement)
