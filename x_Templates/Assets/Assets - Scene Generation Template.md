**Style Name:** _Semi-Realistic Fantasy Landscape Art_  
**Visual Traits:**

- **Medium:** Digital painting
- **Palette:** Cold and muted tones (mainly icy blues, greys, and desaturated whites)
- **Lighting:** Diffused, soft atmospheric lighting; overcast sky with mist or haze to convey depth
- **Detail Level:** High fidelity in the environment, but stylized — not photorealistic; textures are painterly but rich
- **Mood:** Vast, desolate, awe-inspiring; evokes wonder and isolation
- **Composition Elements:**
    - Monumental natural features (e.g., jagged ice spires, frozen plains)
    - Far-reaching views with strong depth perspective
    - Cracked ice surfaces, layered terrain, and environmental storytelling
- **Inspiration Sources:** Works from concept artists like _Jordan Grimmer_, _Raphael Lacoste_
