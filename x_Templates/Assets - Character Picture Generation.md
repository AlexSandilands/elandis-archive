### Artistic Style

- **Fantasy RPG aesthetic:** Bold, painterly strokes with detailed textures and high-contrast lighting.
- **Color palette:** Rich and dramatic, with warm tones (golden lantern light) contrasting against cool shadows.
- **Focus:** Emphasis on character details and mood, with less photo-realism and more artistic expression.
- **Composition:** Balanced and dynamic, drawing attention to the central figure while maintaining environmental context.

### Character Design

- **Person:** A woman in her 20s with red hair, happy expression
- **Clothes:** Travelling clothes, greens and leathers, linen top, tight around the chest
- **Pose:** Driving a caravan

### Setting

- **Environment:** Driving a merchant caravan through a forest
- **Lighting:** Middle of the day lighting

### Techniques

- **Lighting and Shadow:** Strong interplay of light and shadow to create depth and drama.
- **Texture:** Visible brushstrokes for wood grain, fabric folds, and metallic surfaces.
- **Depth:** Slight blurring or soft focus in the background to draw attention to the subject.
