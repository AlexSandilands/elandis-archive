## Districts

The hub. One subsection per quarter — lead with the district's character, then catalogue its establishments inline. Tier B establishments get a sub-heading and full detail; Tier C are named in the prose. NPCs are described inline, the name **not bolded**, linking out to a character page where one exists. Establishment headings stay **plain text**; when an establishment graduates to its own page, the wikilink goes in the first sentence or two of its description, not in the heading. Don't link an establishment until it has its own page.
