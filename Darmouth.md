# Darmouth

## Inns & Taverns

### The Iron Anchor
**Quality:** Modest (5s per night)
A popular, bustling tavern where sailors and locals gather to swap stories and enjoy a drink.
- **Proprietor:** [[Captain Barnaby "Old Salt" Hargrave]] — A retired sailor with a wealth of knowledge about the sea and the [[Mawbreakers]].

### The Seafarer's Rest Inn
**Quality:** Comfortable (8s per night)
A clean, comfortable inn catering to travelers and merchants. It prides itself on offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
- **Owner:** [[Eliza Farthington]] — A kind-hearted widow.

### The Wharf Rat
**Quality:** Poor (1s per night)
A seedy, dimly lit tavern frequented by pirates, smugglers, and other unsavory characters.
- **Owner:** [[ "One-Eyed" Jack Flint]] — A rough and gruff man known to turn a blind eye to illegal activities in exchange for a few extra coins.

### The Golden Hook
**Quality:** Aristocratic (4g per night)
A renowned seafood restaurant serving freshly caught fish and other delicacies.
- **Chef/Owner:** [[Henri Leclair]] — Charismatic and reputable for his innovative and flavorful dishes.

## Guilds & Organizations

### The Green Griffin Mercenary Guild
A well-established guild where adventurers and mercenaries can find work, training, and camaraderie. The guild is known for taking on dangerous and high-paying jobs.
- **Leader:** [[Commander Alaric Brack]]
	- *Description:* Human, middle-aged, heavy set, shaggy red hair, braided beard (Viking style).

### The Tilted Scales Merchant Guild
The hub for the city's wealthy merchants and traders. It serves as a place for negotiations, agreements, and the setting of trade policies within the city and beyond.
- **Guildmaster:** [[Lysandra Goldspindle]] — A shrewd and intelligent businesswoman. Known for her knack for diplomacy and negotiation, she constantly strives to ensure fair commerce.

### The Azure Sail Shipwrights
A reputable shipbuilding company responsible for constructing and repairing many of the vessels that sail the [[Gulf of Miriel]].
- **Shipwright:** [[Morgan Redwood]] — Highly skilled in the craft.

## Shops & Commerce

### Market Square
A bustling marketplace filled with stalls offering fresh produce, fish, spices, and various imported goods. A lively place for locals to shop, trade, and socialize.

### Ironsong Forge / Lumberloft
A well-regarded blacksmith known for crafting high-quality weapons and armor, situated next to the lumber yard.
- **Blacksmith:** [[Thora Ironsong]] — Talented smith, eager to discuss her craft.
- **Woodworker:** [[Freya Ironsong]] — Thora's twin sister who works in the Lumberloft next door.
- *Description:* Both are gorgeous gnomes in their late 20s, with wavy brown hair tied up into pigtails with matching ties.

### The Silver Waves Alchemist
An alchemy shop offering a wide variety of potions, ingredients, and magical supplies.
- **Owner:** [[Agatha Nightshade]] — A mysterious and enigmatic alchemist.

### The Albatross Trading Company
A prominent trading company with connections throughout the Empire.
- **Owner:** [[Jasper Harrington]] — An ambitious merchant always seeking new opportunities and partnerships.

### The Tidal Pearl
A fine jewelry store specializing in pearls and other precious gems found in the region. Caters to the wealthy and discerning clientele of Darmouth.
- **Proprietor:** [[Lady Isabella Montague]] — Elegant and refined.

### Sundew General Store
A friendly, family-run general goods store that offers a wide range of everyday items and supplies.
- **Owners:** [[Oliver Sundew]] and [[Maribel Sundew]] — A cheerful husband-and-wife team.

## Government & Law

### Town Hall (Governor's Hall)
The civic center of Darmouth, representing the might and prosperity of the Empire. It's where the city's officials conduct affairs.
- **Governor:** [[Eamon Hightower]] — A seasoned politician and negotiator, working tirelessly to ensure Darmouth's growth.

### The Imperial Lawmaster's Office
Positioned near the center of the city, this formidable edifice is where order is maintained and Imperial laws are interpreted.
- **Lawmaster:** [[Adelia Veridhart]] — A no-nonsense, hardworking woman with an unerring sense of justice, known for fair and impartial judgment.

### Darmouth Guard Barracks
A well-fortified and orderly building housing the city's guard and local law enforcement.
- **Captain:** [[Gavric Stonehelm]] — Commands the guards with a firm but fair hand.

### Darmouth Bastille
Located on the outskirts, this is a grim and foreboding fortress where those who break the Empire's laws are confined. Thick stone walls and constant patrols make it inescapable.
- **Warden:** [[Korban Slate]] — A stern, unyielding, and incorruptible man who maintains strict order with relentless efficiency.

### Harbor Master's Office
A stately building overseeing the comings and goings of ships.
- **Harbor Master:** [[Linus Clearwater]] — Ensures the smooth operation of the port.

## Faith & Knowledge

### Temple of the Seven Divines
A beautiful and serene temple dedicated to the worship of the Seven.
- **High Priestess:** [[Sister Arianna Brightstar]] — Wise and compassionate, she offers guidance and blessings to those who seek the aid of the goddess.

### The Mariner's Library
A quiet, well-stocked library dedicated to the collection and preservation of maritime knowledge, charts, and lore.
- **Librarian:** [[Seraphina Blackwood]] — Knowledgeable and always eager to assist visitors in their research.
