## Leadership

### [[Harland Finegold]]
**Role:** Lead Merchant
**Voice:** Low, Scottish. Rich and jovial with a slight lisp.
* **Description:** Male, late 40s, portly with a twinkle in his hazel eyes. He sports a well-groomed mustache and thinning, sandy hair. Always impeccably dressed in vibrant merchant robes, adorned with various trinkets.
* **Background:** Harland is a prosperous merchant known for his business acumen. He is a shrewd negotiator but is also well-liked for his friendly demeanor and infectious laughter.

### [[Gemma Finegold]]
**Role:** Harland's Daughter and Assistant
**Voice:** High, Scottish.
* **Description:** Female, early 20s, average height with a slim build. Her light brown hair is usually tied up in a practical bun, and her sharp blue eyes reveal a keen mind. She dresses in practical travel clothing (earth tones).
* **Background:** Harland's only child, currently being groomed to take over the business. She is diligent and intelligent, often handling the logistics of the caravan.

## The Crew

### [[Valen Leafwhisper]]
**Role:** Scout and Guide
**Voice:** Mid, smooth.
* **Description:** Male, mid-30s (Half-Elf). Lean with wiry strength and graceful agility. He has long auburn hair tied back, striking green eyes, tanned skin, and pointed ears. Dressed in simple, muted tones to blend into nature.
* **Background:** A wood elf who knows the roads and trails like the back of his hand. He is reserved, preferring the company of nature over people.

### [[Maeve Greycloud]]
**Role:** Cook and Healer
**Voice:** High, old.
* **Description:** Female, mid-40s, average height and stout figure. Graying wavy hair tied in a bun, with soft brown eyes holding a warm gaze. Dressed in simple, clean clothing with a healing satchel always at her side.
* **Background:** A skilled cook and proficient healer (formerly a village nurse). She joined for a change of scenery. She is caring, considerate, and often the voice of reason.

### [[Doran Ironfist]]
**Role:** Blacksmith and Wagon Repair
**Voice:** Low, normal.
* **Description:** Male, early 50s (Dwarf). Short with an imposing muscular build. Fiery red hair and beard, intricately braided with metallic accents. Weathered face, bright eyes, and clothes often smudged with soot.
* **Background:** Takes care of the caravan's wagons and horses. He is a skilled blacksmith who enjoys telling stories about his craftsmanship.

### [[Lilliana Featherfoot]]
**Role:** Performer and Fortune Teller
**Voice:** Mid, normal.
* **Description:** Female, late 20s (Half-Elf). Slender with an aura of mystique. Long curly auburn hair, bright emerald eyes, and slightly pointed ears. Dressed in colorful flowing garments adorned with feathers and trinkets.
* **Background:** A bard who provides entertainment during the journey. She claims her fortune-telling skills were inherited from her elven mother.
