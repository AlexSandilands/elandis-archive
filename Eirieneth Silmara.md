- Major Character
- Elven Woman, ~200 years old
- Long black hair, green eyes, pale skin
- Mother of Eryndor
- Sister of [[Rhennaya Silmara]]
- Wife of [[Jarek]]
- Was disillusioned with the ways of the elves, thought them too ellusive and should be trying to integrate more with the peoples of Valtorra
- Spent more time with humans, wandering the villages and the forests, learning what she could
- Fell in love with Jarek, left her royal family behind, had a child
- Was taken by the Empire, currently imprisoned in Val Luminor
- Extremely kind, particularly to non elves, was fascinated by their cultures and how different they were to elves, more simple but more complex, variations of emotions, much shorter lives leading to heightened feelings almost like everything needs to be rushed
- This drew her to Jarek, love him and Eryndor more than anything
- When the war started, she tried to plead with her sister for the elves to stay and help, but was not successful. She turned her back on her people and stayed with her family, until she was taken
