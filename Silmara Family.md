Royal Bloodline of the Elves
"Blessed by Mother" (referring to the World Tree)
[[Ratha Silmara]] and [[Rhennaya Silmara]] were the last known rulers, parents of [[Ryo]]

Stayed behind to ensure all remaining elves were safely transported to Sumara
Were ambushed and hunted by agents of the Empire, fled, Rhennaya was pregnant unknown to most, she had the baby on the road, left in Lighthaven with the gnome [[Jek]], before leading agents away but ultimately being assassinated.

Killed by [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]] near [[Lighthaven]]

See [[Ryordan Silmara]] for more info on the family

The silmara family are the rulers and leaders of the Sumaran elves, however they do not control or govern the people. They settle disputes in the Confluence of the Seven when the council cannot internally come to an agreement, but they aren't supposed to do much more than that. They are highly loved and respected, they are also the only ones who can use the [[World Gates]] (portals between the realms, traverses the roots of the world tree magically, without needing to planeshift) along with opening the [[Vault of Memories]]
