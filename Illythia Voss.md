Illythia Voss is the current Meridian of [[Circle of the Weave|the Weave]] in [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]]. She assumed leadership of the Circle when [[Ayana Syndrosa]], her predecessor and former lover, departed during [[The Emerald Ceremony]].
