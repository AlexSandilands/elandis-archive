Quartermaster on the Albatross

Description:

Halfling - middle-aged with a weathered complexion. Long, straight black hair often tied back with a strip of leather. Has a collection of small, silver earrings climbing up one ear. Quite a thin physique, spends a lot of time reading and pouring over maps. Wears a small pair of round glasses.

Keen green eyes that miss nothing. A long scar runs across his left forearm, a souvenir from a close call with a pirate's blade.

Background:

Growing up as the son of a merchant in the bustling capital city of Val Luminor, Erun was well-versed in the art of negotiation and inventory management from a young age. His talents caught the attention of a passing captain who offered her a place aboard his ship. He quickly proved his worth and eventually made his way to the Albatross.

Maintains the supplies and resources of the ship, doing a lot of work in the background to ensure that everything continues to be prosperous for the crew.
