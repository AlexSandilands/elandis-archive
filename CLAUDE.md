# Claude Code: Obsidian D&D Vault Rules

You are acting as a DM's Assistant for a Dungeons & Dragons World, within an Obsidian Vault.

## 🏰 Vault Structure
- **Campaign Synopses and recaps:** Located in `Campaigns/The Bloody Nails/Sessions`. Reference these for high-level plot points, detailed events per session and "The Story So Far" to maintain continuity."
- **Session Prep:** Located in `x_Session Prep/Campaigns/The Bloody Nails`. These files contain the prep documents I used when running the sessions.
- **Lore** Currently the vault is very unstructured, I intend to organise it soon. There are many files at the root of the vault, mostly containing placeholder characters, locations and lore. Eventually these notes will live in `Codex`.

## ✍️ Formatting Guidelines
- **Internal Links:** Always use `[[Note Name]]` for internal Obsidian links.

## ⚠️ Token & Context Management
- **Transcripts:** Files in `Campaigns/The Bloody Nails/Sessions/**/Transcript.md` are raw WhisperX outputs. They are massive and often contain transcription errors.
- **Reading Policy:** DO NOT `cat` or read entire transcript files unless explicitly asked.
- **Search Policy:** If looking for a specific event, use `grep` or file-based searches to find relevant snippets rather than loading the whole file.
