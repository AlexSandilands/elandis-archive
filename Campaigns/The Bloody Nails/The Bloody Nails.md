## Introduction

The Bloody Nails are a group of adventurers who found themselves brought together by fate (and the oppressive hand of [[The Crimson Empire]]) on the continent of [[Valtorra]], in the year 2508.

They became brothers in arms while being transported on a slave ship destined for [[Skyreach]].
United by a common purpose, they travelled the lands of [[Valtorra]] doing what they can to impede the [[The Crimson Empire|Empire]] at any chance, while also pursuing some of their own goals.

The name of the group originates from [[Eryndor]], who tore a rusty nail from the hull of the slave ship with the idea to use it to free the group from their cells.
This nail was left in the skull of the [[Hand of The Empire]] at [[Skyreach]] after the party defeated him and destroyed the structure.

[[Eryndor]] later retrieved a new nail as a keepsake, and the symbol of a rusty nail is now used as an icon of the growing rebellion against the [[The Crimson Empire|Empire]].
