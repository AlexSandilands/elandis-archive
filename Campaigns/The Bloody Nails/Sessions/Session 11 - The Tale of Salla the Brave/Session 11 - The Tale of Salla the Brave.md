## Summary

The session began in the freezing chill of [[Faelon]], where the party prepared for their trek into the mountains. After reinforcing weapons and sharing temporary hit points under [[Vaeleran]]'s guidance, the group braved a blinding snowstorm to reach [[Tumbler's Crag]]. Through a combination of magical scouting and keen tracking by [[Ryo]], they discovered a hidden pit leading into a subterranean cavern. [[Dondar]] sensed overwhelming necromantic energy radiating from below, and the group descended into the dark, root-filled depths.

The investigation took a terrifying turn when [[Vaeleran]] picked up a silver locket found clutched in the hand of a small child's skeleton. The spirit of [[Salla the Brave]], the lost sister of [[Tara]], manifested with burning hatred. Her initial wail was devastating, instantly knocking Vaeleran and [[Ryo]] unconscious and damaging the rest of the party. The situation escalated as two shadow creatures emerged from the walls, threatening to overwhelm the scattered group. [[Kai]] utilised his Twilight Sanctuary to keep the party on their feet, while also noticing a spatial rift - similar to one seen in a previous goblin cave - that reacted volatilely to healing magic.

The battle was a desperate scramble for survival. [[Eryndor]] used telekinesis to repeatedly shove the vengeful ghost away from her victims, while [[Ryo]] and [[Vaeleran]] fought to recover from their near-death state. In a display of raw power, [[Dondar]] incinerated the ghost with a high-level lightning spell, vaporising her ectoplasmic form. [[Kai]] and Ryo finished off the remaining shadows with radiant magic and arrows. In the sudden silence that followed, the party discovered a woman cowering in the corner - [[Teresa]], the alchemist's wife, alive but terrified.

## Details

### Preparation and Travel

* **Weapon Crafting:** [[Kai]] and [[Dondar]] spent the evening fashioning a spear/staff hybrid using a warg fang obtained from a previous journey.
* **Morning Buffs:** The party awoke in [[Faelon]] to a heavy snowstorm. [[Vaeleran]] spent ten minutes motivating the group, granting everyone temporary hit points. [[Kai]] cast *Vigilant Blessing* on [[Vaeleran]] and *Eyes of the Night* to aid vision.
* **The Trek:** The group headed north toward [[Tumbler's Crag]]. The weather imposed disadvantage on perception, but [[Ryo]] used a *Rope of Climbing* to scale a tree and scout the rocky spine of the crags above the treeline.
* **Tracking:** [[Kai]] spotted a fresh broken branch, and [[Ryo]] identified humanoid tracks—small boots, spaced wide as if running—leading toward the rocks.
* **Disturbances:** [[Eryndor]] cast *Thunderclap* to provoke a reaction. Amidst the ringing silence, the group heard a faint, ghostly wail coming from the crags.

### The Cavern Discovery

* **The Pit:** The tracks led to a flat clearing containing a hidden hole, approximately 20 feet deep. [[Ryo]] nearly fell in but caught himself.
* **Magical Detection:** [[Dondar]] cast a ritual *Detect Magic*. He sensed a magical rope (the party's) but, more importantly, an overwhelming source of necromantic energy radiating from within the hole.
* **The Descent:** Using a crowbar and hammer to anchor a rope, the party descended into the cavern one by one. [[Vaeleran]] took point.
* **The Room:** The cavern was cold and moonlit by the hole above. [[Kai]] spotted a "crack in space" warping the air, similar to a phenomenon seen in an ancient goblin temple.
* **The Skeleton:** A small, non-ancient skeleton of a child lay on the floor, clutching a silver locket.

### The Encounter: Salla and the Shadows

* **The Trigger:** [[Vaeleran]] picked up the locket. Immediately, the ghost of [[Salla the Brave]] appeared, staring with hatred at the locket in his hand.
* **The Wail:** Sala unleashed a psychic shriek. Vaeleran and [[Ryo]] dropped to 0 HP instantly. The rest of the party took significant psychic damage.
* **Combat Mechanics:**
    * **Recovery:** [[Kai]] used *Twilight Sanctuary* to provide recurring temporary hit points, keeping the party conscious and mitigating fear effects.
    * **The Rift:** When Kai cast healing spells near the spatial rift, it pulsed with yellow energy, seemingly amplifying the magic.
    * **Tactics:** [[Eryndor]] used his telekinetic ability to repeatedly shove Salla away from the prone party members. Vaeleran attempted to return the locket to appease the spirit, but she remained hostile.
    * **Reinforcements:** Two Shadow creatures emerged from the cavern walls, attacking Eryndor and Ryo (who had been revived by Kai).
* **The Climax:**
    * [[Dondar]] upcast *Witch Bolt* (Lightning), vaporising Salla instantly.
    * Kai destroyed a Shadow with a high-level *Guiding Bolt*.
    * Ryo finished off the final Shadow with his bow.

### Aftermath

* With the undead defeated, the silence returned to the cave.
* The party heard breathing from the far side of the cavern and discovered [[Teresa]], the alchemist's wife, cowering against the rocks, alive but traumatised.
