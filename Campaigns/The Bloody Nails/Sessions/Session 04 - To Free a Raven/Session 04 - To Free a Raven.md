## Summary

Under the shroud of night, the party’s rescue mission for [[The Order of Ravens]] began with a treacherous descent into the filth-ridden sewers of [[Darmouth]]. Before they could even locate an entry point into the prison, the group was swarmed by a literal tide of vermin. Three massive Rat Swarms emerged from the darkness of the tunnels, forcing a desperate, close-quarters skirmish amidst the sludge. Once the pests were cleared, the group located a crumbling section of brickwork that led into a forgotten maintenance tunnel, allowing them to bypass the heavily guarded main entrances of the jail. Inside the sub-basement, the party orchestrated a masterful "Trojan Horse" deception: four members donned confiscated guard uniforms - with [[Ryo]] assuming the high-stakes disguise of a [[Hand of the Empire]] in a red-lined cloak - while the remaining two were bound in manacles to pose as prisoners.

The group successfully navigated the basement levels, creeping past sleeping guards and ascending into the main prison block. Their ruse was put to the ultimate test when they were intercepted by a patrol. Through a chilling display of intimidation and authority, the party convinced the sentries they were conducting an unscheduled prisoner transfer, gaining access to the cell of the rebel contact, [[Ella Sable]]. While retrieving her belongings and the stolen [[Skyreach]] intel from the storage room, the group was nearly compromised by a suspicious guard named Layla. Recognizing the deception, Layla attempted to sound the alarm bell, but the party moved with lethal speed, binding and gagging her before a single note could ring out.

With the clock ticking, [[Eryndor]] moved to brace the prison's main doors, buying the group precious seconds to retreat. They vanished back through the maintenance hole, meticulously covering their tracks with heavy crates before slipping away through the sewer tunnels. On the surface, they were met by [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] and [[Uric Graad|Uric]], who provided the party with healing potions and ensured their path out of the city was clear. As they prepared to depart, [[Uric Graad|Uric]] noticed a flicker of latent arcane talent in Eryndor’s eyes, taking a brief moment to teach the rogue the fundamentals of the *Mage Hand* cantrip - a tool for his burgeoning magical potential.

The session reached a spiritual crescendo at a local temple of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]], where the party sought a secret exit from the city. While within the holy sanctum, [[Kai]] was drawn to a statue of the Moon God [[Elaris Moonsong - Restricted]] and pulled into a breath-taking celestial vision. He found himself adrift in the stars, standing before not Elaris, but a female figure. The goddess claimed him as her own, urging him to embrace both the earthly and the divine; when the vision faded, a shimmering holy symbol remained etched upon [[Kai]]'s hand, manifesting whenever he assumed his starry form. Guided by Sister Arianna Brightstar, the party escaped [[Darmouth]] through a hidden subterranean passage within the temple, finally turning their attention toward the goblin-infested wilds to the north.

## Details

### Sewer Entry and Infiltration

* **The Swarm:** Upon entering the sewers, the party was immediately ambushed by three massive Rat Swarms. The party dispatched them quickly to prevent their presence from being detected by guards above.
* **The Entry:** The party located a hidden maintenance tunnel behind a section of crumbling brickwork (DC 13 Perception).
* **The Disguise:** In the sub-basement, the group recovered old uniforms.
    * [[Ryo]] wore the **Red Cloak** of a Hand of the Empire.
    * Three others dressed as standard guards.
    * Two members acted as prisoners in manacles.
* **The Descent:** The group successfully snuck past the sleeping guards in the basement levels.

### The Jailbreak

* **The Bluff:** The party encountered two guards on the main floor. Through a successful Intimidation check, they convinced the guards they were there for a "Prisoner Transfer."
* **The Rescue:** The guards opened the cell for [[Ella Sable]], who informed them that the stolen intel was hidden in her gear.
* **The Storage Room Skirmish:** While searching the storage room for Ella's equipment, they were confronted by Layla. 
    * Layla saw through the ruse and ran for the alarm bell.
    * The party neutralized her before she could alert the garrison, leaving her bound and gagged in the processing room.

### The Retreat

* [[Eryndor]] utilized his skills to brace the main entrance doors, preventing guards in the courtyard from entering immediately.
* The party retreated through the maintenance tunnel and moved crates over the hole to obscure the breach.
* They exited the city via the sewer canal and rendezvoused with [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] and [[Uric Graad]].

### Parting Gifts and Divine Visions

* **Logistics:** [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] provided [[Neros]] with **two Potions of Healing**. She agreed to manage the [[The Green Gryphons]] contract paperwork so the party could avoid returning to the city.
* **The Rogue’s Gift:** [[Uric Graad|Uric]] recognized [[Eryndor]]’s magical potential and taught him the *Mage Hand* spell, hinting at the rogue's connection to his father, [[Jarek]].
* **The Temple Vision:** At the temple of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]], [[Kai]] experienced a divine communion:
    * He saw a goddess, who bestowed upon him a holy symbol that glows when he enters his Starry Form.
* **Escape:** Sister Arianna Brightstar facilitated the party's exit through a secret tunnel behind the temple, leading them safely beyond the city walls.
