## Summary

With the revelation of [[Ryo]] and [[Eryndor]]'s shared bloodline still fresh, [[Ayana Syndrosa]] led the party cloaked through the [[Sumara, The Shining City#The Etherean Canton|Etheran Canton]] to the Locus Arcanum — the crystalline sphere that serves as the home of [[Illythia Voss]], Meridian of the [[Circle of the Weave]] and Ayana's closest companion of forty years. The reunion was volcanic. Illythia turned her back to the room, let a long silence hang, and then delivered a single line that carried the weight of four decades: *"Forty years — and not one message."* A slap across the face was followed, without pause, by Illythia pulling Ayana in and kissing her. *"I have been furious, terrified, proud and lonely every single day."* With the emotional accounting settled — at least for tonight — Illythia changed Ayana into proper Weave robes, heard a full briefing on [[Empress Morganna Eventide|The Empress]], [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow]], and [[The Red Priests]], and watched [[Ryo]] silently turn his hand and show her the [[Silmara Family|Silmara]] signet ring. Her composure cracked completely. *"Your parents were [[Ratha Silmara|Ratha]] and [[Rhennaya Silmara|Rhennaya]]. When were you born? How do you exist?"*

As moonrise came, the seven Nexus Citadels docked above the Royal Citadel and the [[Confluence of the Seven]] convened. [[Ayana Syndrosa]] threw back her hood before the full council and the chamber erupted. [[Kaelen Vayne]], scarred and dangerous, was immediately hostile — dismissing the claim, accusing Ayana of fabricating a pretender to justify her own return, and delivering a speech to the full council: *"We built this council to protect the elven people. We needed no king for a hundred years."* [[Ryo]] stepped forward, removed his hood and his bandana, and in silence held out his hand to show the ring. Justiciar Naivara Siannodel immediately reached for Article 7 of the Accords — no living witness, no documentation, no legal standing. [[Illythia Voss|Illythia]], dropping her pretence of neutrality, countered with Article 12: the [[Vault of Memories]] is a binding verification mechanism. Naivara went pale and could not refute it. When Warden Sylvaris — quiet until now, moss on her robes — broke her silence to report that she could feel the Faewild fraying through the roots, and the party laid out four planar rifts they had encountered across the continent, the balance shifted. The motion to open the Vault passed five to two. Kaelen stormed out across his light-bridge without a word.

The party descended into the Heartstone — the petrified remnant of a [[World Tree]] root beneath the Royal Citadel — and [[Ryo]] activated the same dais mechanism he had used at [[Ithilmara]]. As they spiralled down into a vast cylindrical void, the Vault read them: visions bloomed in the dark for all members of the party. Ryo witnesses two hooded elves handing a bundle to a frightened gnome — and then a figure in black armour, armour the party recognises, driving a sword through both of his parents at once. A dozen dwarves stand in grim resolve with something concealed beneath their clothes, their families watching in tears while a hooded dwarf storms out. A frail figure completes a ritual before a broken altar in a ruined castle — green energy pours into the sky as undead claw their way up through an endless dark forest. A black-haired woman sits in a stone prison cell, a river city visible below her window, scratching something into the wall. A solitary figure kneels before a great stone statue, something laid reverently at its base — and slowly begins to rise, blazing with celestial light so intense the other witnesses have to look away. At the bottom of the descent, a cipher chamber blocked the way — eight runic flagstones in a grid and a sentence in an ancient, unknowable script above the door. After Dondar took a lightning bolt for touching the wrong rune, the party decoded the inscription: *"The vault yields to no crown and no will / Name what you carry that the crown cannot grant / The vault has waited to hear the answer / What the crown cannot grant is blood."* Ryo stepped the runes in order — B, L, O, O, D — and the door cracked open. Beyond it, a second chamber waited, with four glowing pillars and a massive stone construct slumped dormant in the centre. The moment the party crossed the threshold, two enormous red eyes opened. Session ends as it rises.

## Details

### A Reunion Forty Years Overdue

* The party followed [[Ayana Syndrosa]] cloaked through the Etherean Canton to the Locus Arcanum, a crystalline sphere encircled by golden orrery rings.
* [[Illythia Voss]] dismissed her attendants and led them to her private quarters without a word.
* **Character Moment:** Illythia said *"Forty years — and not one message,"* slapped Ayana, then immediately pulled her into a kiss: *"I have been furious, terrified, proud and lonely every single day."* Ayana: *"Never again."* Illythia: *"Okay."*
* Ayana changed into Circle of the Weave robes; Illythia got down to business.
* The party briefed Illythia on [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow|Kharazoth]] the Crimson Shadow, the Red Priests, and the evidence of a second Shattering in the making.
* Illythia recognised [[Kharazoth the Crimson Shadow|Kharazoth]] from the original Chaos Wars — *"Long thought dead."* — and was visibly shaken.
* [[Ryo]] silently showed Illythia the Silmara signet ring; she looked at his face and went pale: *"Your parents were Ratha and Rhenaya. How do you exist?"*
* Ryo disclosed he woke 80 years ago in a forest with only the ring and no memories.
* Illythia confirmed the council landscape: [[Kaelen Vayne|Kaelen]] has Naivara and Elrohir; Silaqui, Fenian, and Sylvaris are persuadable; she must appear neutral.
* The Locus Arcanum rose as Illythia signalled the start of the Confluence.

### The Confluence of the Seven

* The seven Nexus Citadels docked above the Royal Citadel; bridges of pale light connected them to a central crystalline platform open to the night sky.
* The council chamber held a circular table with seven seats and one seat conspicuously empty — the royal throne.
* Ayana threw back her hood; [[Kaelen Vayne]] immediately stood and demanded an explanation.
* **Major Plot Twist:** [[Ryo]] stepped forward, removed his hood and bandana, and held out the signet ring in silence. Kaelen called it impossible — the ring was believed lost when the king and queen were murdered a century ago.
* Kaelen delivered his objection to the full council: *"We do not know what he has already traded away to the forces that helped him get here. The woman vouching for him abandoned this city forty years ago."*
* Justiciar Naivara cited Article 7 of the Accords — no witness, no documentation, no legal standing.
* [[Illythia Voss]] countered with Article 12: the Vault of Memories is a recognised verification mechanism within the Accords. Naivara went pale and conceded.
* Archivist Elrohir (Scroll) invoked historical precedent; [[Berberis]] challenged him — *"During the last Shattering, were the other realms unaffected?"* — and Elrohir admitted isolation offered no true safety.
* Elder Silaqui (Dawn) asked the party directly what kind of person Ryo is; Berberis answered plainly: *"He does what needs to be done. He's not intent on taking the throne. He keeps his word."*
* Warden Sylvaris broke her silence: *"The Faewild is already fraying. I feel it through the roots. Things are pressing against the mountains that don't belong here."*
* [[Eryndor]] produced the Arachnid Queen's amulet; Elrohir identified it immediately.
* The party recalled four planar rifts they had encountered: the goblin-infested temple, the Arachnid Queen's cave, the cave where [[Salla the Brave]] died, and the frost worm nest with the heat-emanating tear.
* Illythia called a vote to open the [[Vault of Memories]].
* **Vote Result:** Five hands raised (Illythia, Sylvaris, Fenian, Naivara, Silaqui). Two abstentions. Kaelen stormed out across his light-bridge.

### Into the Hearthstone

* The party descended from the Confluence platform directly through the Royal Citadel.
* **Character Moment:** [[Ryo]] passed portraits of his parents — [[Ratha Silmara|Ratha]] standing beside [[Rhennaya Silmara|Rhennaya]] seated on a chair, young and happy — for the first time in his life.
* The stonework changed as they entered the Hearthstone: warm to the touch, alive-feeling, not cold like normal rock.
* Ryo activated the portal dais with the signet ring; the mechanism engaged and a staircase descended in a slow spiral into darkness.
* [[Illythia Voss|Illythia]] told the party quietly before they went down: *"There is something of great importance in the depths. You'll know it when you find it. Come back."*
* The Vault sealed behind them.

### The Visions

* Descending the cylindrical void, the walls lit with ancient symbols and the Vault pressed into each party member's mind — reading their memories.
* **Vision I:** Two cloaked figures hand a bundled infant to a frightened old gnome in Lighthaven. Then: a kneeling elven man cradling a wounded elven woman — a figure in black armour drives a longsword through both at once. The party recognised the armour immediately.
* **Vision II:** An underground hall; a dozen dwarves in padded clothing stand in a grim line as a leader moves down it, clapping each on the shoulder. Families watch with tears. A hooded dwarf storms out and slams the door without looking back.
* **Vision III:** A frail figure in a ruined castle completing a ritual before a broken altar — green energy pours into the sky and undead claw their way up across an endless dark forest.
* **Vision IV:** A woman with long black hair in a stone prison cell. A river city visible below the barred window. She is scratching something into the wall. Alive. Present tense.
* **Vision V:** A figure cross-legged before a statue of a woman, head bowed, something placed on the ground before it. Slowly the figure begins to glow with celestial light, rising, blazing so brightly the rest of the party had to look away.
* The visions ceased as the party reached the bottom landing. A corridor led forward.

### The Cipher Chamber

* A labyrinth of tunnels pulled the party magnetically toward a sealed door.
* The cipher chamber: eight runic flagstones in a four-by-two grid, a firmly shut door beyond, and a multi-line sentence inscribed above in an ancient, unknowable cipher.
* **Moment:** [[Dondar]] touched a rune to cast Comprehend Languages; the Glyph of Warding detonated — 14 lightning damage, partially absorbed by his Arcane Ward.
* The party decoded the cipher collaboratively, with [[Kai]] creating a copy via Prestidigitation and [[Berberis]]'s prior knowledge of runic alphabets proving decisive.
* **Puzzle Answer:** *"The vault yields to no crown and no will / Name what you carry that the crown cannot grant / The vault has waited to hear the answer / What the crown cannot grant is blood."*
* [[Ryo]] stepped the flagstones in order spelling BLOOD; the door cracked open.

### The Stone Guardian

* Beyond the cipher door: a wide chamber with four tall pillars topped with glowing blue orbs.
* A massive stone construct stood dormant in the centre — no weapons, but fists larger than a torso.
* **Cliffhanger:** The moment the party crossed the chamber threshold, the construct's head lifted and two enormous red eyes ignited. It rose, took one slow step toward them, and session ended.
