## Summary

Seeking to fulfill their contract for the [[Green Gryphons]], the party launched a coordinated frontal assault on a hidden goblin lair within the wilds of [[Valtorra]]. The initial skirmish at the cave entrance was a decisive victory, as the group cut through a pack of hyenas and their goblin handlers with lethal efficiency. However, their momentum was nearly halted upon entering the cavern. Failing to detect a cleverly hidden tripwire, the entire party triggered a trapdoor mechanism, sliding down a steep, carved tunnel and plummeting into an ancient, sealed chamber dedicated to the god [[Eldrin Starweaver]].

The chamber served as a lethal "Trial of the Weave." As the party recovered from their fall, cracks in the floor began emitting a deadly green poison gas. While [[Neros]] utilized magic to confirm the toxicity of the air, the group was forced to decipher an arcane riddle involving the eight schools of magic. With the gas rising and time running out, the party successfully activated the pedestals in the correct sequence - beginning with Conjuration and ending with Transmutation - allowing the heavy stone doors to grind open just as the atmosphere became unbreathable.

Pushing deeper into the complex, the party infiltrated a side cavern used as a holding cell. They swiftly dispatched two goblin guards who were distracted by a game of cards, discovering a young human woman held in the cages. Though visibly battered and exhausted, the prisoner - who identified herself as "[[Vallania Miriel|Nia]]" - possessed a fierce blue-eyed gaze that suggested she was no ordinary traveller. Unable to proceed due to her injuries, [[Neros]] elected to remain behind to protect her and secure their path of retreat, while the rest of the party pressed on toward the heart of the temple.

The session reached a tense climax as the remaining members of the party reached the threshold of a massive, vaulted shrine. Hidden in the shadows of the corridor, they observed the flickering light of a bonfire and the echoes of a dark ritual within. Their stealthy approach was nearly compromised when a goblin sentry, hearing a stray noise from the party’s position, began to slowly approach their hiding spot. The group now stands on the precipice of a major confrontation, weapons drawn, awaiting the inevitable discovery.

## Details

### The Assault on the Lair

* The party arrived at the cave entrance and engaged a patrol consisting of a Worg, several Goblins, and a pack of hyenas.
* The combat was handled efficiently, allowing the party to secure the entrance without alerting the entire complex.
* Upon entering the first tunnel, the group failed a Perception check to notice a tripwire/pressure plate. 
* The floor gave way, acting as a slide that deposited all six members into a subterranean puzzle room.

### The Trial of the Weave

* **The Puzzle Room:** An octagonal chamber featuring a massive floor mosaic of [[Eldrin Starweaver]]'s symbol.
* **The Trap:** Shortly after the party entered, poison gas began filling the room from cracks in the floor.
* **The Solution:** The party had to interact with eight tablets representing the schools of magic in the order described by a wall riddle:
    1. **Conjuration** ("shapes the land")
    2. **Divination** ("peeks beyond")
    3. **Enchantment** ("forms a bond")
    4. **Abjuration** ("shield against the night")
    5. **Evocation** ("burns and smites")
    6. **Illusion** ("dreams and shadows")
    7. **Necromancy** ("life and death")
    8. **Transmutation** ("shifts, transforms")
* After several tense minutes of trial and error, the door was unlocked, and the gas was vented.

### Rescue in the Dark

* The party moved into a prisoner holding area and surprised two goblins playing cards.
* The guards were killed before they could raise an alarm.
* Inside the cage, they found a human woman who identified herself as [[Vallania Miriel|Nia]].
* **The Prisoner:** Though muddy and exhausted, she attempted to grab a weapon immediately upon release, showing a surprising amount of grit despite her state.
* **Tactical Split:** Because Nia was too weak to fight or move quickly, [[Neros]] stayed behind to protect her and ensure she wasn't recaptured by any stragglers.

### Breach of the Inner Sanctum

* The rest of the party crept toward the "Shrine Room," which appeared to be the main hub of the goblin tribe.
* **The Cliffhanger:** A goblin sentry heard a noise and is currently walking toward the party's hiding spot at the entrance of the shrine.
