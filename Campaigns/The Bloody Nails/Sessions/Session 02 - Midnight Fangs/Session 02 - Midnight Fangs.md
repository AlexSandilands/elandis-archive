## Summary

Following their escape from the shipwreck, the party began a four-day trek toward the port city of [[Darmouth]] under the protection of the [[Finegolds]] merchant caravan. The early days of the journey were marked by a growing sense of unease; as the group travelled the Coastal Road, the distant laughter of hyenas punctuated the nights, serving as a constant reminder of the dangers lurking in the [[Valtorra|Valtorran]] wilds. During their first evening together, [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] probed the party for information regarding their origins, though the group remained guarded about their recent incarceration by the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]].

On the second night, the atmosphere shifted from suspicion to wonder. [[Lilliana Featherfoot|Lilliana]], the caravan’s resident performer, shared an ancient legend regarding the "split" of the world. She told of a time before magic, defined by "machines of iron and steam," which was irrevocably changed when a divine Dragon Queen descended and infused the world with arcane power. This peace was further disturbed for [[Kai]], who experienced a vivid, celestial dream. He found himself drifting through the stars, reaching toward a pulsing black orb, only to wake with his arms shimmering with starlight and a physical energized crystal clutched in his hand - a clear sign of a burgeoning divine or arcane awakening.

The relative safety of the caravan was shattered on the third night. A coordinated strike by a goblin tribe caught the camp off-guard during the night watch. A pack of three Worgs, accompanied by hobgoblin warriors and hyenas, tore into the campsite. The ensuing battle was the most brutal the party had faced yet; the ferocity of the Worgs pushed the group to their absolute limits, leaving two party members unconscious and bleeding out in the dirt. Realizing the adventurers were being overwhelmed, the members of the [[Finegolds]] were forced to draw steel and join the fray. Through a desperate combined effort, the party and the merchants managed to slay the last of the beasts and their riders, leaving the camp bloodied, exhausted, and wary of what other horrors the road to [[Darmouth]] might hold.

## Details

### Whispers on the High Road

* The party officially began their tenure as guards for the [[Finegolds]], traveling alongside [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]], [[Harland Finegold|Harland]], and the rest of their caravan.
* **The Shapeshifter’s Mystery:** During the first night, [[Neros]] utilized his druidic abilities to shapeshift alongside the ship's dog they had rescued; however, the dog mysteriously wandered away into the darkness and vanished.
* **First Watch:** A Perception check during the night revealed the sound of hyenas tracking the caravan from a distance, though no attack materialized immediately.

### Lilliana’s Tale and the Star-Child

* **The Legend of Elandis:** [[Lilliana Featherfoot]] performed a narrative song detailing the arrival of magic. 
    * She described an ancient world of "science and iron" that was transformed when the Dragon Queen brought magic and dragons to the realm.
    * She mentioned a seven-century war that only ended when the "Seven Divines" (or perhaps Titans, according to [[Harland Finegold|Harland]]) banished the dragons.
* **Kai’s Vision:** [[Kai]] experienced a supernatural dream sequence where he ascended into the stars and witnessed a pulsing black orb.
* **The Manifestation:** Upon waking, [[Kai]] found his arms glowing with a starry luminescence and discovered he was holding an energized crystal, a new magical focus.

### The Midnight Ambush

* **Combat Engagement:** The camp was assaulted by a strike force consisting of 3 Worg, 3 Hobgoblins, and 3 Hyenas.
* **The Toll of Battle:** The fight was nearly catastrophic for the party. Two members were rendered unconscious by the Worgs' powerful bites and the hobgoblins' tactical strikes.
* **Merchant Intervention:** The NPCs of the [[Finegolds]] caravan entered the initiative to prevent a total party wipe, providing enough of a distraction for the healers to stabilize the fallen.
* **Outcome:** The party emerged victorious but severely depleted, ending the session amidst the bodies of the slain raiders as they tended to their wounds.
