## Summary

The session opens with high-stakes combat within the ancient, sunken shrine dedicated to [[Eldrin Starweaver]]. The party initiates a chaotic ambush against the goblin leader [[Grakthor]], his warg, and a goblin priestess. The battle is complicated by the shrine’s unstable magic; every spell cast triggers a Wild Magic surge, resulting in blue lightning, disorienting whispers, and gravity fluctuations. The chaos reaches a peak when [[Vaeleran]], mid-combat, is struck by a vision of a slender pale hand holding a purple orb; disoriented, he loses control and strikes his ally [[Eryndor]]. Meanwhile, [[Kai]] finds himself levitating upside down and laughing uncontrollably while [[Ryo]] focuses fire on the leader. The conflict ends decisively when Ryo lands a lethal arrow on Grakthor, and the party dispatches the remaining foes, though they are left unsettled by a mysterious "crack in space" discovered hovering near the shrine's ceiling.

Following the battle, the party loots the area, recovering significant magical artifacts and rescuing a human prisoner, [[Vallania Miriel|Nia]], from the adjacent caves. Their exploration leads them deeper into the lair, discovering a subterranean tent city. [[Kai]] flushes out a hiding goblin named [[Ellie the Goblin]] by setting her tent ablaze. Rather than executing her, the party captures her and escorts her - along with the rescued human prisoner - back to the surface to regroup with their allies.

The session concludes at the [[Finegolds]]' merchant camp. The party reunites [[Ellie the Goblin|Ellie]] with [[Lenny the Goblin|Lenny]], a goblin prisoner captured in the previous session. In a moment of mercy, the party releases the goblin pair on the condition they leave the region immediately. The group then settles in to identify their spoils, discovering a hoard of magical items including a raven-hilted dagger and a spellbook, before planning their next move toward [[Darmouth]] to turn in their contract.

## Details

### Showdown in the Shrine

* The party ambushed [[Grakthor]], a massive red-skinned hobgoblin with a grey mohawk, in the main shrine room.
* **Wild Magic Surge:** The shrine reacted to spellcasting with unpredictable magical effects:
    * Blue lightning and disorienting whispers plagued the casters.
    * [[Kai]] was levitated 10 feet into the air, turned upside down, and afflicted with uncontrollable laughter.
    * The goblin priestess was also levitated before being killed.
* **The Vision & Betrayal:** Upon failing a Wisdom save, [[Vaeleran]] was overcome by a sudden vision of a slender pale hand holding a crackling purple orb. In his confusion, he lost control of his actions and attacked [[Eryndor]], wounding his ally.
* **Combat Highlights:**
    * [[Ryo]] utilised *Hunter's Mark* and high-damage archery to eliminate the initial guards and land the killing blow on [[Grakthor]].
    * [[Neros]] (in Dire Wolf form) managed the frontline, keeping the warg occupied.
    * [[Eryndor]] struggled to find openings but eventually landed sneak attacks with his "rusty nail" tactic.
    * [[Dondar]] utilised *Magic Missile* to bypass high defences.
* **The Anomaly:** [[Kai]] observed a crack in space—resembling a broken mirror—hovering in the air near the ceiling, though the party could not identify its nature.

### Exploration and The Tent City

* The party recovered the human female prisoner, [[Vallania Miriel|Nia]], from the cages; she rode to safety on [[Neros]]'s back.
* Exploring a side tunnel, the group discovered a large cavern containing a bridge over a goblin tent city.
* [[Kai]] scouted the area and found a runty goblin hiding in a tent. To flush them out, he threw a torch and a barrel of alcohol onto the tent, setting it ablaze.
* The goblin, a female named [[Ellie the Goblin]], surrendered. [[Ryo]] and [[Eryndor]] used the severed ears of the dead goblins to intimidate her into compliance.

### Return to the Surface

* The party exited the caves, bypassing the pit trap that had caught them previously.
* They returned to the [[Finegolds]]' campsite near the river.
* **The Goblin Reunion:** [[Ellie the Goblin]] was reunited with [[Lenny the Goblin]] (the previous prisoner). The party honoured their deal, releasing both goblins with a warning to never return to the high road.
* **Next Steps:** [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] agreed to return to [[Darmouth]] to hand in the [[Green Gryphons]] contract on the party's behalf, allowing the group to bypass the city and head toward [[Camaar]].
