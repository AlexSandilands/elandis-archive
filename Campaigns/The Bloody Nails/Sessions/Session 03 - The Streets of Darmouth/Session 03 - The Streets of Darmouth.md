## Summary

The session opened in the grim aftermath of the goblin ambush. Still bloodied and reeling from the ferocity of the Worgs, the party made their final approach to the gates of [[Darmouth]]. Their entry was nearly barred by a suspicious guard named, who interrogated the group regarding their presence and origins. Though the tension was high, the group managed to bypass the gate and enter the city - a stark, military-style grid of cobblestone and colonial timber that felt far more like an imperial garrison than a home. While the rest of the group settled in, [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] briefly separated from the party under the guise of "dockside business," though her true intent was to coordinate with local insurgent contacts regarding a project known as [[Skyreach]].

Life in [[Darmouth]] proved to be a delicate balancing act of preparation and paranoia. The party visited the Sundew General Store to replace their tattered peasant clothes and purchase maps of the region, while [[Neros]] commissioned a custom quarterstaff from the gnomish Ironsong sisters at the Ironsong Forge and Lumberloft. However, the city’s safety felt illusory. While navigating the market, the party’s blood ran cold at the sight of a woman in a black cloak lined with crimson, the guard from the ill-fated prison ship, walking through the streets. Terrified of being recognized as escaped convicts, the group felt a sudden, urgent need to complete their business and depart the city as quickly as possible. Despite this fear, they visited the [[The Green Gryphons]] mercenary guild and accepted a high-stakes contract: 150 gold pieces each to track down and eliminate the goblin clan that has been terrorizing the trade routes.

The evening took a dark turn when the party witnessed a woman in a black cloak being chased and apprehended by city guards in a side alley. Choosing caution over heroics, the group did not intervene, only to later discover the significance of the event at The Seafarer's Rest. There, [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] revealed she knew the party were the missing ship-wrecked prisoners and confessed her own ties to a movement fighting against [[Valtorran Empire|The Empire]]. She introduced the party to [[Uric Graad]], a member of the [[The Order of Ravens|Order of Ravens]], who explained that [[Ella Sable]] had been captured while attempting to steal schematics for [[Skyreach]] from the Lawmaster's office.

The session concluded with a series of staggering revelations. To secure the party’s help in a daring jailbreak to rescue [[Ella Sable|Ella]] and the stolen info, [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] and [[Uric Graad|Uric]] appealed to their shared hatred of the Empire. In a quiet moment, [[Uric Graad|Uric]] pulled [[Eryndor]] aside, revealing that he knew the rogue's lineage and that Eryndor's father, [[Jarek]], was still alive and in [[Val Miriel]]. Bound by personal stakes and the promise of the rebellion, the party agreed to the rescue mission, setting their sights on the city’s dungeons before heading out to fulfil their goblin contract.

## Details

### The Gates of Darmouth

* The party arrived at the gates still recovering from the Worg attack; [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] urged them to keep their heads down.
* **The Interrogation:** A guard pulled [[Ryo]] and [[Dondar]] aside for questioning. Through a successful social check (or bribe), the party was allowed entry.
* **City Layout:** The party noted the "military design" of the city—straight lines and practical grids, lacking the organic growth of older elven or human cities.

### Preparations and Paranoia

* **The Sighting:** The party spotted the female guard from the prison ship, wearing a red-lined black cloak. This heightened the party's desire to leave the city quickly.
* **Shopping:**
    * Purchased new clothing and a regional map from the Sundew General Store.
    * [[Neros]] met Thora and Freya Ironsong, local blacksmith and carpenters, and commissioned a specialized quarterstaff.
* **The Mercenary Contract:** The group officially joined [[The Green Gryphons]] for the "Goblin Job."
    * **Reward:** 150 gold pieces per person, plus bonuses for "interesting" finds.

### The Shadow of the Raven

* **The Alley Incident:** The party observed [[Ella Sable]] being captured by three guards. They spotted [[Uric Graad]] watching from the rooftops but chose not to engage the guards.
* **The Tavern Meeting:** At The Seafarer's Rest, [[Gemma Finegold|Gemma]] confronted the party about the shipwreck. She admitted she had known they were prisoners since they met on the road.
* **Rebel Ties:** Gemma revealed her work with the rebellion. She explained that Ella and Uric Graad are part of a cell trying to uncover details about [[Skyreach]].
* **Jerek’s Fate:** Uric spoke privately with [[Eryndor]], revealing that his father, [[Jarek]], is alive in [[Val Miriel]].
* **The Next Move:** The party agreed to help the rebellion break Ella out of custody in exchange for the intel she gathered, which likely links to the Skyreach project mentioned in the Captain's letter.
