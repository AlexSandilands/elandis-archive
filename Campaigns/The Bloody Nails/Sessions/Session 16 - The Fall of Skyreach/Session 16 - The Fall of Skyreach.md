## Summary

The assault on [[Skyreach]] began in chaos and ended in cataclysm. Amidst the burning tents of the slave camp, the party managed to free a significant number of prisoners, including a shocking reunion for [[Kai]], who discovered his foster mother, [[Lirien]], among the enslaved. The initial skirmish escalated rapidly when a hostile Magister rained fire upon the party, prompting [[Dondar]] to retaliate with a devastating fireball that incinerated the barracks. With the alarm raised and time ticking, the party ascended the massive 400-foot pillars of the viaduct, navigating deadly traps and planting gunpowder kegs at strategic weak points to destabilise the structure.

After rigging the explosives and utilising a daring *Feather Fall* manoeuvre to descend, the party landed in a scene of slaughter. The extraction point had been compromised by a [[Hand of the Empire]], an elite warrior who had cut down the [[Mawbreakers|Mawbreaker]] captain, [[Barak Stormrider]]. The ensuing duel was brutal, pushing the party to their limits. In a moment that stunned the group, the Hand recognized [[Vaeleran]], calling him "Korvas" and referring to him as an "old mentor," before nearly killing him. [[Ryo]] and the party rallied to save their companion, allowing Vaeleran to deliver the killing blow.

In a grim act of defiance to mark the rebellion's presence, [[Eryndor]] hammered a rusty nail - kept from their original prison ship - into the dead Hand's forehead, leaving a brutal message for the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]]. As the party fled to [[The Albatross]] with the critically wounded [[Barak Stormrider|Barak]], the fuses burned down. [[Skyreach]] collapsed in a thunderous explosion of rubble and dust, blotting out the moon and dealing a crippling blow to the Empire's infrastructure. While the mission was a success, the cost was high - thirty sailors lost - and the party was left with heavy pockets (10,000 gold pieces) and heavier questions regarding [[Vaeleran]]'s secret past.

## Details

### The Infiltration and The Barracks

* The party pushed through the slave camp, where [[Dondar]] used a stolen key to begin freeing slaves.
* **Reunion:** [[Kai]] recognised one of the slaves as [[Lirien]], his foster mother. He cast *Cat's Grace* on her, entrusting her to free the others and head for the river while the party continued the mission.
* **The Magister:** The party was intercepted by guards, dogs, and a Magister. [[Eryndor]] and [[Ryo]] eliminated the beasts and guards, but the Magister cast *Fireball* into the tower Ryo was occupying.
* **Counter-Attack:** Dondar retaliated with his own *Fireball* into the barracks, killing the Magister and setting the entire building ablaze, causing chaos among the enemy ranks.
* **Loot:** [[Vaeleran]] and Kai looted the Magister’s charred corpse, recovering vital keys for the tower doors.

### The Ascent

* **The Door:** [[Vaeleran]] attempted to unlock the base of the massive pillar but triggered an acid trap, taking significant damage.
* **The Climb:** The party began the gruelling 400-foot climb up the spiral interior.
    * **First Landing:** Vaeleran triggered a dart trap but evaded it by clinging to the window ledge.
    * **Second Landing:** Halfway up, [[Ryo]] set the first cluster of gunpowder kegs with a slow-burning 22-minute fuse.
    * **Third Landing:** [[Eryndor]] spotted a deadly "Black Goo" trap on the ceiling (Nat 20 Investigation), allowing the party to navigate the room safely.

### The Bridge and The Drop

* Reaching the top, the party found the gatehouse unfinished and empty. They moved to the centre of the bridge.
* **Rigging the Bridge:** [[Dondar]] cast *Fly* on [[Eryndor]], who flew beneath the bridge to hammer in pitons and lash the final kegs to the structure's underside.
* **The Descent:** With fuses lit, the party ran back down the tower. To save time, they leaped from a lower balcony, using *Feather Fall* to drift safely to the ground just as the countdown neared zero.

### The Hand of the Empire

* Upon landing, the party found the extraction point littered with the corpses of sailors and guards. [[Barak Stormrider]] was found lying in a pool of blood, defeated.
* **The Boss:** A Crimson Cloak - a [[Hand of the Empire]] - confronted them.
* **The Reveal:** The Hand locked eyes with [[Vaeleran]], his composure breaking. He asked, *"Korvas, what game are you playing at?"* and referred to Vaeleran as his "old mentor," revealing Vaeleran's identity as the former [[General Korvas]].
* **The Fight:**
    * The Hand was a juggernaut, deflecting arrows and spells.
    * He knocked [[Kai]] unconscious and brought Vaeleran to death's door (2 failed death saves).
    * He offered the party a deal: Leave "Korvas" and walk away. The party refused.
* **Resolution:** [[Ryo]] used his last spell slot to heal Vaeleran. Back on his feet, Vaeleran knocked the Hand prone and executed him with a sword thrust through the chest.
* **The Message:** [[Eryndor]] hammered a rusty nail (kept from the prison ship) into the Hand's forehead as a symbol of the rebellion.

### Aftermath and Rewards

* **Barak's Survival:** [[Kai]] and the party utilised every resource available (Medicine checks, potions, spells) to barely stabilise [[Barak Stormrider]], who was clinically dead.
* **Destruction of Skyreach:** As they boarded [[The Albatross]], the charges detonated. The bridge collapsed completely, destroying the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]]'s strategic supply line.
* **Casualties:** [[Ari]] reported that roughly 50% of the sailors (30 men) died holding the line to let the slaves escape.
* **Medical Bay:** [[Illaris Sharin]], the Drow healer, tended to the wounded. [[Kai]] bonded with her over the moon goddess [[Selûne]], performing a ceremony to dedicate Illaris to the goddess for 24 hours.
* **Lirien:** [[Lirien]] and Kai caught up. She revealed rumors that the missing Elves were taken to the dungeons of [[Val Luminor]]. She also identified [[Ryo]]'s signet ring as the symbol of the [[Silmara Family]], the ruling class of the lost city [[Ithilmara]].
* **The Reward:** Ari presented the party with a chest from Barak containing **1,000 Platinum Pieces** (10,000 Gold).
* **Next Steps:** The party is sailing toward [[Camaar]] to drop off refugees before heading toward [[Val Noren]].
