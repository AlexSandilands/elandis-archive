## Summary

Deep beneath the crystalline spires of [[Sumara]], the party stood in the presence of an ancient legend. The session opened with the awakening of [[Praetor'Varanous]], a colossal silver dragon whose wingspan eclipsed the vast columns of the [[Vault of Memories]]. After a brief, playful scare, the dragon shifted into his humanoid form—the Silver Sage—revealing a frail-looking elder bearing a blackened, necrotic curse upon his chest. He led the heroes to the [[Pool of Insight]], a swirling basin of silvery liquid designed to test their connection to reality. To proceed, each member of the party had to peer into visions of their past—from [[Ryo]]’s childhood in the [[Darkwood]] to the bloody beginnings of their rebellion on a prisoner ship—and identify the deliberate falsities woven into the memories by the vault’s magic.

As the trials concluded, the Silver Sage shared a grim vision of the "Chaos Wars," revealing his own history with the "Crimson Shadow," [[Kharazoth]]. The dragon confirmed that [[Empress Morganna]] is likely a Lich, maintaining her youthful appearance through a hidden phylactery. However, the threat of Kharazoth is far more dire; the ancient red dragon has achieved a unique form of immortality by anchoring his soul "nowhere." To combat this, [[Praetor'Varanous]] revealed the existence of a [[Soulstone Anchor]], a powerful artifact found in the [[Vale of Eternal Night]] to the north. He explained that a weapon could be forged using this anchor that, if paired with a mortal sacrifice, could permanently sever Kharazoth’s tether to the material world.

The session took a deeply personal turn as the Pool of Insight revealed long-hidden truths to the individual heroes. [[Ryo]] discovered his true name is [[Riordan Silmara]], named after the legendary first of his line who bound the elven people to the sundered root of the [[World Tree]]. This revelation was bittersweet, as a vision confirmed his parents, [[Ratha]] and [[Rennaya]], were murdered by [[General Blackmarsh]] with the very blade the party now carries. Meanwhile, [[Eryndor]] learned his mother, [[Eirieneth]], is indeed being held captive by the Empress in a tower within [[Val Luminor]], and [[Dondar]] received a cryptic vision of his relative, [[Hvedra]], alive and moving through torchlit tunnels with other dwarves.

As the weight of these revelations settled, the party ascended via a magical platform back to the [[Royal Citadel]]. A projected hologram of [[Ayana Syndrosa]] informed them that the council would grant them a night of reprieve, leaving the entire citadel at their disposal. The heroes spent their first evening in their ancestral home sharing a drink in the kitchens and contemplating the monumental task ahead. With the path to the north clear and their resolve hardened by the truth, the party settled in for a long-awaited rest, emerging as more powerful champions of the rebellion.

## Details

### The Awakening of the Silver Sage
*   The party confronts the "statue" in the back of the vault, which reveals itself as the ancient silver dragon [[Praetor'Varanous]].
*   The dragon initially threatens to eat the "mice in his hole" before laughing and shifting into the form of an elven elder leaning on a gnarled staff.
*   He reveals the black mark on his chest is a "death curse" placed upon him by [[Kharazoth]] three centuries ago, binding him to the vault's magic to survive.

### The Pool of Insight: Trials of Memory
*   The party uses the [[Pool of Insight]] to verify their identities and ground their "threads of fate."
*   **The Falsities identified:**
    *   [[Ryo]] noticed his childhood treehouse in the [[Darkwood]] had windows in the vision; in reality, he never allowed light to escape.
    *   [[Eryndor]] saw too many houses in his childhood village in the [[Whispering Woods]]; his family lived in isolation.
    *   The vision of the prisoner ship featured [[Berberis]] and a woman dressed as a "Hand," though she was actually an "Eye" ([[Order of Ravens]]).
    *   A vision of [[Lia]] showed her fighting goblins; in truth, she was found caged and near death.
    *   The skeleton of [[Salla the Brave]] appeared as an adult, but she had died as a child.
    *   The "Rusty Nail" was remembered in [[General Blackmarsh]]’s chest, but was actually driven into his forehead.
    *   [[Harland Finegold]] was shown propped up, but the party actually found him buried in a field.
    *   [[Lady Arlen]]’s ritual was shown before a crowd, though only she had originally attended.

### Lore of the Crimson Shadow and the Lich Queen
*   [[Berberis]] catches a glimpse of the dragon's memory: [[Kharazoth]] and [[Praetor'Varanous]] clashing over a massive rift in space.
*   The Silver Sage theorizes that [[Empress Morganna]] is a Lich using a "glamour" to hide her withered form.
*   **The Soulstone Weapon:** [[Praetor'Varanous]] offers to forge a weapon using a [[Soulstone Anchor]] from the [[Vale of Eternal Night]].
*   **The Killing Blow:** To kill [[Kharazoth]], one must either leave the weapon inside him upon death or sacrifice a mortal soul bound to the artifact to ensure he never reforms.

### Personal Revelations
*   **The Silmara Lineage:** [[Ryo]] is told his birth name is [[Riordan Silmara]], named after the hero who saved the cosmos from horrors during the Chaos Wars.
*   **The Death of Ratha and Rennaya:** A vision confirms [[Ryo]]'s parents were impaled by [[General Blackmarsh]] using the dark blade now in the party's possession.
*   **Eryndor's Mother:** [[Eirieneth]] is confirmed to be alive, held in [[Val Luminor]] by the Empress.
*   **Dondar's Kin:** [[Hvedra]] is seen alive, traveling through tunnels with a group of dwarves.

### Conclusion and Level Up
*   The party ascends to the basement of the [[Royal Citadel]].
*   A message from [[Ayana Syndrosa]] and [[Illythia Voss]] grants them the run of the palace for the night.
*   The group shares an evening of reflection and a hundred-year-old bottle of wine in the royal kitchens.
*   **Reward:** The party reaches **Level 10**.
