## Summary

The session begins in the chaotic aftermath of the undead attack on [[Val Noren]] and the news of the King being kidnapped. The party races to the Keep, passing slain guards and confused civilians. Inside, they regroup with [[Barak Stormrider]] and meet [[Captain Erendir Thalos]], who reveals a devastating truth: the kidnappers were dressed in rebel armor to bypass defenses, and witnesses claim they were led by none other than [[General Korvas]]—the former ally known to the party as [[Vaeleran]].

Determined to retrieve [[King Marius Noren]], the party investigates a bloody trail leading away from the Keep. Their tracking skills lead them to a nondescript building against the eastern wall, revealing a hidden smuggler's tunnel beneath the city. Navigating the subterranean passage, they overcome a massive cave-in through a combination of brute badger strength and [[Dondar]]'s arcane fabrication, eventually emerging in a cellar within Imperial-controlled territory.

Upon surfacing, the group executes a chaotic ambush on two guards stationed outside. After a brutal interrogation where [[Ryo]] executes one prisoner to break the other's resolve, they learn the King has likely been taken to [[The Stoneheart]], the massive temple to the Earth God. [[Kai]] confirms this via aerial reconnaissance and magic. The session concludes with the party stealthily traversing the occupied city, establishing a forward operating base in a building overlooking the temple courtyard, poised to plan their rescue operation against overwhelming odds.

## Details

### The Keep and the Revelation

* The party arrives at the Keep of [[Val Noren]] amidst heavy snow and confusion; bodies of guards are found along the path and at the gates.
* [[Barak Stormrider|Barak]] introduces the party to [[Captain Erendir Thalos]], the commander of the city guard.
* Erendir reports that the kidnappers of the King, numbering around 20-30, were dressed in Rebel armour, allowing them to slip past defences.
* Witnesses identified the leader of the assault as [[General Korvas]] (formerly the party member [[Vaeleran]]), causing significant distress and anger among the party.
* Realising the reinforcements and the attack were a coordinated distraction, the party decides to track the kidnappers immediately.

### The Smuggler's Tunnel

* [[Eryndor]] and [[Ryo]] investigate the crime scene, finding a trail of blood and heavy boot prints leading away from the Keep.
* With the aid of [[Berberis]]'s owl and [[Kai]]'s guidance, the party tracks the group through the snowy streets to an abandoned building built against the eastern wall.
* Inside, they discover a hidden hatch leading to a well-maintained underground tunnel.
* Berberis summons a badger to carry a written message back to [[Barak Stormrider|Barak]], informing him of their location.
* The party encounters a massive cave-in blocking the path. Berberis (in Giant Badger form) and Ryo attempt to dig, but [[Dondar]] solves the problem by casting *Fabricate*, turning the debris into a perfectly reinforced stone archway.
* The tunnel passes under the river and leads to a cellar in a house on the Imperial-controlled south side of the city.

### Ambush in Imperial Territory

* [[Berberis]] scouts the house as a badger and detects two guards chatting outside the front door.
* The party orchestrates a distraction: Berberis bursts out the door as a wild badger, confusing the guards.
* [[Kai]] sneaks out and casts *Sleep*, successfully knocking both guards unconscious.
* The party drags the guards into the cellar, binds, and gags them for interrogation.

### Interrogation and Reconnaissance

* [[Kai]] attempts to interrogate one guard, but the soldier is defiant.
* [[Ryo]] executes the gagged guard to intimidate the survivor.
* The surviving guard reveals that while he doesn't know the exact location, he suspects the King was taken to [[The Stoneheart]] (Temple of [[Torgar Earthshaker]]) due to increased activity.
* The guard mentions the presence of the "[[Red Priests]]" near the General, describing them as unsettling and "hungry."
* Kai knocks the remaining guard unconscious and stages the room to look like a struggle, locking him in a bedroom.
* Kai summons a familiar (Raven) and uses *Wild Shape* (Mouse) to fly over the city. using *Locate Object* on the King's shirt.
* The spell detects the King inside The Stoneheart at ground level. Kai observes thousands of Imperial troops mobilising in the streets.

### Infiltration

* Realising a frontal assault is impossible, the party decides to move closer to the target.
* Utilising *Pass Without Trace*, the group stealthily navigates the occupied streets, avoiding patrols.
* They locate a multi-story building overlooking [[The Stoneheart]]'s courtyard to use as a hideout and observation point.
* The session ends with the party holed up near the temple, preparing to plan their next move.
