## World

A place between realms that shouldn't exist.
At the very edge of time and space.
Broken worlds and dying stars make their way here to pass from existence.
Whether by some divine punishment, catastrophic magical accidents or overly ambitious explorers, people sometimes find themselves here, trapped forever.

A society has developed on a large broken land mass that has formed from many different chunks of worlds colliding.

The central society forms around an ancient tower that has been here longer than anyone knows.

A flaming rune burns above the tower, visible to all, counting down at set intervals.
Within the tower, "The First Sinner" was sealed, but the seal degrades over time. The burning rune is a warning for when the seal will break.
