### Speak with Dead

>[!summary]+ Details
> You grant the semblance of life to a corpse of your choice within range, allowing it to answer questions you pose. The corpse must have a mouth, and this spell fails if the deceased creature was Undead when it died. The spell also fails if the corpse was the target of this spell within the past 10 days.
> Until the spell ends, you can ask the corpse up to five questions. The corpse knows only what it knew in life, including the languages it knew. Answers are usually brief, cryptic, or repetitive, and the corpse is under no compulsion to offer a truthful answer if you are antagonistic toward it or it recognizes you as an enemy. This spell doesn’t return the creature’s soul to its body, only its animating spirit. Thus, the corpse can’t learn new information, doesn’t comprehend anything that has happened since it died, and can’t speculate about future events.

### The Emberlight Vigil

>[!summary]+ Details
> - Everyone in town gathers in The Embermarket around the spire
> - Everyone wears grey cloaks with a yellow stripe down the back, hoods up, carrying candles
> - Elda Arkwright presides from a balcony halfway up and does a speech

#### The Speech

>[!cite]+ Read
> Welcome, friends, family, guests and all to the 547th Emberlight Vigil
>
> Tonight, once again at the conjunction of the planes, we give thanks to our ancient protector for constantly standing watch, unwavering, in our darkest hours.
>
> - Continues on, talking about positive and negative events during the past year, people who were lost, new people who have joined the community, and so on
>
> And now as always, to show our appreciation, we take our turn to stand vigil
>
> - Everyone bows their head and stands in silence for 1 minute, while the Spire is list, and bright golden energy climbs up the side of the structure, bursting out with radiant light when it reaches the top.
