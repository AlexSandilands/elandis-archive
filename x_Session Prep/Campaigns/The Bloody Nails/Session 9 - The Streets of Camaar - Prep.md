## The Veiled Cubs

>[!summary]+ The Setup
> - [[Tessa]] will approach from the front begging for coin
> - [[Lia]] will sneak up behind and attempt to pick pocket
> - [[Brom]] is the lookout - if he notices something bad he whistles.
> - If anyone gets caught [[Brom]] will attempt to kick out the legs of the people then flee
> - All 3 kids will scatter in separate directions, and meet back eventually at the [[Veiled Den]]

### Mechanics

> [!question] Trigger
> Bait makes a Performance Check
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **DC 10:** If they have a good disguise
> > - **DC 15:** Otherwise
> > - **Result:** If they get attention, 3 kids will take notice

> [!question] Trigger
> Party makes a Perception Check to notice the kids
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **DC 10:** Will notice 1 kid
> > - **DC 15:** Will notice 2 kids
> > - **DC 20:** Will notice all 3 kids

> [!question] Trigger
> Bait makes a Perception Check to see the kid approaching
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **DC 17:** Will notice

> [!question] Trigger
> [[Lia]] makes a Sleight of Hand Check vs Bait's Acrobatics or Sleight of Hand
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **Disadvantage:** If bait failed the previous Perception check
> > - **Result:** If bait wins they will either roll out of the way or grab the thief's arm

## The Veiled Den

[[Veiled Den]]

>[!summary]+ Details
> - 3 story building
> - Outside is rundown and non-descript
> - Inside is quite nice, kids plenty of room across the 3 stories
> - Enough food and supplies for everyone, they share the wealth fairly equally
> - Kids have living quarters on the top two floors, play areas, escape routes etc
> - [[Ellesar]] stays to the bottom floor as he can't walk well, his room is here along with the main dining area

### Entry
- Can enter either through hatches on the 2nd or 3rd floors
- There is a door that looks broken and run down around the back - it actually works perfectly well, just disguised
- If they come in the front door a kid will answer then disappear, hear an old voice "Lets them in"

### Meeting Elesar

>[!cite]+ Read
> "Well well this is exciting! It's been a long while since one of my cubs were caught, let alone leading people back to our den! You must be very a resourceful group"

>[!summary]+ Details
> - Missing Person, not returned from the forest near [[Faelon]]
> - Apprentice of the [[Cobblestone Alchemists|Cobblestone Apothecary]]

#### Faelon
- Small town about half a day's ride inland (west) of [[Camaar]]
- The inn is called the [[Redleaf Inn]] (forest nearby has a lot of beautiful red trees)
	- Dammond and Ania run the place
	- 2s per room per night

#### Folk Tale: Salla the Brave
- Decades ago, teenage girl would do all sorts of wild adventuring in the woods
- One day she never returned, body never found
- People say they have seen her spirit in the woods throughout the years, some don't believe
- Her sister ([[Tarra]]) is still alive, old woman now living in the village
- [[Tarra]] will mention that [[Salla]] always used to wear a locket around her neck with a drawing of their parents who died young - [[Salla]] loved it more than anything

### Clear the Coastal Cave

- Some "merchants" (smugglers) want help clearing a coastal cave around half a day north of the city in a hidden cove
- Some of their crew didn't return from storing some supplies and when investigating heard terrifying sounds
- Clear the cave of danger
- They are docked on a boat called The Kraken
	- Captain's name is Erin Nayla
	- Half Orc, very serious, scar across one eye that is white
	- Person who came back from the cave was Olly - topman of the ship - skittery fast speaking nervous fellow
- **Reward:** 150g per person
