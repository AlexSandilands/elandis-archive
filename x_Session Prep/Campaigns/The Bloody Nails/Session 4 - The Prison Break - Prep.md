## Combat: Sewer Entry

> [!Danger] Combat

## Entering The Prison

> [!question] Trigger
> Investigation Check to find an entry point
> > [!info]- Options
> > - **DC 5:** Ladder to the Hatch
> > - **DC 10:** Waste Chute
> > - **DC 13:** Forgotten Maintenance Tunnel

### Option 1: The Ladder
- Located at the far end of a sewer tunnel.
- **Lock:** DC 20 Thieves Tools to pick the hatch.
- **Destination:** Opens into the **Prison Courtyard**.

### Option 2: The Waste Chute
- A sloping drain pipe emptying into the canal. Requires crouching.
- **Climb:** DC 13 Group Athletics/Acrobatics to get up the slope.
	- *Failure:* Slide back into canal, disadvantage on the Con save.
- **Sickness:** DC 13 Constitution Save.
	- *Failure:* Disadvantage on attacks/checks for 20 minutes.
- **Vertical Shaft:** DC 13 Athletics to lift a character to the top.
- **Lock:** DC 8 Thieves Tools (Simple padlock under wooden cover).
- **Break:** DC 5 Athletics to break the wood.
- **Destination:** Opens into the **Basement Waste Room**.

### Option 3: Forgotten Maintenance Tunnel
- Section of different/crumbling bricks.
- **Break In:** DC 10 Athletics.
- **Check Room:** DC 13 Perception to look through a hole and confirm the room is empty.
- **Break Out:** DC 10 Athletics to break the bricks at the other end.
- **Destination:** Opens into the **Sub-Basement**.

>[!summary]+ Description

> **Layout:** 10ft high ceiling, old brick walls.
> **Contents:** Crates of rope, batons, whips, manacles, bed rolls, prisoner clothes.
> **Exits:** Stairs lead up to the Basement.
