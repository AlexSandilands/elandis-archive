### Ayana (Syndrosa)

> [!question] Trigger
> Ayana will catch the party as they are leaving for a secretive conversation

- Some small talk regarding their next mission
- Asks to see the sketching again
- The world currently resides on a knifes edge. More than you know. One wrong move and it shall fall to darkness 
	- And because of this, despite what you've done for this city, I cannot fully trust you yet, so there may things in what I'm about to tell you that are missing or incomplete. I know it is unfair considering what I just said, but I need to trust me
- What do you know of [[The Shattering]]?
	- There was a time before magic
	- 2500 years ago, barriers between the realms of existence were broken
	- Creatures, monsters, and magic entered the world
	- 500 years of the Chaos wars, led by cunning evil creatures who sought to break the shattered barrier permanently, unleashing a darkness unknown to this world. They are known to myth as The Chromatic Dragons. Children speak of them in their stories, but they were all too real, and even more terrifying than the children imagine.
	- Civilisation fell, great cities were sundered, until the remaining peoples of this world formed a final united front, led by seven champions blessed by the gods themselves, and closed shut the shattered portal.

*pause*

- That was two millennia ago - but where does that leave us know? And why do I tell you this
	- You may see an empire rising, the oppression of your fellow kinsman
	- We see seeds of chaos that are all too familiar
		- Civilisation falling overnight
		- Citizens turning against their own
		- Monsters leaving their dens and attacking people on the roads
	- We believe the agents of chaos are at work again. Despite what we thought, we now believe they must not have all been destroyed or banished at the end of the chaos war
- Why am I telling you this?
	- They are cunning beyond measure. They could be anyone
	- The reason I am placing faith in you, is because you showed me this - the first true bit of evidence we have seen.

*pause*

- I ask you this. Earn yourself my trust. In your travels if you find anything that point to more information like this, bring it to me. Find these red priests.
	- But be warned, if what we fear is true, you cannot let it be known that anyone is aware of their presence as it may only lead to accelerating their plans
