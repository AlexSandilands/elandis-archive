## Kai's Purification Ritual

>[!summary]+ Details
> - Light will possess Helda, and she will finish creation of the shield at Midnight when the moon in shining

### Stage 1: The Fever (Shadowflame)
- **Mechanic:** Constitution Save DC 13.
- **Visual:** The armor heats up. Kai feels the heat *inside* his veins.
- **Effect:** On fail, take 3d6 Fire damage and hallucinate that his friends are burning.

### Stage 2: The Bargain (The Whisper)
- **Mechanic:** Charisma Save DC 15.
- **Narrative:** The armor speaks with the voice of the General/Shar.
	- *"You killed me, but you have my hunger. Let me in, and you will never fear death again. Tell me, why do you fight..."*
- **Choice:** Kai must speak a truth about *why* he fights. If the truth is selfish, the DC increases to 20.
- **Success:** Clue to the fight - Selune's voice - "that's right, compassion and redemption is the only path forward"
- **Fail:** "that's right the shadows give you strength"

### Stage 3: Blurred Boundaries (The Puzzle)
- **Mechanic:** Wisdom Save DC 17.
- **The Scene:** The Temple dissolves. Kai is back in the Stoneheart, but he is the General on the throne. The party is kneeling before him.
- **Action:** He must physically interact with the "illusion" to break it (e.g., attack the throne, heal a friend who isn't there). Passive checks fail.

### Stage 4: The Shadow Within (Combat)
- **Enemy:** **Kai's Shadow**.
- **Mechanic:**
	- The Shadow has Kai's stats but is vulnerable to **Radiant** damage.
	- Immune to all damage apart from Radiant and Healing
	- **Mirror Move:** Whatever damage Kai deals to the Shadow, he takes half back as Psychic damage *unless* he uses the Chain Whip (non-lethal/binding) or healing magic to "cure" the shadow.
	- **Victory:** The Shadow is not killed, but accepted/absorbed.
