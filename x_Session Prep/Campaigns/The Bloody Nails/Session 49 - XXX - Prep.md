### Meeting the dragon

- Ancient Silver Dragon
	- Need a name!
	- She nearly died during the Chaos Wars, was bound to the Heart Stone, only lives while she remains inside
	- Has vast knowledge due to time spent

This scene and session needs to be used to give agency back to the players.
The dragon should question the group as to their purpose, what do they want, how are they going to achieve it, what can she do to help. What are they going to do about the political situation in Sumara? What to do with Kaelen?

How to kill Kharazoth

- He is likely immortal, the only way he survived was likely with dark magic, similar to what a lich would do binding the soul to an object, except he needed no phylactery. Therefore he can always return to form.
- **Big reveal**: The only way to kill him is if she helps them to craft an item that must be driven into him before being killed. A mortal person's soul will be bound to the item, and Kharazoths soul will be bound to the mortal. Then the only way to permanently kill Kharazoth is for that mortal to die before his body reforms.
