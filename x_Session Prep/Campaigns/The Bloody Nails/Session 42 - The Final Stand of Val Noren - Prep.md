## Part 1: Securing the Flank (The Heist)

>[!summary]+ Context
> The party must choose how to enable the flanking maneuver. They cannot do both effectively in the time allotted.
> - **Objective:** Allow [[Barak Stormrider|Barak]] and [[Ari]] to move the fleet into position without being spotted.

### Option A: Seizing the River Gate

**Objective:** Open the heavy iron portcullis allowing boats to flow from the inner city river to the flanking position.
**Obstacles:** The mechanism is ancient, rusted, and guarded by a skeleton crew of engineers and 1 "Hand of the Empire" overseer.

> [!info]+ The Skill Challenge (Target: 5 Successes before 3 Failures)
> **The Environment:** The gatehouse is damp, loud with machinery, and smells of oil.
> 
> * **Stealth (Dexterity):** To approach the winch room without alerting the overseer.
> * **Investigation (Intelligence):** To figure out which counterweights release the lock (it's trapped).
> * **Athletics (Strength):** The gears are rusted shut. Requires a DC 20 check to crank manually if the mechanism fails.
> * **Thieves Tools (Dexterity):** To disable the alarm bells rigged to the winch.
> 
> **Failure Consequence:** The alarm is raised. The "Hand" attempts to destroy the winch controls, forcing the party to lift the gate manually (Exhaustion) while under fire.

### Option B: Seizing the Docks

**Objective:** Commandeer enough small merchant scows and fishing boats to form the bridge without alerting the patrols on the riverbanks.
**Obstacles:** Civilian dockworkers (who might panic), Imperial patrols, and sabotage risks.

> [!info]+ Roll For the Mawbreakers
> - D20 for passing out the gates
> - D20 for securing the boats

> [!info]+ The Skill Challenge (Target: 5 Successes before 3 Failures)
> **The Environment:** Foggy riverbank, slippery wood, lantern light cutting through the mist.
> 
> * **Persuasion/Intimidation (Charisma):** Convincing the dockmaster to lend the boats quietly without screaming for guards.
> * **Sleight of Hand (Dexterity):** Cutting mooring lines simultaneously to drift boats out silently.
> * **Perception (Wisdom):** Timing the patrols of the riverboats.
> * **Deception (Charisma):** If spotted, convincing a guard this is an authorized cargo movement.
> 
> **Failure Consequence:** A patrol boat spots them. They must silence the boat (Combat) before it fires a signal flare. If the flare goes up, the Parley will start with the enemy already alerted to the flank.

## Part 2: The Pontoon Bridge

>[!summary]+ Details
> Once the assets are secured, [[Barak Stormrider|Barak]] and [[Ari]] need help coordinating the rapid construction.
> - **River Width:** 100 ft.
> - **Boats Needed:** Approx 15-20 small boats lashed side-by-side.
> - **Capacity:** Needs to support 1,500 soldiers crossing in heavy armor.

### The Construction Challenge
*Group Check (DC 15). If the group fails more than half, the bridge is unstable (Dex save required to cross).*

1.  **Stealth (The Approach):** Keeping the boats low in the water, using fog cover. 
2.  **Survival/Nature (Currents):** The river is fast; the boats need to be anchored at an angle to fight the drag.
3.  **Sleight of Hand/Athletics (Lashing):** Tying the planks and boats together rapidly.
    * *Flavor:* "Ari is shouting whispers, throwing ropes like snakes across the gaps."

### Tear Down?
- **Strategic Choice:** Do they leave it for a retreat, or destroy it to prevent the enemy from using it to counter-attack the city?

## Part 3: The Parley

**Location:** The main stone bridge. Sunset.
**Attendees:** [[King Marius Noren]], [[Ayana Syndrosa|Ayana Wynne]], The Party (optional).
**Opponents:** The Imperial Legion (Massed), Led by **Hand of the Empire Talerius** (The Spokesperson).

### The Standoff

> [!cite]+ Talerius's Stance
> "You speak of peace, pretender? The Empress does not bargain with ash. Surrender the Keep, and we will grant you a swift death. Refuse, and we will burn this city until not even history remembers its name."

- **The 5 Hands:** They stand at the front. They are heavily armored, faces hidden, radiating fanaticism. They will **never** surrender.
- **The Regulars:** Behind them, the soldiers look tired. Their armor is dirty. They are wavering.

### The Turn (The Flank)
- **Trigger:** When negotiations fail (or Marius gives the signal), a horn blows from the *East* (behind the Imperial army).
- **Effect:** The Loyalist army pours across the pontoon.
	- **Imperial Morale:** The regular soldiers realize they are surrounded.
	- **The Hands' Reaction:** They do not look back. Valerius draws his blade. "For the Empress! Death before dishonor!"
	- **Resolution:** The Hands kill their own wavering sergeants to force a fight. They will suicide-bomb (Magical overloads) or fight to the death. They will not be taken prisoner.

## Part 5: Kai's Purification Ritual

>[!summary]+ Details
> - **Location:** St. Eveline's Temple (Desecrated/Purifying).
> - Light will possess Helda, and she will finish creation of the shield at Midnight when the moon in shining

### Stage 1: The Fever (Shadowflame)
- **Mechanic:** Constitution Save DC 13.
- **Visual:** The armor heats up. Kai feels the heat *inside* his veins.
- **Effect:** On fail, take 3d6 Fire damage and hallucinate that his friends are burning.

### Stage 2: The Bargain (The Whisper)
- **Mechanic:** Charisma Save DC 15.
- **Narrative:** The armor speaks with the voice of the General/Shar.
	- *"You killed me, but you have my hunger. Let me in, and you will never fear death again. Tell me, why do you fight..."*
- **Choice:** Kai must speak a truth about *why* he fights. If the truth is selfish, the DC increases to 20.

### Stage 3: Blurred Boundaries (The Puzzle)
- **Mechanic:** Wisdom Save DC 17.
- **The Scene:** The Temple dissolves. Kai is back in the Stoneheart, but he is the General on the throne. The party is kneeling before him.
- **Action:** He must physically interact with the "illusion" to break it (e.g., attack the throne, heal a friend who isn't there). Passive checks fail.

### Stage 4: The Shadow Within (Combat)
- **Enemy:** **Kai's Shadow**.
- **Mechanic:**
	- The Shadow has Kai's stats but is vulnerable to **Radiant** damage.
	- Immune to all damage apart from Radiant and Healing
	- **Mirror Move:** Whatever damage Kai deals to the Shadow, he takes half back as Psychic damage *unless* he uses the Chain Whip (non-lethal/binding) or healing magic to "cure" the shadow.
	- **Victory:** The Shadow is not killed, but accepted/absorbed.
