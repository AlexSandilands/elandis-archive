## Empire Ship Encounter

A ship is seen on the horizen, flying the Red flag of the Empire

- This is The Devil's Wish
- The Captain is Yarna Coldfist
	- Serious woman with dark eyeshadow, Captains hat with long black hair in a pony tail going down her back, Officers pauldrons
- She will get two men (Gale and Lorath) to search the supplies of the Albatross
- They will eventually find the stack of gunpowder barrels
	- DC20 to convince them it is innocent
	- On a fail they will pretend to go along with it, but will report it to Yarna
	- Yarna will tell the crew to stand down and be escorted to Darmouth for processing and investigation
- If everything starts going wrong Yarna will signal to her crew to arm, giving one last chance
	- If no stand down then she will run back to her ship and call for oars and open fire

If ship is boarded, completing a strike team objective will give +3 to the roll
  
Strike Team Objectives:
	- Kill Commander
	- Destroy Cannon Teams
	- Kill Archery Squad
