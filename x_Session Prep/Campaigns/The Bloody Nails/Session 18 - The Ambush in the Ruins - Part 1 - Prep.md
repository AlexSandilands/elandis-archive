## Farhaven

>[!summary]+ Details
> - The group arrives at [[Farhaven]] about Midday
> - The city is built on a wide peninsular that juts out into the gulf on your East
> - No walls, but the density of the buildings form somewhat of a boarder
> - The main road carries on north past the city, and branches at a T intersection where the off road travels into the city proper
> - On the other side of the road, towards the mainland are crops and pastures, dotted here and there with farm houses

### Lia
- Someone notices her pull out a small bronze octagonal box
- Begins lock picking it
- After a few moments it clicks open and she looks into it for while, before going back to staring out the wagon

### Painted Nail on a Wall

- As you get closer to the city, someone notices a farmhouse close to the road that has a large black symbol painted on it
- This is a Nail

### Hanging at the Junction

>[!summary]+ Details
> - At the junction to [[Farhaven]], there are 4 guards in the middle of hoisting a woman up by the neck, to join 2 other men already hanging

- [[Lia]] hisses, becomes very tense, fingers claws into the arm of someone sitting beside her

- The first man has a sign that reads "Rabble Rousing"
- Second sign reads "Conspiring for treason"
- The sign on the woman reads "Attacking Imperial Soldiers"  

- Some time after another woman notices this (Aradale) and screams
	- "No, nooo Juliana!!"
	- "She did nothing, nothing!!!"

- She begins lunging for one of the guards, who promptly pushes her back, smashes her in the face with the hilt of his sword
- Guard says "Looks like we've got another one who wants to join the decorations lads!"
- [[Lia]] says "We have to help her!!!"

### How to get to the Ruins

- North East of [[Farhaven]] in the deep forest
- Can carry on the road for a few miles past [[Farhaven]] until reaching an obelisk of stone
	- (This is a remnant of the old world, used for magical light signals with ships in the gulf)

- Once reaching the obelisk there is a faint path that heads inland towards the forest
	- This path carries on into the forest. It's not a great path but just beaten enough for a cart to get through.
	- Eventually the path terminates in the ruins and there is no clear way forward

- Alternatively, can head straight for the forest across the fields
- DC 15 survival check once inside the forest to not get lost heading into the center towards the ruins
	- Requires 2 consecutive passes. Fails lose an hour
	- 3 fails they are lost, encounter with Displacer Beasts

## The Farhaven Forest

>[!summary]+ Details
> - This is a dense forest to the north east of [[Farhaven]]
> - There are ruins of an ancient city crumbling between the trees
> - There are many monsters in here

### Arriving

- You start feeling like you getting closer to a confluence of the ruins
- DC 13 Perception - There's almost a circular pattern, and you can see these angles getting smaller and smaller
- Darkened stone, cracked, moss and vines
- Hear the odd growl of a creature or shriek of a bird

- [[Harland Finegold]] has been killed and buried near a wall
