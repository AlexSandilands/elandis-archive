### Chase:

> [!info]+ Action
> - If followed immediately by also jumping out of the window, DC 13 perception check (remember it is dark) to see which way the assassin went
> - Assassin is moving 60ft per round, and rolls a stealth check
> - Can dash an additional 2 times (since it has 2 constitution), but must roll DC10 con save to not be exhausted
> - Each round the chaser must make a perception check (against the stealth roll) to see if they see the direction the assassin went

## Skyreach Planning

Barak will lead them down to a basement, which used to be some kind of supply room but has been converted into a war room.

- There are several small structures that look like models of a bridge, many are broken and cracked
- Large table in the center of the room, with papers all over it.
- Gromdal is down here, going over some notes

Barak's plan:

- Primary objective, destroy Skyreach
- Secondary objective, save as many slaves as possible
	- The Albatross will go ahead to prepare, a fleet of 5 additional longships with skeleton crews will follow, spaced out to avoid attention
	- The albatross can hold 100 passengers, the other can hold a bit more
	- Approach from the sea, sail up the Aeolian river as far as possible
- 3 sections of Skyreach will cause massive vulnerability if damaged at the same time
- Gunpowder kegs will be used at each location. The amount of damage that is done will effect how fast the structure breaks

> - Want to hear from the adventurers how they would approach it

## The Maw

- Players can choose to stay on top deck or go below.
- If on top they will view what happens, but make a DC10 athletics check to hold on (advantage if they have braced)

- If fail they will start sliding. A crew member can make a DC10 acrobatics check
- If fail they will fall over the side and start spinning around the maw
- The ship will come around twice and throw a rope, athletics DC13 to grab and hold on
- If fail twice the person sucked into the center of the maw deep underwater
