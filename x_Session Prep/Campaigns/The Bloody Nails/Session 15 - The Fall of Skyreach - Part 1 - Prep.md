### Smokepowder Arrows

Roll either once with advantage to get one arrow, or twice normally to get two arrows

- Less than DC10 = Roll a D20, less than 10 it explodes on you with 3d6
- DC10 1d6 fire & force
- DC15 2d6 fire & force
- DC20 4d6 fire & force

### Dynamite Arrow
