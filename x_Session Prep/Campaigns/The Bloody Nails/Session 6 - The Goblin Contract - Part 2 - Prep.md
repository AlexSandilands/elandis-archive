### 1. The Trap Room (Entrance)

**Transition:** The party enters the cave.
- **DC 15 Perception:** Notice a trip wire.
- **DC 25 Investigation:** Realize the floor will give way with 6 people on it.

**The Fall:** If triggered, they fall down a carved tunnel and land in the **Puzzle Room**.

### 2. The Puzzle Room

>[!summary]+ Description
> A grand, circular chamber with eight walls. The stone is eroded but strong.
> **Floor:** A massive, ornate depiction of [[Mystra]]'s symbol: seven blue-white stars with red mist flowing from the center. Large cracks run through the floor.
> **The Door:** Built into one wall. Ornate octagonal symbol carved into it. Each vertex has a dull blue stone circle.
> **The Mechanism:** A stone bowl with a **magical blue flame** on a pedestal in front of the door. A handprint is carved into a block on the pedestal.
> **The Walls:** Each of the 8 walls has a bowl/pedestal and a tablet with an Arcane Symbol (Abjuration, Conjuration, Divination, Enchantment, Evocation, Illusion, Necromancy, Transmutation).

**The Trap (Time Limit):**
- When they leave the center, **Poison Gas** begins emitting from cracks in the floor (placed by the Goblin Priest).
- **Time:** 10 Minutes before the room fills (Unconscious -> Captured).

**The Riddle:**
> *To honor Her weave so grand,*
> *First seek the school that shapes the land.*
> *Then find the art that peeks beyond,*
> *Followed by charms that form a bond.*
> *The next, a shield, against the night,*
> *Then the force that burns and smites.*
> *With dreams and shadows, full of delights,*
> *Then life and death in endless fights.*
> *Finally the art that shifts, transforms, and remakes,*
> *Will complete the sequence, and the door awakes.*

**The Solution:**
1. **Conjuration** (shapes the land)
2. **Divination** (peeks beyond)
3. **Enchantment** (forms a bond)
4. **Abjuration** (shield against the night)
5. **Evocation** (burns and smites)
6. **Illusion** (dreams and shadows)
7. **Necromancy** (life and death)
8. **Transmutation** (shifts, transforms)

### 6. The Shrine (Boss Room)



>[!summary]+ Description
> A vast octagonal room.
> **Old:** Ruined pieces of a massive [[Mystra]] statue scattered about.
> **New:** A grotesque shrine on a raised platform. Made of clay and metal, featuring **Three Serpentine Heads** (one under construction).
> **The Anomaly:** A **Medium Riftshard** floats above the shrine. It looks like a crack in a mirror; space is warped.

**The Bosses:**
- **Captain [[Grakthor]]:**
	- Half Plate, Greatsword.
	- **Cloak of Protection +1**.
	- Scarred eye, mohawk mane.
- **Priestess [[Lyliss]]:**
	- Black robes, grey hair.
	- **Dust of Disappearance** (2 uses).
	- **Spells:** *Chromatic Orb, Disguise Self, Tasha's Hideous Laughter, Hold Person, Misty Step*.

> [!Danger] Combat
> - Grakthor
> - Lyliss
> - 1x Worg
> - 2x Goblin

> [!Danger] Combat Note
> Remember **Vaeleran's** Wisdom Saving Throw if triggered by specific effects or the Priestess.
