## Nelfandriss' Lair

### Chase Sequence

- Spiders start 80 feet away
- Players need to run 250 feet to get to the entrance
	- This is 5 rounds if they dash each round
	- If they only dash, the spiders will gain 20 feet
	- If they succeed an escape ability  spiders do not gain distance  

- When within 60 feet spiders can start casting Web (disadvantage)
- When within 30 feet spiders can start casting Web
	- Being hit by web will require and action to break the person out, meaning spiders gain 40 feet

### The Chasm

>[!cite]+ Read
> The narrow tunnel opens abruptly onto the edge of a vast, yawning chasm that seems to a hundred or so feet left and right. The other side lies about 30 feet away, the rocky ledge mirroring the one you stand upon. 
> 
> Far below, the faint sound of dripping water echoes up from unseen depths, and a cold draft rises, carrying with it the scent of damp stone and earth.
> 
>Spanning the chasm is a delicate-looking bridge, about 15 feet wide, made of interwoven spider silk, its gossamer threads shimmering faintly with the bioluminescent glow of nearby fungi. The bridge sways gently, anchored to stalagmites on either side, but its stability is questionable. Above, the ceiling is lost in shadow.

- DC 10 acrobatics for a single character to cross
	- +2 for each additional person at the same time on the bridge
- Difficult terrain
- DC 15 perception to see weak spots
	- DC 15 dex to not fall through
- 3 phase spiders watch from above

### The Lair

> 
> But unlike the harsh, natural chambers you've seen so far, this cavern is adorned with an array of glittering treasures and delicate trinkets. Along the natural rock ledges and nestled in crevices, you see ornate goblets encrusted with jewels, finely crafted statues, and intricate clockwork devices ticking softly. Gold coins and gleaming ingots are scattered about seemingly at random, their polished surfaces catching the faint light and casting dancing reflections that illuminate the cavern in a kaleidoscope of colors.
> 
> Silken fabrics of exotic make drape elegantly from some of the stalactites, their rich dyes depicting scenes of far-off lands and forgotten lore. Strings of pearls and gemstones are woven intricately into the webbing itself, creating patterns that shimmer with every subtle movement of the air.

> As you take in the surroundings, a large figure, hanging in the centre of the room shifts ever so slightly, her legs moving with a grace that belies her immense size.

> A massive arachnid creature, 15 feet wide with long sharp legs. Where you would expect to see the monstrous 8 eyed face of a spider, instead you see a human torso connected to the arachnid body at the waist. She has long black hair, the length of the human part of her body and pale skin. Her arms are slender with fingers that end in sharp points. Her face is beautiful, except slightly off because her smile seems slightly too large and her eyes too wide.
>

- [[Nelfandriss the Banished|Nelfandriss]] lived in the underworld hundreds of years ago, she is the daughter of a spider queen deep below.
- She experimented with dark magic, wanting to be able to be able to communicate more freely with other peoples
- She transformed herself into what you see, but in doing so was banished by her mother
- She climbed up out of the depths, hoping to find new lands, but was unable to go out in sunlight, and so was trapped in this middle area, not deep enough to be part of [[The Underdark]] societies, but not able to go out into the overland above.
- She loves stories, pretty things and trinkets
- She stole her mother's amulet when she left  
- Willing to make a deal that would save her children from being disturbed or attacked by people
