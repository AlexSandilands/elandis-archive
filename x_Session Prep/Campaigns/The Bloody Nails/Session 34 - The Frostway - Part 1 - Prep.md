### Storm

- D20 at the end of each day
	- <= 13 = Storm on the next day, otherwise clears

> [!info]+ Storm
> - DC 13 Constitution Save, exhaustion on a fail
> - Advantage if travelling with Frostwardens
> - DC increases for each concurrent storm day

### Unguided

- If the party does not bring a [[The Frostwarden Crews|Frostwarden Crew]] with them:
	- DC 15 Survival Check for each day of their travel, or they lose 1/2 a day
	- DC 15 Constitution save at the end of each day, or they gain a 2 points of exhaustion

### Discovery: Broken Cave

- Frostwarden stopped in tracks, confused by the new terrain
- Massive cave seen broken out the side of an icy wall
- Smooth tunnel, until an opening to a fairly wide cavern
- There is a Riftshard in here (Plane of Fire), along with a Remorhaz egg, and 3 scales can be found
- Criss crossing tunnels in the cavern

### Combat: Remorhaz 

- Unnatural mounts of snow found
- Searching will uncover many bodies of dead soldiers and 1 Frostwarden

> [!Danger] Combat
>- 2x Young Attack initially
>	- 1/2 Health
>	- Fox Crew will call out warnings about fire
>	- Rumbling can be felt
>- Adult attacks on 2nd round
>	- Viable Swallow Targets: Ryo, Kai, 
>	- **HEAT AURA**
>	- Bite -> Swallow (Saving Throw)
>	- Burrow -> Go to other character

- Frostwardens will leave a Mourning bell for their fallen comrade
