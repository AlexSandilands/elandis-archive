## The Underrun

### Getting There

- Require help from the [[Order of Ravens|Ravens]]
- In order for the whole group to enter through the [[The Gilded Crow|Tavern]] they'll need to wait for 15 minutes or so for two noble looking people (a man and a woman) to leave. These are nobles in the city not currently under the payroll or influence of the [[Order of Ravens|Ravens]], so a bit more subtlety would be required.

### In the Nest

- [[Lia]] will notice them leaving
> [!info]- Action
> - Concerned about her future
> - No one has asked her what she wants
> - She wants to kill [[General Varan Brado]], after that will do whatever people want from her.  
- Turns out the [[Order of Ravens - Val Miriel|Nest]] is actually built into part of the [[Underrun]], so the architecture is similar.
- There is a secret entrance in one of the side chambers, where a wall will slide back and open to the main [[Underrun]].
- There are a couple of eyeholes so a [[Order of Ravens|Raven]] can look out to check no one is on the other side.

### Travelling Through the Underrun

- A raven [[Trak Aeris]], can lead them through the twisted tunnels under the city
> [!info]+ Action
> - At one point will gesture to stop, as heard footsteps
> - Probably [[Glitter]] smugglers
- Eventually come to a grating that can be pried open
- This will be closed behind - it is essentially a one way trip
- The raven will tell them to follow the driest path, eventually there will be a dead end cavern with an opening to the land above
- The tunnel has some wash from the main water way, but eventually starts being fed from openings and pipes in the walls and ceiling of the tunnel. Filth is dropped into the tunnel from these.
- Eventually the smell becomes horrible, and at one point they will need to crawl through a narrow opening

> [!question] Trigger
> If they crawl through the opening without taking any precautions
> > [!info]- Action
> > - DC 13 Con Save - Poisoned on failure  

- Split in the tunnel, most of the waste water flowing to the right, a more solid tunnel with minimal wash is on the left

### Brown Mold

- The air starts becoming colder, much colder
- Someone may notice a couple of skeletons of small dead animals on the ground
- Ahead of them is a large crack in the wall. From this crack can be seen:
- A brown mossy like substance growing along the walls and the floor of the tunnel
- Looks like of like a soft brown carpet that stretches around 20 feet total distance (10 feet either side of the crack)

> [!question] Trigger
> When a creature enters a space within 5 feet, or when they start a turn there:
> > [!info]- Action
> > - DC 12 Con Save - 4d10 cold damage, half on save

- Fire brought within 5 feet will cause another 10ft patch to grow towards the flame
- Any cold damage destroys a patch

### Black Puddings

- Another 30 minutes or so down the tunnel another path will join theirs
> [!question] Trigger
> High passive perception will notice a large black substance on the ceiling that moves in an odd way, chance for 1 action before initiative
> > [!Danger] Combat
> > Black Puddings fall from the ceiling around the group, roll initiative!

### Exit

- Eventually they come to a cave with an opening in the ceiling 15 feet above, covered by foliage.

## Travel to Lighthaven

>[!summary]- Travel Details
> - It's ~125 miles to [[Lighthaven]] from [[Val Miriel]]
> - This is 5 days at normal pace, but can be done in ~4 since it's an [[Imperial Highways of Valtorra|Imperial Highway]] and is very easy travelling
> - Imperial highways allow traveling at a fast pace without penalty
> - They can travel for 1/2 a day on the first day (the 10th)

### Stripped Caravan

- They will encounter some merchants ([[Ana Greywhistle]], [[Petro Greywhistle]]) travelling back to [[Val Miriel]]
- They are merchants from Hillcrest, trying to sell of the final wares of their farm in [[Lighthaven]] or [[Point Blackrock]].

> [!question] Trigger
> - Perception 13 - Notice that the woman has tears in her eyes, the man is gripping the reins with white knuckles
> - If asked about what is going on:
> > [!info]- Action
> > - They had their wares "requisitioned" by a group of Imperial soldiers
> > - They were transporting mostly root vegetables, potatoes, carrots etc
> > - They are hurrying back to Hillcrest to try get some more wares to make a final sale of the winter - hoping everything hasn't gone bad by now.

### Wandering Tower

> [!question] Trigger
> - Perception DC 15
> > [!info]- Action
> > - See a tall tower made of stone out on the plains
> > - When try to move towards it they never get closer
> > - Wisdom save DC 27 will allow someone to immediately appear at the base of the tower

- They come across a group of soldiers who have just been attacked by Hobgoblin's
- Several dead soldiers, horses and hobgoblins litter the road
- There is a small cart with a missing horse
- There are three hobgoblins standing over the last living Soldier getting ready to kill him

> [!question] Trigger
> If the group is seen moving towards the remaining hobgoblins:
> > [!info]- Action
> > - They will flee if the group moves towards them and are seen
> > - They will take one of the bags from the back of the cart
