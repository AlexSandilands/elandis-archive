## Arrival at Point Blackrock

>[!summary]+ Details
> - The party arrives at evening
> - A storm is rolling in from the coast
> - Massive jagged black rocks are seen protruding from islands heading out across the ocean, where a frozen mass of ice now connects them all
> - The main road heads down into the centre of the settlement to what can be seen is the largest stone building, among mostly tents of vary colors
> - The settlement is surrounded by red tents, almost like it has been infected
> - Torches can be seen throughout, and a few people wandering back and forth between the artificial highways between the tents

### Captured (as mercenaries)

- If the party walks straight into town without stealth they will be approached by several soldiers, one of them being [[Point Blackrock#The Press Yards|Captain Kathryn Teren]]

- As they arrive at [[Point Blackrock#The Press Yards|The Press Yards]] more soldiers will be gathering around and following

## The Press Yards

>[!summary]+ Details
> - There have been 4 crossings of the [[Frostway]] recently, taking nearly 10000 soldiers across in groups
> - The most recent group left about 1 week ago
> - Only 2 of [[Frostwardens#Crews|Frostwarden Crews]] have returned so far
> - Their gear is being searched nearby in the commander's tents

### Meeting Sela Ren
 
- [[Frostwardens#Crews|Sela Ren]] is being held nearby with the rest of [[Frostwardens#Crews|Fox Crew]]
- Only she is close enough to speak
- She will suggest they have been considering breaking free
- A distraction by setting fire to the imperial tents?
- Get as many of the people being held in the Press Yards free as possible and get to [[Val Noren]]
- Probably want to wait for the storm to pass, or maybe use the storm as a way to avoid pursuit (higher danger)

### Distraction

- If they make a good enough distraction, most of the soldiers will leave the press yards
