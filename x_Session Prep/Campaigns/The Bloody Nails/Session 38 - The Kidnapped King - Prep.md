## Item Crafting

#### Smokepowder Arrows:

Roll either once with advantage to get one arrow, or twice normally to get two arrows

- Less than DC10 = Roll a D20, less than 10 it explodes on you with 3d6
	- DC10 1d6 fire & force
	- DC15 2d6 fire & force
	- DC20 4d6 fire & force

#### Dynamite Arrow

- D20
	- <=10 = 1d6 + small 5foot smoke cloud
	- >11 = 1d6 fire + 1d6 force

Fuse

- Fast fuse:
	- 10 seconds per foot
- Slow fuse:
	- 1 minute per foot

#### Chain Whip

- Will require training and practice to be proficient
- If attached to shield will require more time
