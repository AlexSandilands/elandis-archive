## Travelling the City

>[!summary]+ Details
> - Many signs of fighting
> - Buildings destroyed, burned
> - People still live here outside of the rebel territory, oppressed
> - Rebels holding the main keep and some surrounding territory, with a secondary wall of barricades 
> - There is a key pillar of black smoke visible deeper in the centre of the city. This was a cathedral that was recently attacked outside of the Rebels territory and is now burning

### Avoiding Patrols

- Group Stealth DC 15 to avoid
- Patrols of 3 or 4 soldiers commonly seen
- Will require entering buildings
- Some buildings are occupied, residents used to seeing rebels, but won't offer shelter

### Trapped Buildings

- On successful stealth, group will need to enter buildings to avoid some patrols
