## A Council is Called


#### Military Commanders

- [[Captain Erendir Thalos]] – Commander of the city guard. Loyal to the king, but pragmatic. His men are overstretched maintaining order in the city and manning the walls.
- **Marshal Kaelen Dravorn** – Veteran rebel leader who commanded much of the uprising. Fiercely opposed to the Empire, quick to push for aggressive action, but sometimes disregards logistics.
- [[Ayana Syndrosa|Ayana Wynne]] – Young but respected lead advisor of the King. Was there since the beginning and help encourage Marius to start the rebellion. (secretly an Elf in disguise)

#### Nobility and Loyal Houses

- **Lady Elira Norven** – A cousin of the king, one of the few nobles with legitimate claim to bloodline loyalty. She feels entitled to lead in his absence and may push her own agenda.
- **Lord Veyric Halstrom** – A wealthy noble who turned against the Empire only when the rebellion began to succeed. Seen as opportunistic, but his coin and men are crucial.
- **Baroness Maedra Sornel** – An older noble who sheltered rebels during the occupation. Respected, but cautious, urging diplomacy over rash strikes.

#### Civic and Civil Voices

- **High Priest Orlen of Torgar** – Voice of the temple, representing faith and tradition. Argues for resilience and unity, sees the kidnapping as a test from the gods.
- **Mistress Aeva Durn** – Matron of the refugee council. Speaks for the displaced and poor of the city, reminding the council of the human cost of war.
