## Miriel's Rest

>[!summary]- Details
> - [[Miriel's Rest]] is the cemetery behind [[The Miriel Keep]]
> - It holds the graves of members of the Miriel family, as well as relatives, nobles that were close to the family, honoured servants and others that served the family in high regard.
> - There is a large tree in the centre, and many stone paths that criss-cross around between the stones

### Vallania

- During the evening there are two guards at the gate
- Wall can be climbed DC (13)
- [[Vallania Miriel]] is waiting in the middle of the graveyard, standing in front of Miriel's Tomb, holding a hooded lantern
> [!example]- Description
> - Long straight blond hair
> - Blue eyes
> - Faint freckles
> - There is something kind of familiar

- If they don't recognise her she moves to a nearby steel gate and stands behind it holding the bars, as a way to try remind them how they saw her the first time
> [!info]+ Action
> - She wants to introduce them to her father, who has a plan to fight the Empire
> - She trusts the group as they saved her, and they are also anonymous - they cannot use people in their court or employ without risking suspicion, and it must stay that way.
> - Choice is theirs to make right now - come and learn how we could bring down the Empire or go your own way

- Can press her hand against the plaque (which reads just "Miriel") on the stone door or the tomb, which will cause it to open magically
- Inside the first level is an open room with urns stone sarcophaguses in openings around the walls.
- Stairs descend down to a second level (must be underground now) more openings along the way
- Single large sarcophagus in the room. [[Vallania Miriel|Vallania]] can press this, causing it to shift aside revealing a hidden stair beneath
- This leads to a long underground tunnel, stone walls, that carries on for 10 or so minutes
- Eventually stairs lead up to a secret basement level room

### The Plan

- [[The Kingdoms of Old]] should never have fallen
- They had grown complacent, weak and were unsuspecting of any threat
- [[Empress Morganna Eventide|The Empress]] burned across the land like a Wild Fire and before any of them began to take her seriously it was too late - her army was too large, their defences not fortified enough
- But I believe there is still strength
- I aim to reunite the Kingdoms
- Alone, any one of us would fall easily, but together, and with the right people leading the way I believe we can expel the Red Cloaks from our cities and march on [[Val Luminor]], tearing this Empire apart once and for all
- But do you see the problem?? [[Val Noren]] for example
- Any hint of sedition and we would be struck down immediately
- Our communications are constantly being monitored
- The [[Eye of the Empire|Eyes of the Empire]] are everywhere and nowhere all at once - I can't trust anyone close to me.
- So this is where you would come in. You are nobodies. You can easily traverse the continent without anyone taking notice, and you can bring my message to the right people in the right places
- Here! (Opens a box containing 12 Sending Stones)
- These are Stones of Sending - each one is a pair and if you speak into one of them, someone at the other end will hear it.
- You can bring these to key people in each of the Kingdoms, and I'll use them to arrange a meeting, a war council of sorts
- Does this sound like something within your abilities?
