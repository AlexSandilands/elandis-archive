## Intro

>[!cite]+ Read
> Welcome to Valtorra, one of the primary continents of the world Elandis.
> 
> For centuries, the ancient cities on this continent coexisted in relative harmony. Stories tell of a time, roughly a century ago, when a lone woman arrived on the eastern shores, initiating a devastating campaign across the land. The human cities fell first, followed by those of the dwarves. When it came time to invade elven territory, the invaders found their cities deserted, the inhabitants vanished without a trace.
> 
> Thus, the Crimson Empire was born, with Empress Morganna as its unyielding leader.
> 
> The past dozen years have seen increasingly difficult times. The Empire's rules have grown harsher. Men and women vanish in the dead of night. And so it was that a prison ship left dock from the northern island city of Val Norren, heading south.
> 
> Each of you found yourselves bereft of your possessions and incarcerated on this ship. You were confined two to a cell on the ship's middle deck, the metal bars cold and unyielding. Your belongings were confiscated, replaced with matching dark green peasant clothes.

> **Above Deck:** A storm is raging.

	- Steel helmet with hair crest, dark red cloak.

## The Escape

### Escaping the Cells

> [!question] Trigger
> **Option 1:** Get the Keys
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **Animal Handling (DC 12):** Convince the ship's dog to fetch the keys.
> > - **Magic/Rope:** Use mage hand or rope belts to fish them.

> [!question] Trigger
> **Option 2:** Force the Gate
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **Strength Check (DC 15):** Assisted check to force the bars.

> [!question] Trigger
> **Option 3:** Pick the Lock
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **Thieves Tools (DC 10):** Improvised using a loose nail.

### Alternate Routes Up
- **The Hatch:** Pulley is broken. Needs fixing or prying open.
- **Aft Deck:** Climb out the back of the ship.
- **Windows:** Cargo net or loose rope dangling outside.

### Storage Room (Loot Hook)

> [!question] Trigger
> Searching the Storage Room
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **DC 5 Investigation:** 2 Shortswords, 1 Dagger, 1 Leather Armor.
> > - **DC 10 Investigation:** +1 Leather Armor, Bow + 10 Arrows.
> > - **DC 12 Investigation:** Medium locked chest (Random clothes + 76g).
> > - **DC 15 Investigation:** 1 Chainmail.

### Top Deck & Lifeboats

**Escape Thresholds (Based on Actions Used):**
- **Fast:** Find a lifeboat easily.
- **Medium:** Lifeboat available, but a Sailor is there (Kill/Persuade/Abandon?).
- **Slow:** No boats left. Washed overboard.

**Boat Mechanics:**
- **Dexterity Check:** Required for low strength players to stay in the boat during the storm.

## The Beach

### Washing Ashore

> [!question] Trigger
> If anyone was washed overboard
> > [!info]- Action
> > - **Constitution Check (Pass):** Wash up with no damage.
> > - **Constitution Check (Fail):** Take 1 Hit Die of damage.
> > - **Nat 1:** Take 2 Hit Dice of damage.
> > - **Unconscious:** Party members are nearby to stabilize, otherwise start Death Saves.

### The Cave (Shelter)

- Cliff face is too slippery to climb in the storm.
- Found a cave to rest in.

**Night Watch:**
- **Watch 1 (Perception DC 10):** Hear the shipwreck crashing onto the shore.
- **Watch 3 (Perception DC 13):** Hear Goblins chattering/noticing the wreck.
