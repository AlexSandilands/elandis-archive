Ancient red dragon, survived the chaos wars by dark magic. He has been able to become immortal some evil necromantic magic similar to what a lich does, except he requires no vessel.  If he is destroyed his body and soul will reform some time later on the material plane. The only way to kill him is to weaken him to the point of death, bind his soul to a mortal, then for that mortal to die.
