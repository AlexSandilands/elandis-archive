## Overview

Ayana Wynne is the name she has used in the Material Plane. Her true name — and the name she is known by in [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]] — is **Ayana Syndrosa**. She was the former leader of the **Circle of the Weave**, the arcane circle of the Seven Circles of Sumara.

She left Sumara as part of the [[Emerald Ceremony]], leaving her second-in-command **Illythia Voss** in charge of the Circle. Most of Sumara believed her dead.

## Relationships

### Illythia Voss

Illythia Voss, current leader of the Circle of the Weave, is Ayana's **former lover**. Ayana left her in charge of the Circle when she departed Sumara twenty years ago — without adequate explanation or farewell. Illythia has led the Circle successfully but has been furious at Ayana ever since.

### Warden Caeryn

Ayana trained Caeryn years ago. He is visibly shaken by her return and is the first person in Sumara to address her by name after her restoration.
