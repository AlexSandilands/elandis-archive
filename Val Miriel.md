## Taverns

River's Watch  

- Quality: Mid-High 
    
- Description: Perched on the tranquil shores of the Luminor River, River's Watch is an elegant and welcoming tavern known for its picturesque views and sophisticated ambiance. The exterior features a charming combination of stone and timber, with large windows that offer patrons a stunning view of the river and the gentle flow of boats passing by. Inside, the decor is tasteful and cozy, with polished wooden floors, plush seating, and a large stone fireplace that crackles warmly during cooler evenings. Soft lighting and the gentle murmur of conversation create an inviting atmosphere, perfect for relaxing after a long day or celebrating special occasions.  
    
- NPC 1: Owner Aric Laris - Aric is a tall, handsome man in his late thirties, with dark hair neatly tied back and a perpetual warm smile. He is known for his impeccable manners and keen sense of hospitality. Aric inherited the tavern from his parents and has worked tirelessly to make it one of the most esteemed establishments in Val Miriel. He takes pride in knowing his regulars by name and always goes the extra mile to ensure his guests feel at home. Aric is also a good listener and often provides a sympathetic ear and wise advice to those who need it. 
    
- NPC 2: Barmaid [[Lila Spring]] - Lila is a spirited and quick-witted young woman who has worked at River's Watch for several years. She is known for her sharp tongue and warm heart, often mediating disputes and keeping the peace among the patrons. Lila dreams of one day owning her own tavern and works tirelessly to save up for her future. 
    
- NPC3: Chef Isolde - Isolde is a middle-aged woman with a round, cheerful face framed by curly auburn hair. Her passion for cooking is evident in every dish she creates, blending traditional recipes with her own innovative twists. Isolde's meals are both comforting and exquisite, making River's Watch a favored dining spot. She is known for her hearty laughter and friendly demeanor, often seen chatting with patrons who come to compliment her cuisine. Isolde's kitchen is a place of warmth and camaraderie, where she treats her staff like family. 
    

The Rusty Anchor  

- Quality: Low-end 
    
- Description: The Rusty Anchor is a rough-and-tumble tavern situated in a less reputable part of Val Miriel, close to the docks. The building is old and weathered, with creaky floors, mismatched furniture, and a pervasive smell of stale ale. The lighting is dim, and the clientele is a mix of sailors, mercenaries, and dubious characters. Despite its shabby appearance, the Rusty Anchor is a place where one can find cheap drinks, hearty meals, and a wealth of information from the city's underbelly.  
    
- NPC 1: Bartender Old Man Corwin - Corwin is a grizzled veteran with a thousand-yard stare and a wealth of stories from his adventurous past. He is a man of few words but possesses a deep well of knowledge about the city's darker corners. Corwin is fiercely protective of his tavern and its patrons, and woe betide anyone who causes trouble in his establishment. 
    
- NPC 2: Regular Patron Finn the Fence - Finn is a shifty, smooth-talking rogue who frequents the Rusty Anchor to conduct his less-than-legal business. He is well-connected and always has an ear to the ground for the latest rumors and opportunities. Finn is both charming and cunning, making him a valuable ally—or a dangerous enemy. 
    

The Gilded Crow 

- Quality: High-end 
    
- Description: The Gilded Crow is a luxurious tavern located in the heart of the Artisan Way. The exterior boasts ornate carvings and golden accents, while the interior is adorned with plush velvet seating, intricate tapestries, and a grand chandelier. The air is filled with the soft hum of live classical music, and the aroma of gourmet dishes wafts through the room. Patrons are treated to the finest wines and ales, and the service is impeccable. 
    
- NPC 1: Proprietor Lady Marielle - A former noblewoman, Lady Marielle is a graceful and refined hostess. She is known for her sharp wit and keen business acumen. Lady Marielle ensures that every guest receives the highest level of service and often engages in discreet conversations with the city's elite, gathering valuable information. 
    
- NPC 2: Head Chef Tomas - A culinary genius from a distant land, Tomas brings an exotic flair to the tavern's menu. He is passionate about his craft and takes great pride in creating dishes that are both delicious and visually stunning. Tomas is a friendly and approachable man who enjoys sharing stories of his travels with curious patrons.

Fur and Fang 

- Description: Fur and Fang is a dimly lit, rustic shop tucked between larger market stalls in the trade district. The exterior boasts a massive iron-banded wooden door adorned with the carvings of wolves and snakes, their snarling jaws and coiled tails hinting at the exotic goods inside. Upon entering, the earthy scent of cured leathers and fresh furs fills the air. Display cases hold rare claws, polished horns, and ornate trappings from creatures of the farthest reaches. The walls are lined with racks of pelts, furs of vibrant colors, and even the occasional mounted head of a slain beast. Behind the main counter, an imposing fireplace crackles, where trophies too large for display hang overhead.  
    
    - NPC1: Gara "Grimhide" Morrow - A grizzled middle-aged man with deep-set eyes, a thick, greying beard, and calloused hands. Gara is a former hunter who retired after losing a leg to a dire wolf. Now, he runs the shop, dealing in exotic hides and rare trophies. His demeanor is stern, and he speaks with the efficiency of someone who has seen too much in life. Though he can be brusque, he has a wealth of knowledge on the creatures of the wild and respects those who have earned their kills. He’s always on the lookout for rare and dangerous pelts to add to his collection. 
        
    - NPC2: Kira "Softpaw" Voss - Gara’s daughter and his primary assistant. Kira is in her early 20s, with dark hair tied back in a tight braid, and an agile build that hints at her own time spent in the wilderness. She’s quiet but keenly observant, always keeping an ear out for new rumors of rare beasts or trophies. Though she’s often in the shop, she occasionally takes on dangerous hunts, bringing back exotic goods. Kira is also skilled in crafting intricate trappings from the materials they buy and is more personable than her uncle when dealing with customers.  
        
- Quest: "The Hydra" - Gara has heard of a large "lizard like" monster that nearly killed some hunters in the forest north of Farhaven

## Raven's Nest

Nest Father: Thaddeus "Ravenwing" Karr 

Role: Nest Father of the Val Miriel Nest 

Appearance: Thaddeus is a tall, wiry man in his early 50s with sharp features and a perpetual air of calm. His hair, once jet black, is streaked with silver, and he often keeps it tied back. His piercing grey eyes are always on alert, missing nothing. 

Background: Thaddeus grew up in the underbelly of Valtorra, learning the arts of stealth and subterfuge from a young age. He rose through the ranks due to his cunning and unyielding dedication to the Order. As Nest Father, he is both respected and feared, known for his strategic mind and ruthless enforcement of the Order’s code. 

Personality: Thaddeus is a master manipulator with a deep understanding of human nature. He is patient, preferring to play the long game in his dealings. Despite his ruthless exterior, he has a strong sense of loyalty to those within the Order and sees them as his family. 

Special Skills: Expert in strategy, stealth, and hand-to-hand combat. Fluent in several languages and adept at blending into any crowd. 

 

Talon: Sera "Nightshade" Velora 

Role: Talon of the Camaar Nest 

Appearance: Sera is a striking woman in her late 30s with a lithe, athletic build. Her long, dark hair is usually worn loose, and her eyes are a captivating green. She often wears dark clothing that allows her to move silently and swiftly. 

Background: Originally from Val Solis, Sera was a dancer before joining the Order. Her grace and agility made her a natural fit for espionage. She quickly proved her worth by executing several high-profile missions, earning her the title of Talon. 

Personality: Sera is charming and charismatic, able to win over allies and seduce information out of enemies. She has a sharp wit and a playful demeanour, though she can be deadly serious when the situation demands it. 

Special Skills: Master of disguise, infiltration, and seduction. Skilled with poisons and small blades, and possesses an uncanny ability to read people. 

 

Raven: Trak "Shadow" Aeris 

Role: Raven, an operative of the Camaar Nest 

Appearance: Trak is a half-elf in his early 40s, with a lean, muscular build. His hair, once a rich brown, is now streaked with grey, and he keeps it tied back. His eyes are a deep green, often shadowed with a hint of sadness and determination. He has a few scars across his face and hands, testaments to his years in the field. 

Background: Trak has been a member of the Order of the Ravens for a significant time. He joined after the disappearance of his wife, whom he has been obsessed with finding ever since. His dedication to this personal quest has kept him in the role of a Raven, as he hasn't focused on ascending the ranks. Despite this, his skills and experience make him a valuable operative. 

Personality: Trak is driven and single-minded, often coming across as distant and aloof. His obsession with finding his wife sometimes puts him at odds with the Order's objectives, but his loyalty to the Ravens remains unshaken. He has a dry sense of humor and is fiercely protective of those he cares about, especially his child, who is one of the player characters. 

Special Skills: Trak is a master tracker and scout, with an uncanny ability to find and follow trails that others would miss. He is skilled in stealth, survival, and hand-to-hand combat. His determination and resourcefulness make him relentless in pursuit of his goals. 

 

Raven: Gideon "Whisper" Blackthorne 

Role: Raven, an operative of the Camaar Nest 

Appearance: Gideon is a young man in his mid-20s with a slender build and quick reflexes. His sandy brown hair and nondescript features allow him to blend into any crowd. He has a distinctive scar running from his left ear to his chin. 

Background: Raised as a street urchin in Val Luminor, Gideon survived by his wits and quick hands. He was recruited into the Order after he successfully pickpocketed a Raven. Impressed by his skills, they gave him a choice: join them or face the consequences. He chose to join and has never looked back. 

Personality: Gideon is resourceful and quick-thinking, always ready with a plan or an escape route. He is fiercely independent but has a strong sense of loyalty to those he considers his comrades. 

Special Skills: Exceptional pickpocket and lock-picker. Skilled in acrobatics and evasion, making him an excellent scout and courier for the Order. 

 

Fledgling: Leona "Sparrow" Voss 

Role: Fledgling, a new recruit in the Camaar Nest 

Appearance: Leona is a petite, agile young woman in her early 20s with short, cropped black hair and intense blue eyes. She often wears practical clothing that allows her to move freely. 

Background: Leona grew up in the outskirts of Camaar, working as a stable hand. She discovered her knack for stealth and observation when she overheard and relayed critical information that saved a local farmer from bandits. This caught the attention of the Order, and they offered her a place among them. 

Personality: Eager to prove herself, Leona is ambitious and quick to learn. She is curious and inquisitive, always seeking to understand the world around her. While still naive in some ways, she is determined to carve out her place in the Order. 

Special Skills: Skilled in stealth and surveillance. Has a natural talent for reading animal behavior, which she uses to her advantage in her missions. 

 

Talon: Valerius "Blackclaw" Draven 

Role: Talon of the Camaar Nest 

Appearance: Valerius is a broad-shouldered man in his 40s with a commanding presence. His black hair is peppered with grey, and he sports a neatly trimmed beard. His eyes are dark and intense, often giving the impression that he can see into one’s soul. 

Background: Valerius hails from a lineage of warriors. His family’s downfall came when they opposed the Empire, and Valerius was forced to flee. He joined the Order seeking revenge and redemption, quickly becoming one of their most formidable operatives. 

Personality: Valerius is stoic and disciplined, with a deep sense of honor and duty. He is fiercely protective of his fellow Ravens, often taking on the role of mentor and protector. Despite his intimidating exterior, he has a deep compassion for the downtrodden. 

Special Skills: Expert swordsman and tactician. Skilled in leadership and training, often tasked with preparing new recruits for the dangers they will face.
