The Confluence is the point at which the seven Nexus Citadels of [[Sumara, The Shining City|Sumara]] converge. It serves as both a physical place and the name for the council of the seven Meridians — the leaders of Sumara's Circles. To "call a Confluence" is to summon all seven Meridians to council.
