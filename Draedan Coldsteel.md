- Minor Character
- Hand of the Empire
- Was the commander of the prison ship carrying the Bloody nails to Skyreach, until it was destroyed by bad weather
- Was killed when being struck by lightning, the key he carried allows the blood nails to escape

Draedan was a human male in his, mid-40s. He was an imposing figure with a scarred face and icy blue eyes. He has a steel helmet with a crest of hair running along the top, and the signature dark red cloak of the Hands of the Empire latched on the shoulders. He speaks with a cold, measured tone, often laced with condescension.
