- Minor character
- Female Elf, mid 300s at time of death

- Silver Hair, shoulder length, straight with a narrow braid on one side tucked behind an ear
- Bright Green eyes
- Shorter than average
- Wore a simple silver, green and brown circlet that appears like roots and leaves of the World Tree

- Mother of Ryo
- Married [[Ratha Silmara]] who was of the Circle of the Blades at the time

- Rhennaya is of the Silmara bloodline, the royal family of the Sumaran elves
- Good friends with Anaya and Illythia
- Well respected by the people of Sumara, considered a benevolent and fair leader, never had any particular political dramas, only ever stepped in to steer the Confluence of the Seven when absolutely necessary if agreements could not be met.
- She made the call for the Valtorran elves to leave and take refuge in Sumara when the Empire rose.
- Killed by [[Dread General Gerard Blackmarsh]]
