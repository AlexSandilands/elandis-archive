- Minor character
Raven: Trak "Shadow" Aeris 

Role: Raven, an operative of the Val Miriel Nest 

Appearance: Trak is a half-elf in his early 40s, with a lean, muscular build. His hair, once a rich brown, is now streaked with grey, and he keeps it tied back. His eyes are a deep green, often shadowed with a hint of sadness and determination. He has a few scars across his face and hands, testaments to his years in the field. 

Background: Trak has been a member of the Order of the Ravens for a significant time. He joined after the disappearance of his wife, whom he has been obsessed with finding ever since. His dedication to this personal quest has kept him in the role of a Raven, as he hasn't focused on ascending the ranks. Despite this, his skills and experience make him a valuable operative. 

Personality: Trak is driven and single-minded, often coming across as distant and aloof. His obsession with finding his wife sometimes puts him at odds with the Order's objectives, but his loyalty to the Ravens remains unshaken. He has a dry sense of humor and is fiercely protective of those he cares about, especially his child, who is one of the player characters. 

Special Skills: Trak is a master tracker and scout, with an uncanny ability to find and follow trails that others would miss. He is skilled in stealth, survival, and hand-to-hand combat. His determination and resourcefulness make him relentless in pursuit of his goals.
