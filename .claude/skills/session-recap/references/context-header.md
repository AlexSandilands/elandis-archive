## Setting

The story takes place in the world of Elandis, on the continent Valtorra. 100 years ago the Crimson Empire took control, and now rules while oppressing the citizenry.

The player characters all have reasons to hate the empire, and are on a campaign to start rebellion and overthrow it.

## Factions

The Crimson Empire: Tyrannical ruler of the continent. Empress Morganna is the leader. Has elite groups of members loyal directly to the Empress — "Hands of the Empire, elite soldiers", "Eyes of the Empire, assassins", "Voice of the Empire, mages".

The Mawbreakers: Elite sailors, the only ones who can consistently navigate "The Maw" which is a treacherous section of water blocking the way into "The Gulf of Miriel".

The Finegolds: A merchant caravan group. Secretly working to help start the rebellion by passing as simple merchants, allowing them to easily travel between cities and get into secure locations.

The Green Gryphons: A mercenary guild. Offers work for adventurers, such as monster hunting or rescue missions.

The Order of Ravens: A thieves/assassins guild. No alignment to any faction. Works to support freedom above all.

The Elves: Missing since the rise of the empire. No one knows where they went.
