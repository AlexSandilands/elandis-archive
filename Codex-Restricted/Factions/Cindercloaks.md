**
**The Cindercloaks** are the enforcement corps and secret police of the Ummal Regency, the most immediate face of the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]]'s grip on the gem-city of [[Glimmerforge]]. To the regents they are an instrument of order; to the dwarves who live under their patrols they are the measure of how badly the current administration fears its own city.

## Purpose & Goals

The Cindercloaks exist to keep [[Glimmerforge]] obedient. They patrol the major districts, guard and shepherd the flow of gem shipments out of the city, and stamp out political dissent wherever it stirs. Above all they hunt the [[Eternal Forge]] — the dwarven resistance growing in the city's underbelly — rooting out its cells, its sympathisers, and the families who shelter them.

Their work runs in two registers. In the open, they are uniformed enforcers, a visible reminder of who holds the city. In the dark, they are a secret police: informers, night arrests, and quiet disappearances meant to ensure that no rebellion takes root the way the last one did.

## Structure & Leadership

The Cindercloaks answer directly to the regents, [[Thorgar Ummal]] and [[Hilda Ummal]], who created the corps and command it as a personal instrument rather than a purely Imperial one. The [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] sanctions their existence and lends them its authority; the Ummals decide where they march and whom they take.

## Methods

The corps relies on fear as much as force. Visible patrols and checkpoints keep the districts cowed; beneath them runs a quieter machinery of paid informants, surveillance of suspected sympathisers, and arrests carried out away from witnesses. Control of the gem shipments doubles as control of the city's lifeblood — the same hands that guard the convoys can choke a neighbourhood that steps out of line.

## History

The Cindercloaks were raised in the aftermath of Glimmerforge's fall, when [[Thorgar Ummal]] and [[Hilda Ummal]] betrayed the dwarven rebellion and handed the city to the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] in exchange for riches and the regency. The corps was built to hold what that betrayal won — to suppress the dissent the occupation bred and to ensure the broken rebellion could never reform. As the [[Eternal Forge]] has risen to take up the old cause, the Cindercloaks have become the Regency's blade against it.

## Relations

### Eternal Forge

The [[Eternal Forge]] is the Cindercloaks' chief quarry — the new dwarven resistance that has inherited the cause the regents once betrayed. Hunting its cells and breaking its sympathisers is the corps' central preoccupation, and the struggle between the two defines daily life in occupied [[Glimmerforge]].

### Valtorran Empire

The [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] is the source of the Cindercloaks' authority but not their master. It sanctions the corps and benefits from the order it imposes; the Ummals, however, raised them and direct them as their own.
