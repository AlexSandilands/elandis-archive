Istishia, the Water Lord, is the god of elemental water — of the sea, the river, and the ceaseless change that water works on all it touches. He is one of the Gods of Old, the ancient powers who held the world before the [[The Shattering|Shattering]], and is known to the present age only through scattered scholarly fragments.

## Overview

Istishia governs water in all its forms: the open ocean, the rain that renews the land, the slow patience of a current that wears stone to sand. In the oldest theology he stands among the four elemental lords, paired with [[Akadi - Restricted|Akadi]] (air), [[Grumbar]] (earth), and [[Kossuth]] (fire) — though the names of the elemental powers mean little to common folk in Elandis today. He was held to embody purification and transformation, the god of those who would be washed clean and made new.

Like the other Gods of Old, Istishia withdrew from the mortal world after the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. His one recorded act of that age was the choosing and empowering of his mortal champion, [[Maris Wavecaller]], during the [[Chaos Wars]].

## In Memory & Belief

Istishia is not worshipped. The reverence sailors and coast-folk still hold for the sea they now offer to [[Maris Wavecaller]], for fair weather and safe passage. Istishia's own name lingers only in mariners' invocations and the marginalia of pre-Shattering texts — uttered, by most, without any notion that a god once answered to it.
