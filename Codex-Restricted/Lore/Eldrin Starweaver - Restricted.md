> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Eldrin Starweaver]]*

## Core Secret
- Eldrin Starweaver was a human wizard — the chosen champion of **[[Mystra]]**, the Lady of Mysteries, the true and ancient goddess of magic — raised at the end of the Chaos Wars and the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] to lingering demigodhood the world has since mistaken for eternal divinity.
- He was consumed in the working that sealed the breach, ascended into demigodhood as the other six champions did, and has since been worshipped as an eternal god of magic. Mystra withdrew into the silence the Gods of Old keep; the world forgot her entirely and kept him.
- The Weave he is said to govern was hers in origin; she, forgotten, stands behind the worship the world gives to her champion.
- The Weave Loom may have been her instrument before it was his — the records do not say with certainty.
- Consistent with the restricted [[The Pantheon - Restricted|Pantheon]], [[The Shattering]], and [[World Tree]] (World Tree ▸ Gods of Old ▸ Seven Divines).

## Mystra's Status
- Unlike [[Selûne]], there are no established signs that Mystra is stirring or reaching into the world. Her status is genuinely open — dormant, withdrawn, or moving in ways not yet visible. An intentional blank for the DM to develop.

## Campaign — Unresolved Anomalies
- **The shrine crack (Session 07):** the crack in space [[Kai]] observed above the shrine is unexplained — possible readings: Weave instability at a site of old arcane significance, a symptom of an approaching second Shattering, or a connection to the Weave Loom. Not yet assigned a cause.
- **The purple orb vision (Session 07):** [[Vaeleran]]'s vision of a pale hand holding a crackling purple orb is similarly unresolved — its connection to Eldrin, Mystra, the Weave Loom, or something else is open.
