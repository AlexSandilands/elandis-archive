Lathander, the Morninglord, is the god of the dawn, of renewal, birth, and the vital force of new beginnings. He is one of the Gods of Old, the ancient powers who held the world before the [[The Shattering|Shattering]], and the present age remembers him only in the hopeful cast of its sunrise prayers.

## Overview

Lathander governs the rising sun and all it stands for: the first light after darkness, the renewal of life, the promise that every ending opens onto a beginning. The oldest theology set him against decay and despair, the patron of those who build, heal, and begin anew.

Like the other Gods of Old, Lathander withdrew from the mortal world after the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. His one recorded act of that age was the choosing and empowering of his mortal champion, [[Seraphina Dawnbringer]], during the [[Chaos Wars]].

## In Memory & Belief

Lathander is not worshipped. The faith the world keeps for the dawn it now directs to [[Seraphina Dawnbringer]], whose name greets the sunrise in temple and on the road alike. Lathander's own name has slipped into scholars' margins and a handful of old morning-blessings — spoken, by most, without any notion that a god once stood behind them.
