Tiamat, the Dragon Queen, is the ancient draconic power of greed, conquest, and the chromatic dragons. Alone among the powers of the elder age she set herself against the Gods of Old, and it was her hand that tore the world open in the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] and loosed the five centuries of the [[Chaos Wars]].

## Overview

Tiamat is a god of the same antiquity as the other Gods of Old, but no part of their number — an adversary of equal age, embodying greed and dominion where they embodied the ordered spheres of the world. The oldest accounts hold that she poured raw chaos into a world that had been made for peace and logic, breaking it open so that her dragons might rule the ruin.

The Gods of Old could not strike her down themselves, for fear of the cosmic war such a clash would unleash. Instead they raised the seven mortal champions of the [[Chaos Wars]], who at the war's end joined their borrowed power into the working that cast the Dragon Queen back into her own realm and sealed the breach behind her. She was driven out, but not destroyed.

## In Memory & Belief

Tiamat is not worshipped, and barely remembered. To the present age she is, at most, the dragon of nursery-tales and the monster behind the broken centuries — her name all but lost, the truth of what she was and what she did kept by only a handful of the deepest scholars. That her design was never finished, and that the work of the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] may yet be taken up again, is held closest of all.
