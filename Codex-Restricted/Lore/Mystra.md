Mystra, the Lady of Mysteries, is the goddess of magic and keeper of the Weave — the unseen fabric through which every spell in the world is drawn. She is one of the Gods of Old, the ancient powers who held the world before the [[The Shattering|Shattering]], and is remembered in the present age only in the half-spoken invocations of those who work magic.

## Overview

Mystra governs magic in all its forms: the spell on a mage's lips, the enchantment worked into steel, the wild surge of raw power and the disciplined art that tames it. The oldest theology names her the Mother of All Magic, for no working is cast that does not pass through the Weave she keeps.

Like the other Gods of Old, Mystra withdrew from the mortal world after the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. Her one recorded act of that age was the choosing and empowering of her mortal champion, [[Eldrin Starweaver]], during the [[Chaos Wars]].

## In Memory & Belief

Mystra is not worshipped. The mastery of the arcane that the world still remembers it lays at the feet of [[Eldrin Starweaver]], whose name the faithful invoke for guidance in matters of magic. Mystra herself survives only in a scholar's footnote and the cant of practising mages — and most who whisper her epithets do not know they name a god at all.
