Kossuth, the Lord of Flames, is the god of elemental fire — of the flame that warms and the flame that consumes, of destruction and the purification that follows it. He is one of the Gods of Old, the ancient powers who held the world before the [[The Shattering|Shattering]], and is known to the present age only through scattered scholarly fragments.

## Overview

Kossuth governs fire in all its forms: the hearth, the forge, the wildfire that razes a forest so that new green may rise from the ash. In the oldest theology he stands among the four elemental lords, paired with [[Akadi - Restricted|Akadi]] (air), [[Grumbar]] (earth), and [[Istishia]] (water) — though the names of the elemental powers mean little to common folk in Elandis today. He was reckoned a stern and uncompromising power, the god of trial by fire and of all that is burned away to leave something purer behind.

Like the other Gods of Old, Kossuth withdrew from the mortal world after the [[The Shattering|Shattering]]. His one recorded act of that age was the choosing and empowering of his mortal champion, [[Azara Flametongue]], during the [[Chaos Wars]].

## In Memory & Belief

Kossuth is not worshipped. The respect and dread the world still holds for fire it turns toward [[Azara Flametongue]], invoked at the forge and the funeral pyre alike. Kossuth's own name endures only in smiths' oaths and the marginalia of pre-Shattering texts — spoken, for the most part, by those who do not know they name a god at all.
