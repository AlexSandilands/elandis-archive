> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Azara Flametongue]]*

## Core Secret
- Azara Flametongue is not an eternal god. She was a tiefling sorcerer — the mortal champion chosen by [[Kossuth]], the Lord of Flames, the God of Old who held the sphere of fire before the world forgot him.
- At the [[The Shattering|Shattering]] she was consumed in the working that sealed the breach, and ascended in that sacrifice to the lingering demigodhood the world now worships as one of the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]].
- She is a real power: she holds the sphere of fire, answers prayer, and sustains the forge and the flame in the world. But she is a demigod, not an eternal god — the residue of a mortal sacrifice, not a first cause.
- Consistent with the restricted [[The Pantheon]], [[The Shattering]], and [[World Tree]] pages (World Tree ▸ Gods of Old ▸ Seven Divines).

## Kossuth, the Lord of Flames
- [[Kossuth]] is the God of Old whose sphere Azara now stands in for: ancient, withdrawn, and silent. The world has forgotten him entirely and prays to his champion in his place.
- Exists as a root stub (`Kossuth.md`). If he stirs as [[Selûne]] has (with a second Shattering approaching), that is a significant development.

## The Emberheart
- The Emberheart predates Azara's divinity as a vessel for Kossuth's own principle.
- Its location is unknown to the faithful. As Kossuth's principle carried in concentrated form, recovering it could open a direct channel to that power with unpredictable implications.
