> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Emberblade]]*

## True Nature
- No spirit, will, or sentience inside it — it is purely a vessel. What fills it is an enormous draught of ancient arcane magic drawn from the [[Emberspire]] during the [[Emberlight Vigil]] and poured into the steel as it was forged.
- The Session 48 "acknowledgement" and the blade's responsiveness in Session 50 are the latent power resonating as arcane passes through it — the first click of attunement, not anything thinking.
- It is new to the world and has not yet settled into its charge; this is why it can still grow.

## Growth / Ascension
- Built to ascend in staged tiers, modelled on a Critical Role Vestige of Divergence — a climb from its current dormant state as [[Eryndor]] bonds with it and channels magic through it as his arcane focus.
- Intended path: a later tier unlocks an activatable power; a further tier raises it to a +3 weapon; growth continues from there. (Currently a +2 rapier.)
- The specific tier-by-tier powers are not yet designed. Open thread: define what each tier grants and what triggers the step up — pure attunement / channelling, or deeds done with the blade, as a true Vestige would.
- Open thread (flavour): whether ascension eventually shows physically — brighter obsidian veining, hotter gold, a lingering glow. Subtle for now.

## Player Knowledge
- Players know none of the growth path. All they have realised (Session 50) is that the blade holds far more power than its +2 suggests, and that using it as a focus is the way to draw that power out.

## Plot Hooks
- [[Arcanist Levvel]] publicly called it one of the most powerful items she has ever seen. That assessment is the kind of thing that could travel — a uniquely potent, growing artifact in a rogue's hand is a natural draw for a collector or the [[Circle of the Weave]] later. No one outside the party covets it yet; it was only just crafted.
