The Moon's Tear is a true relic of the living goddess [[Selûne]] — an opalescent teardrop amulet, not a faith-keepsake but a genuine instrument of her power. It rests within the [[Vault of the Moon]], waiting for the Chosen who unites the three [[Moonstone|Stones of the Moon]] and opens the way to it.

## What It Does
- Grants visions and guidance in the deepest dark, and steers its bearer true — by sea, by road, and in spirit.
- For a God of Old forbidden to act openly, it is one of the few channels by which her hand may reach the world without breaking her silence outright: a way to turn a bearer toward the road she would have them walk while never being seen to push.

## Significance
- The whole hunt for the three [[Moonstone|Moonstones]] is, ultimately, Selûne's mechanism for delivering this relic to her Chosen. The Tear is the prize the Vault was sealed to protect.
- Open threads (from [[Selûne]] restricted notes): whether it has any part in her reaching for a Chosen, and whether her dark sister [[Shar]] would covet it for the same reasons.
