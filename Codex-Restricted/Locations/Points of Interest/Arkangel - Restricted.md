> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Arkangel]]*

## Unrevealed History
- Arkangel is a **[[Chaos Wars]] creation** — one of the few still standing intact (most others are ruins). It was built as a mobile repository to carry the age's accumulated magical knowledge across the world, and as a platform from which mages could travel to wherever they were needed. Its creators understood that fixed institutions could be taken, burned, or occupied; a city that moved had no such vulnerability.
- When the Chaos Wars ended, Arkangel did not — it outlasted the age, the institution that built it, and the mages who first staffed it. The world forgot how it came to exist. Even most mages who have visited know only that it is ancient, not what made it or why. The neutrality constraint was deliberately built into the city by the ancients — not a rule that can be overruled, but a condition of how the place works.

## Plot Hooks
- The party could discover Arkangel's true origin if they gain access to its oldest records — a thread if they ever reach the city themselves. The revelation that it was built to survive a world-ending catastrophe could carry weight if similar threats resurface.

## Hidden Features
- The full depth of the archive is not apparent on first visit. Somewhere in its oldest stacks may be documentation on the Chaos Wars that exists nowhere else in the world.
