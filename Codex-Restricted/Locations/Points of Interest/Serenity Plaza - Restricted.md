> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Serenity Plaza]]*

## The Inferno Was No Accident
- The explosion that killed the Imperial soldiers and summoned the two fire elementals during the Session 22 rally was deliberate — the work of [[Felix]], secretly an elven mage and one of the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]]'s quietest enemies in the city.
- Felix was the street-preacher leading the rally that night. He triggered the blast and the elementals, targeting the guards precisely, then used the chaos as cover to vanish — leaving everyone, the party included, to believe the firebrand orator had been vaporised in his own fire. The party witnessed it all from the middle of the chaos but never identified the source.
- His act here is the visible edge of a long radicalization against the Empire, accelerated by the worsening imperial oppression of [[Val Miriel]].
