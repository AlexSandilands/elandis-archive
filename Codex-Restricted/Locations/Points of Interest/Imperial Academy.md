The Imperial Academy is the institution on Eventide Isle from which the [[Hand of The Empire|Hands of the Empire]] emerge — the training and indoctrination facility where the [[Valtorran Empire]]'s most loyal enforcers are made. Promising soldiers from across the conquered kingdoms are selected, brought to [[Val Luminor]], and put through a rigorous programme before they earn the red cloak. Its presence in the capital is the reason Val Luminor holds a larger concentration of Hands than any other city on [[Valtorra]].

## Location & Geography

The Academy stands on Eventide Isle, the tightly controlled Imperial heart of [[Val Luminor]], its pale stone halls dominating the island skyline alongside the [[Citadel of Val Luminor|Citadel]]. Its physical training grounds are the [[Golden Downs Palaestra]], the broad open drill-field named for the golden grain country visible beyond the city's outer edge. Candidates arrive here from across the Imperial provinces and do not leave until they are either dismissed or have earned the red cloak.

## The Making of a Hand

The Academy drills and tests its candidates relentlessly, and many wash out and are returned to the regular legions. But the physical training is not what makes a Hand. The defining step happens off-site: graduation is a private audience with [[Empress Morganna Eventide|the Empress]] in the [[Citadel of Val Luminor|Citadel]], where the violet orb bends the candidate's will permanently and draws a measure of their life force. The Academy produces the soldier; the ceremony in the Citadel closes them. Full mechanism in [[Hand of The Empire - Restricted]].
