> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Frostway]]*

## The Engineered Slaughter

- The public story is that the [[Valtorran Empire|Crimson Empire]]'s march woke the Frostway's monsters by chance. It was not chance. Held captive and forced to lead the Imperial army across the ice, the [[Frostwardens]] deliberately routed the column through a dangerous stretch, gambling that the noise and vibration of thousands of marching soldiers would draw the hunters and tear the army apart.
- It worked — a little too well. The slaughter that followed was greater than intended, and bled over onto the wardens' own crews.
- The Empire does not know the massacre was engineered. If it ever learns that the Frostwardens turned the ice into a weapon against it, the brotherhood — and [[Point Blackrock]] — would face brutal reprisal.

## Open Threads

- At least one Riftshard lies buried on the ice, and the density of monsters across the whole crossing implies others are scattered through the islands. Where they are, what they truly are, and what the egg in the rift cavern ([[Session 34 - Mourning Bells]]) was incubating are all unresolved — hooks for a future return to the Frostway.
