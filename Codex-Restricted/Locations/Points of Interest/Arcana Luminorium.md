The Arcana Luminorium is one of the two great bastions of magical learning on [[Valtorra]] — the other being the Prismatic Academy in [[Glimmerforge]] — standing on Eventide Isle in [[Val Luminor]]. It pre-dates the [[Valtorran Empire]] but is now wholly under Imperial control, serving as the home and training ground of the [[Voice of the Empire|Voices of the Empire]] in the capital, its faculty and curriculum shaped entirely to serve the Empire's arcane needs.

## Location & Geography

The Luminorium stands on Eventide Isle, the Imperial heart of [[Val Luminor]], among the pale stone monuments of the island. It is the institutional seat from which the [[Voice of the Empire|Voices]] maintain their advisory presence in the capital, the head of the cadre advising [[Empress Morganna Eventide|the Empress]] from close by within the [[Citadel of Val Luminor|Citadel]].

## Seat of the Voices

Human Voices are taken in young and raised here through the full conditioning the programme demands — not arcane education alone, but the shaping of loyalty from childhood. The institution pre-dates the Empire, having stood since the age of reconstruction that followed the [[Chaos Wars]], and was never stormed; the Empress took it by quiet institutional capture, replacing its faculty and reshaping its curriculum over time. Full recruitment and conditioning detail in [[Voice of the Empire - Restricted]].
