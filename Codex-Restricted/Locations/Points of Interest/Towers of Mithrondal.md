The Towers of Mithrondal are a pair of colossal elven sentinel towers standing on opposing headlands at the mouth of the great southern inlet of [[Erivan Island]], flanking the only deep-water passage to the docks of [[Ithilmara]]. Hundreds of feet tall and visible from miles out to sea, they are among the most imposing structures on all of [[Valtorra]] — their scale less that of a fortification than a declaration.

## Location & Geography

The Towers stand at the narrowest point of the broad inlet that cuts into the southern coast of [[Erivan Island]], one on each headland, the channel between them the only viable approach for sea traffic bound for [[Ithilmara]]. From the open water of the Erivan Strait, they appear long before any other feature of the island — two pale spires rising above the treeline, growing taller as a vessel draws near until they fill the horizon entirely.

Each tower is built from the same white stone as the elven cities of the island, rising in slender, tapered columns of distinctly elven proportion. Carved reliefs and arched windows run the full height of both structures, and broad observation platforms crown their peaks. The surrounding headlands are heavily forested, the trees pressing close to the bases of the towers and, after a hundred years of abandonment, beginning to encroach upon the small cluster of buildings — quarters, storehouses, a modest quay — that once housed the garrison and its support population.

## Purpose

The Towers were never a city but a manned outpost. Their permanent population consisted of elven wardens and officials responsible for monitoring the strait and overseeing all maritime traffic approaching [[Ithilmara]], alongside a small civilian population providing provisions and logistical support. Every vessel making for the elven capital passed directly beneath the gaze of both towers.

When the [[Silmara Family|Silmara]] royal line activated the World Gate during the Exile — the desperate passage of the elves into the [[Faewild]] as the [[Valtorran Empire]] closed across the mainland — the garrison and its inhabitants crossed with them. The Towers have stood empty since, sealed like the rest of [[Erivan Island]] behind the wards that still punish any non-elf who lingers.
