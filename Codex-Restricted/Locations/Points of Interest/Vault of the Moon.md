The Vault of the Moon — also called the Celestial Cavern — is a hidden holy place blessed by [[Selûne]]. It is where her champion [[Elaris Moonsong]] first sought her out and became her chosen, and it is now the resting place of her relic, the [[Moon's Tear]].

## Opening the Vault
- Sealed, and opened only by uniting all three [[Moonstone|Stones of the Moon]] — the task set before Selûne's Chosen. (S20 prep, S26)
- The three stones are the keys; the Vault is the lock. Until they are brought together, there is no way in.

## What Lies Within
- A sanctified space, blessed by the goddess at the moment Elaris Moonsong took up her cause — sacred ground at the root of the moon faith.
- The [[Moon's Tear]], Selûne's own relic, rests here, awaiting the Chosen who opens the way.
