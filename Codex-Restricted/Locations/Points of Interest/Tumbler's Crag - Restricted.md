## The Rift

- The rift beneath Tumbler's Crag is one of four planar rift sites the party has encountered across Valtorra. Its exact nature and its relationship to the other sites remain unresolved.
- The rift's necromantic energy is what shaped Salla into a hostile ghost rather than simply a restless spirit — her haunting was as much a product of the rift's corruption as of her own grief and death.
- What the four rift sites share, what they're building toward, and what force or event created them is the deeper mystery; the Crag's rift is the earliest instance the party connected to the larger pattern.
