## Lady Marielle's Complicity
- Lady Marielle is not an unwitting cover — she knows full well about the [[Order of Ravens - Val Miriel|Ravens]] beneath her floor and is paid off by the Order to maintain the front and control who is allowed to pass below.
- Her gossip-trading among the city's elite and her standing as a former noblewoman are an asset to the Nest, not just colour: she is a set of eyes and ears in noble circles as well as a gatekeeper at the bar.
