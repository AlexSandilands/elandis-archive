> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Bridge of Stars]]*

## A Front

- The shop is a cover identity. It is the legitimate business behind which [[Felix]] — an elven mage in sustained human disguise and one of the [[Valtorran Empire]]'s quiet opponents — operates in the heart of the Empire's second city. He founded it on arrival expressly as both a real shop and a useful cover. (See [[Felix]] — Restricted, [[Emerald Ceremony]] — Restricted.)
- The arcane-trade obsession is genuinely his own, not merely cover — his knowledge of magical goods and ruin-salvage is real, which is part of what makes the cover so convincing.

## Nothing to Find

- [[Felix]] works alone. The elves who left through the [[Emerald Ceremony]] do not keep in contact, so the premises are no safehouse, dead-drop, or cell hub — there is no network to uncover here. The cover holds precisely because there is nothing to find: a real shop, run by a real obsessive, who simply happens to be someone other than he appears.

## Plot Hooks

- Serenity Plaza: [[Felix]] was behind the explosion that incinerated the Imperial soldiers and birthed two fire elementals during the anti-Empire rally at [[Serenity Plaza]] in [[Session 22 - Inferno at Serenity Plaza]]. The party witnessed the blast but never traced it to him or to the shop. If they ever investigate the bombing back to its source, it leads here.
