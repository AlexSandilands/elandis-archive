> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[River's Watch]]*

## Why the Meetings Happen Here — Not the Gilded Crow

- River's Watch has **no standing tie to the [[Order of Ravens]]**. The run of clandestine Raven contacts that played out here (Gideon's handover, Jarek's reunion, Trak's pull-aside) happened *around the party because the party was based here* — not because the tavern is a Raven house.
- The deeper reason it became the de facto meeting spot is [[Gemma Finegold]]. She is the **Grand Raven** (see [[Gemma Finegold - Restricted]]), and [[The Gilded Crow]] is the Order's true hidden sanctuary, with a concealed entrance behind a keg.
- Gemma cannot be seen frequenting the Gilded Crow without risking the cover she hides behind — so a refined, neutral, unaffiliated tavern that perfectly suits a well-off merchant's daughter is exactly where she would choose to stay and quietly organise things. River's Watch's role as a meeting place is a by-product of the Grand Raven hiding in plain sight, not of any secret the tavern itself holds.

## Open Threads

- River's Watch holds no secrets of its own for now — genuinely just a good, clean tavern that happens to sit in the right spot. If the campaign ever wants a buried hook here (a back room, a river entrance, an Aric with a past), it's open ground.
