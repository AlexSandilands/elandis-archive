> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[The Maw]]*

## True Cause of the Whirlpool

- The Maw is not driven by ordinary "deep currents" — that is only the explanation the world has settled on. Its true cause is a [[Riftshard]] linked to the Plane of Water, sitting beneath the gulf's mouth.
- This Riftshard pulls water out of the Material and into the Plane of Water — the permanent, inward-spiralling drain that makes the whirlpool. It is the inverse twin of the great Riftshard in the Sunken Temple beneath the [[Darkwood]]'s central lake, which is also a Plane-of-Water leak but runs the other way, gushing water out to feed that lake.
- The public [[The Maw]] entry only ever describes the whirlpool as deep, unshifting currents, so this truth sits hidden beneath the accepted explanation rather than contradicting it. No one in the world connects the Maw to the Shattering or the Riftshards.

## DM Notes

- Ties the Maw into the campaign's planar spine: the same era and the same kind of leak as the [[Riftshard]]s, the [[Emberspire]], and the Sunken Temple in the [[Darkwood]].
- The two matched Plane-of-Water shards (the Maw draining in, the Darkwood lake gushing out) are a deliberate pairing — useful if the party ever studies riftshard behaviour or looks for one they can reach and observe safely.
