> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Ruby Falls Goldmine]]*

## The Standing Arrangement
- The treaty with [[Nelfandriss the Banished]] is live and load-bearing: the party reports the mine collapsed and empty; she lets the party's chosen allies (the rebellion / [[Green Gryphons]] proxies) work it quietly in exchange for privacy. Both halves depend on the Empire never learning the mine is intact.
- The cover story is fragile. The [[Green Gryphons]] leadership ([[Porta Hale]]) were told the mine naturally collapsed and held no gold — the party lied to their own guild for their pay. If the Gryphons or the Empire ever inspect the seal, dig it out, or send a second expedition, the lie unravels and Nelfandriss is exposed.
- Leverage hook: anyone who learns the truth (a surviving miner's family, a suspicious Imperial agent, a Gryphon who doubts the story) has a hold over the party.

## Beneath the Seal
- The sealed entrance is not the only way in or out. The deeper cavern network (the [[Bioluminescent Forest]] caves, the mushroom-folk warren, the [[Pool of Reflection]]) connects onward deeper underground — unexplored, and a potential back door for re-entry or for something to come out.
- The mushroom-folk remain down there as quiet neighbours of Nelfandriss; they could be a future contact point or a hazard depending on how they're approached.
- The gold hoard chamber still holds the stripped wealth of the dead miners and soldiers — far more than the party carried out. It's a treasure the Empire would kill to reach, and a reason for renewed intrusion.
