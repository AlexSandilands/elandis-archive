> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[High Sept]]*

## Hidden Features

- One of the three [[Moonstone|moonstones]] of the Selûne's-Chosen prophecy is set into the Sept's central altar, kept by [[High Priest Oldir]]. Confirmed by Praetor as the moonstone Oldir holds at the High Sept (see [[High Priest Oldir]]'s notes).

## The Buried Heresy

- The Sept is the seat of the faith's deepest secret. Oldir guards the truth, passed down each generation's line of succession: the celebrated Seven are not true gods but ancient mortal champions who ascended, their worship burying the memory of the older, greater true gods — among them [[Selûne]] and her dark sister, the Lady of Vengeance.
- The full secret lives on [[High Priest Oldir]], [[The Pantheon]] ("The Ascended"), and [[Selûne]] — the Sept is simply where it is physically kept and quietly held.

## Plot Hooks

- The moonstone in the altar is one of three the prophecy turns on; the Sept is a destination for anyone hunting them.
- Oldir acknowledged Kai as a potential — if corrupted — Chosen, troubled by the black stars of the Dark Sister in Kai's Starry Form. The Sept is where that thread can be picked up again.
