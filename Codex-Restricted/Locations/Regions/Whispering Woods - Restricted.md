> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Whispering Woods]]*

## Secret History — the vanishing was the raid

- The "vanishing without a trace" the party knows from [[Eryndor]]'s backstory and relived in the Pool of Insight ([[Session 49 - The Silver Sage]]) and the Imperial raid that swept the woods are the same event, seen from different distances. To a five-year-old and his frantic father, [[Eirieneth Silmara|Eirieneth]] simply disappeared overnight with no clue; only later, travelling and learning that all the elves were gone, did the wider pattern emerge.
- The truth Eryndor and [[Jarek]] never grasped at the time: she was taken by the [[Valtorran Empire]] in that raid, not lost to some mystery of the woods.

## Plot Hooks

- [[Eirieneth Silmara|Eirieneth]] is held prisoner in [[Val Luminor]], the Empire's seat — the fate behind her disappearance from the Whispering Woods, and a thread still open for [[Eryndor]].
