> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Seraphon]]*

## The Old Gods Are Real
- The public entry says Seraphon's enduring cults worship "the old gods" rather than the [[The Pantheon|Seven Divines]] — and presents this as forgotten relic-tradition.
- The reserve: those old gods are the Gods of Old — the true, ancient powers behind the Seven Divines, whom the wider world does not know exist (see [[The Pantheon]] restricted lore). The Seven are ascended mortal champions; the powers they stand in for are the real and hidden gods.
- Seraphon is plausibly the place where worship of the Gods of Old survived most openly and most intact. Its old-god cults may therefore sit closer to the genuine theological truth of Elandis than any temple of the Seven anywhere in the world — without necessarily knowing the full shape of what they keep.

## Plot Hooks / Open Threads
- A Seraphon cult could hold knowledge of the Gods of Old that the party (or factions like High Priest Oldir's line) would kill or die for.
- The arcane regulatory institution and the old-god cults coexisting on one continent is fertile ground — does the institution suppress them, study them, or quietly answer to them? Undeveloped; open.
