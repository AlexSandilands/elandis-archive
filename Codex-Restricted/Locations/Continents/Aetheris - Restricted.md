> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Aetheris]]*

## The Lost Epicentre
- The exact site where the Dragon Queen crossed and [[The Shattering]] began cannot be found — it was healed by the Sacrifice of the Seven and leaves no marker on the land. This is true and public.
- The reserve: the rift is genuinely gone, but the *echo* of the Shattering is not. The unstable, wild magic that grows thicker the deeper one goes into the Shattered Wastes is densest of all around the (unfindable) epicentre — the one place on Elandis where the Shattering is, in a sense, still bleeding into the world.

## Plot Hooks
- A deep-Wastes expedition toward the heart of the unstable-magic zone is a ready-made adventure site: the closer the party gets, the more reality frays. What they find at the centre is open — a candidate for a major set-piece.
- Pre-Shattering ruins in the Wastes are the richest cache of lost iron-and-steam-age knowledge in the world. Anything the campaign needs from the age before could surface here.
