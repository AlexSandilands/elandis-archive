**Redhaven** is a small halfling farming town in the [[Luminor Plains]], a day's travel south of the Imperial capital of [[Val Luminor]]. Like its neighbours across the plains it is part of the Empire's breadbasket, a quiet community of farmers whose grain and produce feed the capital to the north.

## Geography & Layout

Redhaven sits in open grassland south of [[Val Luminor]], strung along the cart-road that runs down toward the watch-town of [[Valewatch]] and the southern highlands of [[Val Aerie]]. The soil here carries a distinctive reddish cast, staining the tilled fields and the local brick alike — the likeliest source of the town's name. The settlement itself is low and spread out: red-brick cottages and grain-stores clustered around a dirt market square, with a single roadside inn that gives the place its "haven" and serves the travellers and carters moving south out of the capital.

## The People

Redhaven is a halfling farming community, peaceable and unremarkable, turning on the rhythm of planting and harvest. Its fields feed the capital, and in return the southern road brings a steady trickle of trade and travellers through its one inn.
