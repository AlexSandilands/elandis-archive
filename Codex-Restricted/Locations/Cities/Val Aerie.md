Val Aerie is an ancient mountain fortress-city on [[Valtorra]], seat of the Val Aerie Sept and home of the Cloudwatch. One of the six Kingdoms of Old raised in the long aftermath of the [[Chaos Wars]], it stands in the southern highlands where the peaks climb through the permanent cloud-line and the sky seems to swallow the land. Of the six great kingdoms, Val Aerie is perhaps the most remote — a tiered citadel of cold stone, perpetually wreathed in cloud, its silhouette made unmistakable by the great eagles that circle it through the mist. Today it stands under the [[Valtorran Empire]].

## Geography & Layout

Val Aerie stands in the south-central highlands of [[Valtorra]], near the watch-town of [[Valewatch]], built into and against the sheer face of a mountain at an altitude that places it reliably above the cloud line. The city climbs vertically rather than spreading across a plain — gates, walls, and towers rise in tiers along the cliff face, stacked one above the next so that travellers approaching from below emerge from cloud to find the city already looming above them, its upper spires still lost in grey. The air at its gates is thin and cold in every season. The surrounding highlands offer little farmland or lowland settlement; Val Aerie is not a trading hub by position so much as a fortress whose height is itself its first line of defence.

## The Cloudwatch

Val Aerie is best known beyond the highlands for its flock of giant eagles and the Cloudwatch — the elite defensive corps that maintains them. Members are known individually as Watchers, and their riders banking through the cloud on eagle-back have become the city's defining image across the continent. The eagles serve in scouting, patrol, and high-altitude defence, but they are also a symbol of the city's identity woven into its very name — *aerie* meaning eagle's nest. The Cloudwatch's presence signals that Val Aerie's mountain isolation is not a vulnerability; it is the point.
