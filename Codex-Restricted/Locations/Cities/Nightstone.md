**Nightstone** is a halfling farming town in the north-east of the [[Luminor Plains]], set where the grasslands give way to the eastern mountains. The most easterly of the plains' farming towns, it tends the same breadbasket fields as its neighbours while working a modest stone trade on the side.

## Geography & Layout

Nightstone stands north-east of [[Val Luminor]], at the point where the [[Luminor Plains]] meet the rising ground of the [[Borealia Range]], on the road that branches away toward the Imperial coastal city of [[Shardcrest]]. The grain fields end abruptly against the slopes, where a small quarry cuts into the mountain's foot. The town is built largely of the dark stone drawn from that quarry, which lends it both its name and a sombre look against the gold of the surrounding fields.

## The People & Trade

Like the rest of the plains towns, Nightstone is a peaceable halfling farming community, its harvests bound for the capital. Its quarry sets it a little apart: the dark stone is worked and carted out alongside the grain, and the town is the last settled stop on the plains before the road climbs east toward the coast.
