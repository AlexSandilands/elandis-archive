**Hillcrest** is a halfling farming town in the south-east of the [[Luminor Plains]], set among low rolling hills on the road toward [[Val Miriel]]. Where much of the plains grows grain, Hillcrest works the heavier soil of the rises for root vegetables, and its merchant families haul that produce out to the eastern frontier to sell.

## Geography & Layout

Hillcrest sits on the rolling rises south-east of [[Val Luminor]], strung along the road that runs down toward the Gulf city of [[Val Miriel]]. The low hills that give the town its name are worked into terraced plots and fields, and the surrounding country yields mostly root crops — potatoes, carrots, and the like — rather than the open grain that dominates the plains further west.

## The People & Trade

Hillcrest is a hardy halfling farming community, used to the long haul to market. Its produce is carted east to the Darkwood-edge towns of [[Lighthaven]] and [[Point Blackrock]], a trade run by local merchant families such as the Greywhistles — Ana and Petro Greywhistle among them — who sell off the last of the winter's wares before the roads turn.
