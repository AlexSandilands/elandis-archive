**Shardcrest** is an Imperial port city commanding the north-eastern coast of [[Valtorra]], the great shipping hub through which the eastern plains towns sell their crops to the wider Empire. The end of the long road east out of the [[Luminor Plains]], it is where the breadbasket's grain meets the sea.

## Geography & Layout

Shardcrest stands on the north-eastern shore, east of the [[Borealia Range]] that walls the interior off from the coast, at the seaward end of the road that runs out of the [[Luminor Plains]] past [[Nightstone]] and through the eastern hills. Its harbour is the city's heart — a deepwater port crowded with cargo ships and grain barges — and the city climbs inland from the quays and warehouses of the waterfront.

## Trade & Imperial Role

Shardcrest is the principal outlet for the eastern reaches of the breadbasket: the grain and produce of the plains towns are carted east to its docks and shipped on from there, making it one of the Empire's busiest eastern ports and a city the Imperial administration holds firmly.
