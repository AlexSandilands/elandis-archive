**Brightwater** is an Imperial dock city built at the greener southern edge of the [[Xericana Sands]], raised to give the Empire a sea route to the dwarven kingdoms without recourse to the long river passage up the [[Silver Mountains]]. A purpose-built supply port, its whole reason for being is the line it keeps open between the coast and the mountain holds.

## Geography & Layout

Brightwater stands on the southern coast of [[Valtorra]], where the arid expanse of the [[Xericana Sands]] softens into greener country before meeting the sea. Its harbour faces the dwarven realms of the southern [[Silver Mountains]], and from its docks supplies can be carried inland to the mountain kingdoms by a far easier road than the river route the Empire would otherwise be forced to use.

## Imperial Role

Brightwater exists to feed the supply line to and from the dwarven holds — seated in the [[Silver Mountains]] at [[Glimmerforge]] and guarded at the valley's southern mouth by [[Orespire]] — sparing the Empire the slow, difficult haul up the river through the mountains. It is a working Imperial port, valued for the route it secures rather than for any older heritage.
