Galathilion is one of three elven cities on [[Erivan Island]], the abandoned homeland of The Valtorran Elves, standing on the island's western coast amid dense forest and mountain terrain. Like [[Ithilmara]] to the east, it has stood wholly intact and empty for a century, its streets falling silent the day its people departed through the World Gate and did not return. The wilds have been reclaiming it ever since.

## Geography & Layout

Galathilion sits on the far western coast of [[Erivan Island]], where the land drops directly to open sea. Where [[Ithilmara]] to the east faces inward onto the island's great southern inlet, Galathilion looks outward — a city that met the ocean head-on, the island's westernmost point of habitation. The mountain spine of the interior rises behind it to the east, with forested hills rolling down to the waterfront.

The architecture is unmistakably elven: pale white stone, deliberate ring-streets, spires and arched colonnades built with a precision that human cities never quite match. A century of abandonment has softened the lines. Trees have pushed through the cobblestones, vines climb the facades, and the surrounding forest presses steadily inward at every edge. The city is still beautiful, and still whole — nothing has fallen, nothing has been taken — but the sense of something suspended, waiting, is inescapable.

Like all the cities of [[Erivan Island]], Galathilion is warded against non-elves. Any intruder who lingers suffers mounting psychic agony; the wards have never required renewal since the night of the Exile, when the [[Circle of the Weave]] sealed them as the last elves departed.
