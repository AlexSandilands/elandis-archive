**Valewatch** is an old city of [[Valtorra]], now under Imperial occupation, standing where the [[Luminor Plains]] give way to the southern reaches of the continent. A watch-town of long standing near [[Val Aerie]], it guards the threshold between the heartland plains and the southern uplands.

## Geography & Layout

Valewatch sits in the south-central highlands, at the boundary where the broad grasslands of the [[Luminor Plains]] break against the southern reaches of the continent, close to [[Val Aerie]]. Its position on this frontier of the heartland is the source of its name and its purpose — a watch held over the southern edge of the plains.

## History & Imperial Role

An old city rather than a planted Imperial one, Valewatch was taken and is now held under occupation, its ancient seat overlaid by an Imperial garrison. It keeps watch on the boundary of the southern country, a once-independent town worn by age and kept under the Empire's hand.
