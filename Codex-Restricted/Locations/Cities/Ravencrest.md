**Ravencrest** is a halfling farming town at the north-western edge of the [[Luminor Plains]], where the grasslands rise into the foothills of the mountains. The most north-westerly of the plains' farming towns, it works the thinning fields of the upland edge and serves as a waypoint on the road running north toward [[Val Aran]].

## Geography & Layout

Ravencrest sits north-west of [[Val Luminor]], at the western edge of the plains where the open grassland breaks against the foothills of the [[Silver Mountains]]. Streams come down out of the crags here to feed the headwaters that drain toward the [[Luminor River]], and the fields grow thinner and stonier as the ground climbs. The town is strung along a low ridge on the northern road toward [[Val Aran]], its stone cottages set among the rising rock. Ravens nest in the broken crags above the settlement — the source of its name.

## The People

Ravencrest is a halfling farming community like its neighbours, though its upland fields are leaner than the rich grain country to the south-east. As a waypoint on the northern road it sees more passing traffic than most plains towns, including the Imperial patrols that work the route toward the restless north.
