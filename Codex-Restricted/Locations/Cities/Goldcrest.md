**Goldcrest** is a halfling farming town in the [[Luminor Plains]] east of the Imperial capital, set amid some of the richest grain country on the continent. One of the prime wheat towns of the Empire's breadbasket, much of its harvest flows back west to feed [[Val Luminor]] itself.

## Geography & Layout

Goldcrest lies in the grain country east of [[Val Luminor]], between [[Ashbourne]] to the north and [[Hillcrest]] to the south, where the open plains begin to roll toward the eastern foothills. Broad fields of wheat surround the town and turn the whole landscape gold at harvest — the source of its name — their yield gathered into the granaries that rise above the cottages.

## The People & Trade

Goldcrest is a prosperous halfling farming community, its fortunes tied to the grain harvest. As one of the breadbasket's principal wheat towns it sends a heavy share of its yield back to the capital, and the rhythm of sowing and reaping governs the whole life of the place.
