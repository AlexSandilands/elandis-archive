**Everfall** is an Imperial city set deep in the cold north of [[Valtorra]], raised to give the [[Valtorran Empire]] a foothold over the harsh upper reaches of the continent. A planted stronghold rather than an old settlement, it exists to project Imperial order into a country that resists it.

## Geography & Layout

Everfall lies far to the north, deep within the immense boreal woodland of the [[Borealia Forest]] that blankets the upper third of the continent — well beyond [[Val Aran]], which stands at the forest's southern mouth. Hemmed in by ancient cold-weather wilderness on every side, it is one of the most remote and isolated of the Empire's cities.

## Imperial Role

Everfall was built deliberately, to plant an Imperial presence in the harsh north where Imperial authority otherwise thins to nothing. It serves as a garrison, an administrative seat, and a statement of reach in a region the Empire struggles to hold — a hard posting on a hostile frontier.
