> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Darmouth]]*

## Order of Ravens Cell

The Order still maintains a cell in Darmouth. The party has not returned to the city since the jailbreak in Session 04, so players are unaware of its current composition, leadership, or status under the Imperial crackdown. Details for the DM to establish when the city is revisited.

## The Crackdown's Reach

The Imperial investigation into the Skyreach theft and the Bastille break have left threads in Darmouth that are still being pulled. Specific contacts are exposed:

- Eliza Farthington at the Seafarer's Rest sheltered the party on the night of the jailbreak.
- Thora and Freya Ironsong did commissioned work for Neros.
- Any of these individuals could be under surveillance, under pressure, or available as leverage if the Empire wishes to draw the party back.

## The Temple Tunnel

The subterranean escape passage beneath the Temple of the Seven Divines is known to the Order of Ravens through Sister Arianna Brightstar. It remains an active asset, undiscovered by the Imperial authorities.

## Open Thread

The Bastille guard Layla was left bound and gagged in the processing room after the Session 04 jailbreak. How quickly she was found, what she was able to identify of the party or the disguises used, and the consequences for her career or freedom are unresolved.
