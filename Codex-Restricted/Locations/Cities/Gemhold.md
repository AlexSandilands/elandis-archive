Gemhold is the great mining hold of the [[Silver Mountains]], carved deep into the western range and built around one of the richest gem deposits on the continent. Where [[Glimmerforge]] is the dwarven capital, Gemhold is its industrial counterpart — the place where the wealth of the mountains comes out of the earth. It was the site of the Dwarven Rebellions' only major success, and the [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]] has not managed the hold the same way since.

## Geography & Layout

Gemhold sits in the western arm of the [[Silver Mountains]], built wholly within the mountain and looking out toward the [[Xericana Sands]] beyond. Its exterior is a fortified gate complex cut into the rock face — functional, heavily defended, without the architectural grandeur of [[Glimmerforge]] — and the hold proper is underground: a vast network of mine shafts, carved working halls, and deep tunnels extending into the mountain. The deepest shafts reach veins of gem-bearing stone that have been worked for centuries without exhaustion. A long tunnel road connects Gemhold eastward through the range to [[Glimmerforge]], making it the western terminus of the dwarven underground road.

## History

Gemhold was established alongside [[Glimmerforge]] in the age after the [[Chaos Wars]], the mining counterpart to the capital's civic and cultural institutions. Its clans developed distinct traditions around the management of the deep shafts — proprietary surveying knowledge, clan-held maps of the best veins, and a system of rights and precedence over working tunnels that predated the old kingdom's formal law.

During the Dwarven Rebellions, Gemhold was the site of the uprising's greatest success. Rebel forces seized the hold from its Imperial garrison and held it long enough to march through the connecting tunnels toward [[Glimmerforge]]. The march failed when Glimmerforge fell from within. Imperial forces retook Gemhold in the aftermath, and the hold has been under direct Imperial management ever since.

## Government & Imperial Presence

Gemhold is administered by Imperial extraction overseers who manage quota targets, labour assignments, and shipment schedules. The [[Valtorran Empire|Empire]]'s interest in Gemhold is entirely material: the gem deposits here are among the primary sources of the wealth extracted from the dwarven people since the conquest. Overseers work alongside dwarven foremen — the clans know the tunnels better than any administrator — but decision-making authority over output and trade rests with the occupation.

The gem conscription applies here as it does across the [[Silver Mountains]]: dwarves between eighteen and twenty-five are assigned to work the mines. For Gemhold's young, that service is often served close to home.

## The People

Gemhold's population is almost entirely dwarven, drawn from the mining clans whose family histories are the history of the hold's deep shafts. Life here is more austere and more insular than in [[Silverdeep]] or even [[Glimmerforge]] — the city does not face outward, and the rhythms of daily existence are shaped by the mines rather than by trade or craft. The memory of the rebellion's brief success here has not faded; it is part of the cultural fabric of the hold.
