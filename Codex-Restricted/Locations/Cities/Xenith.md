**Xenith** is a western port city on the ocean shore of the [[Xericana Sands]], the great market where the spoils of the desert are bought and sold. A melting-pot of the monstrous peoples who claim the Sands and beyond the firm reach of Imperial order, it is the place the wealth dragged out of the desert finds its way to the sea.

## Geography & Layout

Xenith clings to the far western coast of [[Valtorra]], where the [[Xericana Sands]] run all the way down to the ocean from the foot of the [[Silver Mountains]] far to the east. A harbour town at the desert's edge, it is the natural outlet for the whole western desert — caravans come in off the dunes and ships put out from its wharves.

## The People & Trade

Unlike the human cities of the Empire, Xenith belongs to the monstrous peoples who claim the [[Xericana Sands]]. Goblinoids of every stripe — goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears — rub shoulders with orcs, with the giantkin ogres and trolls, and with bestial folk such as minotaurs and centaurs, alongside any number of other peoples the Empire would name monsters. Its markets run on the valuables recovered, mined, and extracted from the desert, and it is a rough, polyglot trading port where the law is whatever the strongest interests make it.
