**Ashbourne** is a halfling farming town in the heart of the [[Luminor Plains]], a short way north-east of the Imperial capital of [[Val Luminor]]. Set where the main eastern roads of the plains meet, it is as much a crossroads market town as a farming one, and carts bound for the capital and the eastern towns alike pass through its square.

## Geography & Layout

Ashbourne stands in the open grassland north-east of [[Val Luminor]], at the junction where the road out of the capital forks — north-east toward [[Nightstone]] and the mountains, and east toward the grain country of [[Goldcrest]] and [[Hillcrest]]. The town has grown up around that meeting of roads: a central market square ringed by a coaching inn, grain-stores, and stalls, with cottages and fields spreading outward into the surrounding breadbasket plains.

## The People & Trade

Like the other plains towns, Ashbourne is a peaceable halfling farming community whose harvests feed the capital. Its crossroads position gives it a busier trade than most — produce from the eastern towns is gathered and carted on toward [[Val Luminor]] through its square, and the constant flow of wagons and travellers keeps its market and inn livelier than its size alone would suggest.
