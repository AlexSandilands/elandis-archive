> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Ebon]]*

## Status — Alive
- Ebon is **still alive**, old even for a dwarf, and **retired in [[Silverdeep]]**. The public entry lists his status as Unknown; in truth he watches the [[Order of Ravens]] from a distance and trusts his successor completely.
- He would surface only for an existential threat to the Order — and even then, only to her.

## The Succession
- Ebon personally **chose, groomed, and elevated his successor** as Grand Raven shortly before the campaign began: the merchant [[Gemma Finegold]]. This is among the deepest secrets tied to the Order; no Nest Father has seen the current Grand Raven's face unobscured.
- He found Gemma in [[Silverdeep]] not long after the [[Valtorran Empire]] publicly executed her mother. She had already — on her own initiative, without knowing who Ebon was — coordinated a clandestine rescue of several compromised Raven operatives, smuggling them out and keeping sensitive intelligence (possibly intelligence that would have led the Empire straight to Ebon) out of Imperial hands. He knew exactly who she was.
- Over the following years he folded her into the Order's work, recognising in her what he had once had: a merchant's freedom to move, a wound deep enough to sustain a lifetime's commitment, and a mind that worked in networks and leverage rather than blades.
