**Thorgar Ummal** is a dwarf of [[Glimmerforge]] and the father of [[Dondar]] and his siblings. Born to a poor, low-status family, he and his wife [[Hilda Ummal]] aided the [[Valtorran Empire]] in taking the city — a betrayal that broke the dwarven rebellion and earned the couple their appointment as the Empire's regents, served by the [[Cindercloaks]] secret police.
