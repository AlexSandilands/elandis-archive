**Hilda Ummal** is a dwarf of [[Glimmerforge]] and the mother of [[Dondar]] and his siblings. Together with her husband [[Thorgar Ummal]], she helped the [[Valtorran Empire]] seize the city in exchange for riches and status, and the two now rule as the Empire's regents, backed by the [[Cindercloaks]].
