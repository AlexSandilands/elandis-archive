> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Gemma Finegold]]*

> See also: *[[Order of Ravens - Restricted]]* — the Order-side view of the same secret.

## True Identity — The Grand Raven
- Gemma is the Grand Raven of the [[Order of Ravens]], and currently the only living person to hold the role. She was elevated by her predecessor [[Ebon]] shortly before the campaign began.
- The Grand Raven is concealed from virtually every member of the Order — including the Nest Fathers. No Raven has ever seen her face unobscured.
- The public entry frames her as an intelligence *asset* of the resistance — a clever merchant who smuggles and carries messages. That is the cover. She does not serve the network; she *is* the head of it.

## The Finegolds Are Her Network
- The Finegold caravan is not merely an allied trading company that helps the rebellion — it is the Grand Raven's own concealed network. Its wagons are the Order's smuggling route and information line.
- She gives orders in person, hidden inside her own trade. A Raven receives a trade letter or a passing word from a travelling merchant and never suspects the merchant *is* the Grand Raven, issuing the order to their face. Every node is compartmentalised so her hand never shows.
- The wider Order treats the Finegolds as a useful partnership; in truth it is the Grand Raven hiding in plain sight inside her own family trade.

## Who Knows — No One
- The crew ([[Lilliana Featherfoot]], [[Valen Leafwhisper]], [[Maeve Greycloud]], [[Doran Ironfist]]) know they do rebellion work, but none know Gemma is the Grand Raven. To them she is the late master's clever daughter who took over the business.
- [[Harland Finegold|Harland]] never knew. He thought he was grooming an heir into the family trade; he died without learning his daughter was running a war from his wagons.
- [[Barak Stormrider|Barak]] does not know. The public story is that Barak recruited her off a [[Mawbreakers]] cargo run; in truth she sought *him* out, deliberately "bumping into" him in a [[Camaar]] tavern because she needed a reliable Mawbreaker connection. The friendship is genuine on his end; the introduction was engineered on hers.
- [[The Bloody Nails]] have never known. They have worked for her, through her, by her design, from their very first job. The [[Skyreach]] mission was her opening move as Grand Raven, and folding the party into the caravan was her own initiative — they have never known whose orders they were following.

## How She Rose
- After the Empire publicly executed her mother in [[Silverdeep]], Gemma — on her own initiative and without knowing who [[Ebon]] was — coordinated a clandestine rescue of several compromised Raven operatives, smuggling them out and keeping sensitive intelligence (possibly intelligence that would have led the Empire straight to Ebon) out of Imperial hands.
- Ebon knew exactly who she was. Over the following years he folded her into the Order's work and groomed her for the mantle, recognising in her what he had once had: a merchant's freedom to move, a wound deep enough to sustain a lifetime's commitment, and a mind that worked in networks and leverage rather than blades.
- Ebon is now retired in Silverdeep — old even for a dwarf, alive, watching from a distance and trusting her completely. He would surface only for an existential threat to the Order, and even then only to her.

## Plot Hooks & Open Threads
- Whether the party ever learns Gemma is the Grand Raven — and how that revelation lands, given everything she has done alongside them.
- She is the single person who could betray the entire Order — and the last anyone would suspect. A point of catastrophic failure that looks like a friendly merchant.
