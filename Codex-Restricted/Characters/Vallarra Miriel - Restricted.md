**Vallarra Miriel** was a noblewoman of [[Val Miriel]] and the last sole heir of the ancient Miriel line at the time of her death. She was the wife of [[Governor Tarlis Evandron]] and the mother of [[Vallania Miriel]]. Vallarra died in childbirth, leaving behind a daughter and a grief-stricken husband whose overprotective devotion to Vallania has since shaped the course of both their lives.

## Biography

Vallarra carried the weight of the Miriel name at a precarious moment in its history — the only heir of her generation to a lineage bound to [[Val Miriel]] and the royal graveyard of [[Miriel's Rest]] that bears her family's name. Her marriage to Tarlis Evandron united noble blood with political office, and her death in childbed cut short whatever role she might have played in the resistance that Tarlis would later build in secret.

Her absence is felt most keenly in the life of her daughter. Vallania grew up under her father's fierce protection — a love shaped entirely by the terror of losing Vallarra — and that sheltering hand drove Vallania toward the dangers Tarlis feared most. In this way, Vallarra's death became as formative as her life might have been.
