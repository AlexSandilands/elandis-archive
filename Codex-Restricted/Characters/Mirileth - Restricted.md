> [!abstract] Public Entry: *[[Mirileth]]*

## The Queen Is Two (Full Truth — extremely restricted)
- Mirileth began as a bodiless spirit. Long ago a powerful, deeply evil entity severed her spirit from her body and destroyed that body, leaving her discorporate.
- The pact that ended the [[Vale of Eternal Night#The Long Hunt (~2000 years ago)|Long Hunt]] was her way back. [[Ellesandra Silmara]] agreed to give up her body to Mirileth in exchange for Mirileth seizing the Vale and bringing the Hunt to heel. The two now cohabitate one form — two minds, one face, archfae and elven hero both. This is why the Queen reads as elven-but-wrong.
- Ellesandra honoured the first half at once, returning from the Vale with the head of the Hunt's leader, as the public legend records. She paid the rest a few years later: her recorded death was really her return to the Vale to surrender her body, likely disguised by magic to look natural. Mirileth has worn that body ever since.
- A living Silmara is therefore literally kin to the body on the throne. How the Queen receives [[Ryo]] is the open question of the embassy.

## The Soulstone Anchor (Full Truth — extremely restricted)
- After taking the throne, Mirileth hunted down the entity that had originally unmade her and bound it inside the [[Soulstone Anchor]] kept in the Veiled Citadel of [[Caer Nystral, The Dawnless City|Caer Nystral]].
- The prisoner is ancient, extremely evil, patient and conniving, waiting for any chance to escape. The Anchor is her secret prize and her secret terror.
- She will quietly kill anyone who learns the Anchor exists — which makes the party's retrieval mission acutely dangerous. See [[Vale of Eternal Night]] (Restricted) for the full chain.

## Asha Vayne
- Mirileth did not let [[Asha Vayne]] die sixty years ago and did not waste him: he is held within the Court, bound into its service (glamoured/charmed), his old self buried but recoverable. [[Warden Sylvaris]]'s read is correct.

## Plot Hooks
- The party intends to enter the Vale as a Silmara embassy, invoking Ryo's bloodline. Mirileth will be aware of this before they arrive.
- Her reception turns on the pact above — and on whether the party betrays any knowledge of the Anchor.
