**Hrothgar** is a dwarf of [[Glimmerforge]] and the twin brother of [[Dondar]]. Where their parents threw in with the [[Valtorran Empire]], Hrothgar took the opposite path: he leads the grassroots resistance movement known as the [[Eternal Forge]], the new dwarven rebellion whose members bear its armour.
