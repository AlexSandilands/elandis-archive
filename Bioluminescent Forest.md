A minor underground Location POI — a vast glowing mushroom cavern within the tunnel network near [[Nelfandriss the Banished]]'s caves, home to a community of displaced Fungrals.

## Established
- Accessed through the breach in the [[Ruby Falls Goldmine]] (Session 24 prep)
- Kai harvested blue and purple mushrooms and transplanted them to the spider cave entrance as a warning sign (Session 25)
- Fungrals also appeared in the Faewild, implying a shared origin (Session 45)

## Geography
- A cavern hundreds of feet long and wide, deep within the tunnels near [[Nelfandriss the Banished]]'s caves
- Towering mushrooms fill the space from floor to ceiling, each emitting a soft pulsating glow — hues of blue, green, and purple that dance across the cavern walls
- Web arches span between the larger stalks, forming natural bridges through the upper reaches
- The floor is cushioned with thick spongy moss and smaller fungi, which muffles sound and gives the space an eerie quiet

## Inhabitants
- Home to a community of [[Fungrals]] — small mushroom-folk, roughly three feet tall, with mycelium flesh and mushroom-cap heads varying in shape and colour
- They were transported here from the Faewild — most likely drawn through a riftshard or fae circle leaking into the tunnels — and have made the cavern their permanent home

## Mushrooms
Each mushroom colour has distinct alchemical properties when processed into an oil (10 lbs per vial):

| Colour | Harvested Effect | Spore Effect |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|
| Red | 2d6 fire damage (thrown vial) | Con save DC 13 or friendly faces turn demonic; Wis save DC 13 or attack |
| Green | 3d6 poison damage (drunk) | Con save DC 15 or 2d6 poison + poisoned; otherwise just poisoned |
| Purple | Cures poison | — |
| Blue | Edible | — |
