- Major Character
- Massive imposing man, can't pierce through his expressionless face
- Don't get in his way, sees people the same way as he sees furniture
- Indeed, happily destroys both furniture and people when becoming angry
- Piercing eyes, irises black, short black beard, Wears terrifying looking black plate armor, long red clock similar to the Hands of the Empire, but more embellished, huge pauldrons, evil looking longsword over his shoulder, helmet with horns
- He was the first commander of forces that fell under the sway of [[Empress Morganna Eventide]] after she landed on the eastern shores of Valtorra
- He enabled her to take the first town, and started the snowball of the campaign that quickly spread like fire across the land
- Eventually, once she had determined that he was useful enough to be kept around for a long time, she killed him and raised him as a Death Knight
- She gave him the sword [[Dread's Hunger]]
- He was killed by the Bloody Nails in Val Noren
