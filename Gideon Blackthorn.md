- Minor character
- Male half elf
- Appearance: Gideon is a young man in his mid-20s with a slender build and quick reflexes. His sandy brown hair and nondescript features allow him to blend into any crowd. He has a distinctive scar running from his left ear to his chin. 

Background: Raised as a street urchin in Val Luminor, Gideon survived by his wits and quick hands. He was recruited into the Order after he successfully pickpocketed a Raven. Impressed by his skills, they gave him a choice: join them or face the consequences. He chose to join and has never looked back. 

Personality: Gideon is resourceful and quick-thinking, always ready with a plan or an escape route. He is fiercely independent but has a strong sense of loyalty to those he considers his comrades. 

Special Skills: Exceptional pickpocket and lock-picker. Skilled in acrobatics and evasion, making him an excellent scout and courier for the Order.
