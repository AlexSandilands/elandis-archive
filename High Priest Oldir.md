- Minor character
- Ancient old man, hunched over, high priest of high sept of val miriel
- long white beard, white robes
- As a rule never shares preference for any of the 7 gods of the pantheon
- The knowledge that the pantheon of the 7 are a bit of a stretch of the true was passed down to him via the line of succession.
- He imparted this knowledge to Kai after he revealed himself to be chosen by Selune - the 7 are actually just demi gods, chosen by some of the real gods. In fact there are many more than the 7, but most peoples have forgotten and the 7 has become the most convenient way to spread the religion and keep people happy
