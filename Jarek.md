 Back Story 

- On that night of the heist, we were separated, he got captured by Imperials 
- Interrogated for information on the Ravens - didn't give anything btw 
- Managed to escape the cell 
- When got back to our nest, they reported Eryndor hadn't returned either. 
- Feared the worst but went looking. No sign found, other than the bodies of Yarna and Ilto (her were also on the heist) 
- Looked for months, years, watched imperial garrisons to see if Eryndor was being held but saw nothing. 
- No other nest reported hearing of him, presumed dead 
- Kept eyes and ears out, but had lost all hope. Years of grief having lost the two most important people in his life now. 
- Eventually heard rumours that elves were being held captive in the dungeons of Val Luminor 
- Hope started return - maybe Eirieneth had been taken there! 
- Tried to plan a heist with the Ravens, but after the expulsion from the Capitol City a few decades earlier they are very reluctant to do any work there, for risk of members being captured and a breach into their security being made 
- Too old to do it himself. 
- This hope is all that is driving him now, he wants to see her face again
