- Minor character
- Member of [[The Finegolds]]
**Role:** Performer and Fortune Teller
**Voice:** Mid, normal.
* **Description:** Female, late 20s (Half-Elf). Slender with an aura of mystique. Long curly auburn hair, bright emerald eyes, and slightly pointed ears. Dressed in colorful flowing garments adorned with feathers and trinkets.
* **Background:** A bard who provides entertainment during the journey. She claims her fortune-telling skills were inherited from her elven mother. Has some clerical training
* She saw her father (an elf) be killed by imperials in the Whispering Wood (the same raid that took [[Eirieneth Silmara]])
